# O77 — Amend RAO 4-9 so that Coordinators and the Stated Clerk are Elected to 4-year Terms
**Targets:** RAO 4-9 · **Source:** Southeast Alabama Presbytery (SEAL) · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Overture-77_SEAL_RAO4-9.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Overture-77_SEAL_RAO4-9.pdf)

**What it does:** Amends RAO 4-9 to replace the annual nomination/one-year election of the four Program Committee Coordinators and the Stated Clerk with election to four-year terms (deletes "annually" from both nomination clauses). Effective for the Stated Clerk at GA53 and for the four Coordinators (CDM, MNA, MTW, RUF) at GA54.

**Cites in its own grounds:** None (rationale invokes only parity with the 5-year terms of Permanent Committee members and the no-fixed-term practice of Agency heads; no prior overtures, cases, or CCB rulings cited).

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
None found.

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **Supplemental III.1** (GA49/2022) — Whether Committees of Commissioners may nominate substitute program coordinators; CCB ruled a CoC may only approve or disapprove a nominee, not nominate a substitute. Construes the RAO 4-9 "nominate annually a coordinator … for election" mechanism this overture rewrites. [page](../inquiries/ga49_2022__ci29.md)
- **Supplemental III.2** (GA49/2022) — Floor nomination procedure for the office of Stated Clerk; CCB advised floor nominations follow the same procedure as other contested offices and require TEC examination. Construes the RAO 4-9 Stated Clerk nomination/election clause and the TEC-examination proviso this overture preserves. [page](../inquiries/ga49_2022__ci30.md)
- **Supplemental II.2** (GA52/2025) — Floor nominations for Stated Clerk when the AC brings no nominee; CCB ruled floor nominations are permitted under RAO 3-4 even absent an AC nominee. ⭐ Recent (GA52). Construes the annual AC-nomination/election scheme for the Stated Clerk in RAO 4-9 that this overture lengthens to a four-year term. [page](../inquiries/ga52_2025__ci35.md)

### Prior overtures
- **GA19 O43** (1991, Answered in the negative) — Amend RAO 4-9 to Limit Coordinators & Stated Clerk to Two Four-Year Terms (Presbytery of the Ascension). The closest prior attempt: it already proposed four-year terms for these same officers (capped at two terms) and was defeated — the precise change O77 revives, minus the cap. [page](../markdown/ga19_1991.md#ga19-p60)
- **GA39 O2** (2011, Answered in the negative) — Amend RAO 4-9 to Set Term Limits for Program Committee Coordinators and Stated Clerk (South Florida Presbytery). A second prior attempt to restructure the term/length scheme of the same provision for the same offices; also defeated. [page](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p77)

### Note
RPR exceptions are not applicable — RAO 4-9 is a Rule of Assembly Operations, not a BCO provision subject to presbytery RPR exceptions; the spine returned rpr=[]. No renumbering applies (not in bco_renumberings.jsonl). **GA53 RAO 4-9 cluster — cross-link O40, O41, O43, O73, O77:** O77 is functionally identical to **O43 (PNW)**, which likewise moves Coordinators to four-year terms (O77 adds the Stated Clerk and an effective-date scheme); O40 (Savannah River) sets calendar-year terms; O41 (Savannah River) and O73 (TE Hooper) instead keep annual election but raise the vote threshold to 2/3. The two prior overtures (GA19 O43, GA39 O2) are the highest-value bearing actions: both proposed amending the very same term structure of RAO 4-9 and both were answered in the negative, signaling repeated past resistance. The three inquiries are provision-exact hits retained (none pruned as incidental) because each construes the RAO 4-9 nomination/election clauses that O77 rewrites; they are procedural-mechanism rulings rather than rulings on term length, which is the only sense in which their bearing is partial.
