# O32 — Amend RAO 14-3 for the Review of Committee and Agency Standing Rules and Manuals
**Overtures Committee (2026) recommends:** Refer back *(vote 125-2-0)*
**Assembly (2026) voted:** Refer back to presbytery
**Targets:** RAO 14-3 · **Source:** Westminster · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Overture-32_Westminster_RAO14-3.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Overture-32_Westminster_RAO14-3.pdf)

**What it does:** Amends RAO 14-3 so permanent Committees and Agencies must submit not only their minutes but also their standing rules or manuals to their Committee of Commissioners for review, on the rationale (BCO 14-1.7) that committees serve and execute rather than direct and establish policy, yet can in effect set policy through manuals that never appear in reviewed minutes.

**Cites in its own grounds:** BCO 14-1.7 only. No prior cases, overtures, or inquiries cited by number.

None found.

### Note
The deterministic spine matched RAO 14-3 only via its confessional-subscription sub-clauses (RAO 14-3e.5). All of the spine's inquiries (31-56 IV/VI, 32-36 III) and prior overtures (GA31 O5, GA31 O18) concern whether presbyteries must record ministers'/candidates' exceptions to the Standards — a different question than O32's review of committee/agency manuals — so they are pruned as provision-exact-but-incidental, and all 7 RAO 14-3 RPR rows are pruned likewise. The bearing actions above come from the topical supplement (committee/agency manual review and Committee-of-Commissioners review authority). No renumbering applies (RAO 14-3 is current). Closest companion is GA47 O24, which chose a "precedence statement" solution to the same manual-drift problem O32 attacks via mandatory review.
