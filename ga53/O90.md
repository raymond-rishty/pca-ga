# O90 — Append Reformed Christian Politics to AIC Report on Christian Nationalism
**Targets:** (committee — no BCO provision) · **Source:** Grace PC (sent under RAO 11-10) · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/Overture-90_GracePC_CN.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/Overture-90_GracePC_CN.pdf)

**What it does:** Asks GA53 to supplement the Ad Interim Committee on Christian Nationalism's Report with a separate "Reformed Christian Politics Study Report" (authored by Stephen Wolfe, TE Zach Garris, TE Sean McGowan) as an appendix, commending that appendix to the churches as pious advice. (Rejected by Palmetto Presbytery May 14, 2026; forwarded by the Session of Grace PC.)

**Cites in its own grounds:** None. The whereas clauses make only generic references to "the Committee on Christian Nationalism's Report" and "our own Reformed tradition of political theology"; no specific prior case, overture, or inquiry is cited.

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
- **[1 — Stephen M. Lee, et al. v. Gulf Coast Presbytery](../cases/ga10_1982__case1.md)** (GA10/1982, complaint sustained in part) — The PCA's leading judicial treatment of whether a particular view of the application of God's law ("theonomy") is within the system of doctrine; directly the kind of political-theology dispute the Christian Nationalism committee is studying and that O90's appendix would address. Bears on substance, not on the procedural mechanism of appending a report. [minutes](../markdown/ga10_1982.md)
- **[2015-13 — Barnes v. Heartland Presbytery](../cases/ga45_2017__2015-13.md)** (GA45/2017, sustained) — Adjudicated stated differences on WCF 19 (the law of God), republication, and "two kingdoms"/Klinean theology as fundamentals of the system of doctrine — the confessional terrain (church/civil-magistrate relation) at the center of the Christian Nationalism study. Bears on the doctrinal subject matter, not on the appendix procedure. [minutes](../markdown/ga45_2017.md)

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **11-36 III, 29** (GA11/1983) — CCB advised that a presbytery retains the right to examine and judge a man heretical on WCF 19-4 grounds with specific evidence; bears on how the PCA has handled the orthodoxy of judicial-law / political-theology views that the proposed report engages. [page](../inquiries/ga11_1983__ci01.md)

### Prior overtures
- **GA52 O3** (2025, answered by reference) — Arizona Presbytery, "Erect Ad Interim Committee on Christian Nationalism"; one of the overtures that created the very committee whose report O90 seeks to supplement. ⭐ [page](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1167)
- **GA52 O4** (2025, answered by reference) — South Texas Presbytery, "Erect Ad Interim Study Committee on Christian Nationalism" (naming Moscow theology and postmillennial theonomic reconstructionism); directly defines the committee's mandate that O90 would append to. ⭐ [page](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1168)
- **GA52 O47** (2025, adopted/final) — Great Lakes Presbytery, "Erect an Ad Interim Study Committee on Christian Nationalism"; the adopted overture that actually erected the committee. The most directly bearing prior action. ⭐ [page](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1298)
- **GA24 O9** (1996, answered in the negative) — Southern Florida Presbytery requested a study committee to define and defend or refute "Theonomy"/"Christian Reconstructionism"; a prior, failed attempt at the same political-theology study question now revived by the Christian Nationalism committee and O90's appendix. [page](../markdown/ga24_1996.md#ga24-p52)
- **GA11 O8** (1983, answered in the negative) — Illiana Presbytery sought to rescind the Seventh GA's statement on judicial law and theonomy; shows the long-running PCA contest over how the courts should treat theonomic political theology. [page](../markdown/ga11_1983.md#ga11-p44)
- **GA46 O29** (2018, adopted/final) — Pittsburgh Presbytery formed a study committee to examine the RPCNA report on sexual orientation; precedent for the PCA receiving/commending an outside or sister-tradition study report — the mechanism O90 proposes for the Wolfe/Garris/McGowan report. [page](../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p729)
- **GA29 O20** (2001, answered in the negative) — New River Presbytery, "Allow Exceptions on Length of Creation Days": the whereas clauses object that the 28th GA's action on the Creation Study Committee was "described by the Moderator as simply 'pious advice'" yet was generating pressure on presbyteries — an exact parallel to the "commend as pious advice" mechanism O90 invokes and the disputes that label generates. [page](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p57)
