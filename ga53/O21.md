# O21 — Amend BCO 13-6 to Clarify Exams for Ministers from Another Denomination
**Targets:** BCO 13-6 · **Source:** Westminster · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Overture-21_Westminster_13-6.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Overture-21_Westminster_13-6.pdf)

**What it does:** Amends BCO 13-6.b so that a minister transferring from another denomination is examined "thoroughly as required by the trials listed in BCO 21-4.a, 21-4.c(1), 21-4.c(4), and 21-4.e and any additional trials deemed necessary," rather than under all of BCO 21-4 — pruning the thesis/exegetical-paper requirements that the pre-52nd-GA "knowledge and views" standard never imposed. Preserves the ordination-questions requirement (BCO 21-5) and the extraordinary-provisions sentence.

**Cites in its own grounds:** None by case/overture number. The grounds react to "the recent amendment" of BCO 13-6 made prior to the 52nd GA (the GA51 O2 / O27 changes below) and cross-reference companion GA53 Overture 25.

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
- **[2015-13 — Barnes v. Heartland Presbytery](../cases/ga45_2017__2015-13.md)** (GA45/2017, sustained) — construes the adequacy of presbytery examination under BCO 13-6, the same examination-scope question the overture clarifies. [minutes](../markdown/ga45_2017.md#ga45-p480)
- **[2022-21 — Senters v. Savannah River Presbytery](../cases/ga51_2024__2022-21.md)** (GA51/2024, not sustained) — recent complaint turning on what BCO 13-6 requires when receiving a minister; bears on the examination standard being amended. [minutes](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p721)

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **7-41 III, 21** (GA7/1979) — CCB advised BCO 13-6 requires ordination questions be put to any minister from another denomination, and presbytery may require re-ordination if deemed necessary; directly construes the transfer-examination subject the overture refines. [page](../inquiries/ga07_1979__ci01.md)
- **CI #16** (GA13/1985) — CCB advised that neither BCO 13-6 nor BCO 21-4 contains an extraordinary clause applicable to ministers transferring from other denominations; bears on the extraordinary-provisions sentence the overture retains. [page](../inquiries/ga13_1985__ci12.md)
- **34-34 IV.B** (GA34/2006) — CCB advice on conduct of presbytery examinations under BCO 13-6/21-4 (use of translators), construing the same exam framework. [page](../inquiries/ga34_2006__ci03.md)
- **II.B** (GA51/2024) — CCB found no conflict in amending BCO 13-6 for greater clarity in transferring ordination (Overture 2, Northern California); the immediately prior amendment this overture reacts to. ⭐ [page](../inquiries/ga51_2024__ci02.md)
- **II.V** (GA51/2024) — CCB found amending BCO 13-6 to add character/family-management to transfer examinations not in conflict (Overture 27, Potomac); same recent reshaping of 13-6 transfer exams. ⭐ [page](../inquiries/ga51_2024__ci22.md)

### Prior overtures
- **GA25 O1** (1997, Approved & ratified 1998) — "Amend BCO 13-6 to Require Full Examination of Transfers" (Southwest Florida); the origin of the full-examination standard this overture now narrows. [page](../markdown/ga25_1997.md#ga25-p49)
- **GA37 O8** (2009, Answered in the negative) — "Amend BCO 13-6 to Clarify Examinations for Men Coming from Other Denominations" (Ascension); a prior attempt at substantially the same clarification, rejected. [page](../markdown/ga37_2009.md#ga37-p275)
- **GA37 O9** (2009, Approved & ratified 2011) — "Amend BCO 13-6 to Require Men from Other Denominations to Declare Differences with the Confession" (Ascension); shaped the current transfer-exam text. [page](../markdown/ga37_2009.md#ga37-p276)
- **GA51 O2** (2024, Approved & ratified 2025) — "Amend BCO 13-6 for Clarity in Transfers of Ordination" (Northern California); part of "the recent amendment" the overture's grounds respond to. ⭐ [page](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p1060)
- **GA51 O27** (2024, Approved & ratified 2025) — "Amend BCO 13-6 to Add Personal Character and Family Management to Transfer Examination" (Potomac); the most recent change to 13-6.b examination scope. ⭐ [page](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p1179)

### RPR exceptions
- **BCO 13-6** — 309 RPR citations, overwhelmingly presbytery-minutes deficiencies in transfer/reception examinations (e.g., [North Florida](../rpr/north-florida.md) 28th/2000: "No evidence that the TE was examined on Sacraments and Church Government"; [Korean Capital](../rpr/exc/korean-capital__007.md) 25th/1997: "Incomplete record of transfer exam for minister of another denomination"); the real-world record-keeping friction that motivates clarifying what 13-6 actually requires. Southwest Florida (2009-11), Reese and Bech (2011-14), Aven (2016-1), Schrock (2019-2), and Klett (2022-3) cite 13-6 within broader complaints not centered on transfer-exam scope and were pruned in favor of the three that most directly construe the examination standard.
