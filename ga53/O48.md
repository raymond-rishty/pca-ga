# O48 — Amend BCO 7-2 and 7-3 Regarding Commissioning and Titling of Unordained Persons
**Overtures Committee (2026) recommends:** Answer by reference to [O37](O37.md) *(vote 110-23-0)*
**Assembly (2026) voted:** Answer in the negative
**Targets:** BCO 7-2, 7-3 · **Source:** NWGA (Northwest Georgia Presbytery) · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-48_NWGA_7.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-48_NWGA_7.pdf)

**What it does:** Amends BCO 7-2 to add that, because the offices are open to men only, "it is unlawful for any court to commission or otherwise appoint, or for a congregation to elect or otherwise approve, a woman as a deaconess, shepherdess, or any other pretended office," and amends BCO 7-3 to bar giving unordained persons "other titles which by their nature imply the authority of those offices." It further declares such women's-leadership practices an irregularity subject to review and control of higher courts under BCO 40-3.

**Cites in its own grounds:** No prior cases, overtures, or inquiries cited by number. Grounds rest on Scripture (1 Tim 3:1-13; Titus 1:5-16) and on BCO provisions: 7-2, 7-3, 17-2, 21-7, 24-6 (laying on of hands), 20-3, 24-1, 9-7 (election/appointment), and 40-3 (review and control).

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **38-34 II.E** (GA38/2010) — CCB found an overture appointing unordained men and women to diaconal ministry roles **in conflict** with the Constitution, partly because it implied an unordained office contrary to BCO 17-1; bears directly on the "pretended office" concern in O48's 7-2 amendment. [page](../inquiries/ga38_2010__ci04.md)
- **II.Q** (GA47/2019) — CCB found an overture letting individual sessions decide locally whether women may serve as deacons **not in conflict** (BCO 7-2, 9-3); the opposite policy O48 forecloses, showing the CCB has treated the question as one the Assembly may resolve either way. [page](../inquiries/ga47_2019__ci17.md)
- **II.S** (GA50/2023) — CCB found no constitutional conflict in amending BCO 7-3 to regulate titling of unordained persons (adopted 6-1-1); the direct precedent for the 7-3 half of O48, and the very amendment O48 now extends. [page](../inquiries/ga50_2023__ci19.md)
- **II.J** (GA51/2024) — CCB found an overture restricting BCO 7-2 ordination to biological males **not in conflict** (adopted 8-0-0); the direct precedent that further substantive restrictions to 7-2 are constitutionally permissible. [page](../inquiries/ga51_2024__ci10.md)

### Note
Companion to **[GA50 O26](../overtures/ga50_2023__o26.md)** (same presbytery, ratified 2024) — O48 is the next step in NWGA's program: O26 restricted *titles* in 7-3; O48 closes the *commissioning/electing* gap in 7-2 and broadens 7-3 to titles that "imply the authority" of office. No renumbering affects BCO chapter 7. Pruned as incidental spine hits: the GA12/1984 "serious accusations" case, GA29/2001 "The John Wood Matter" (both provision-exact but with no case page and no construction of the office-gender/titling question), and GA50/2023 case 2022-20 *Wilson v. Pacific Northwest Presbytery* (BCO 7-2 cited twice in a dissenting opinion as an analogy — "the church privilege to be nominated and elected an officer is suspended on the condition of being male (BCO 7-2; 24-1)" — in a case whose actual holding is about minor communicant voting rights, not unordained women's roles or titling); inquiry 12-53 (ordination of men who *believe* women may be deacons — a candidate-exception question, not the practice O48 bars), inquiry 23-43 (creation/male-office candidate exceptions), inquiry App. O III GA42 and CCB II.G GA45 (both 24-7 commissioner/sabbatical mechanics, incidental to 7-2). Also pruned numerous body+title overture matches unrelated to office/titling (Memorial Service, Freemasonry, BCO 46-5, RAO 9-4, BCO 27-46 judicial rules, RAO 4-21.d enrollment, the empty GA51 O32) and overtures touching deacon/elder mechanics but not the women's-office or titling question (GA14 O36 diaconal scope, GA15 O35 "Reverend," GA24 O2 committee voting, GA26 O12 deacon vows, GA30 O14 BCO 24-1, GA42 O22 third ordination vow, GA45 O8 dual office, GA46 O4 RE composition).
