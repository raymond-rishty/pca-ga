# O62 — Divide Tennessee Valley Presbytery into Two Presbyteries
**MNA recommends:** Answer in the affirmative
**Assembly voted:** Answer in the affirmative
**Targets:** (boundaries) · **Source:** TVP · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Overture-62_TVP_Dividing.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Overture-62_TVP_Dividing.pdf)

> *Referred by the Stated Clerk to MNA; not acted on by the Overtures Committee.*

**What it does:** Asks GA53 to divide Tennessee Valley Presbytery (36 churches, 5 missions, 113 ministers, 32 counties) into a continuing TVP (13 southern counties: 8 in TN + 5 in GA, ~24 churches) and a new Smoky Mountain Presbytery (19 northern TN counties around Knoxville, ~12 churches + 4 missions), effective January 1, 2027, with rules for assigning RUF / Covenant College / MTW / MNA / HR / out-of-bounds teaching elders and candidates.

**Cites in its own grounds:** The "Guidelines for Dividing Presbyteries" membership thresholds ("minimum of 10 churches," "total communicant membership of at least 1,000," "at least 3 churches having a membership of at least 125"), which O62 attributes to the 41st GA (see Note — these were in fact adopted by the 26th GA). No prior cases, overtures, or inquiries cited by name.

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **CI 10-77** (GA10/1982, advice given) — Constitutionality of a non-geographical presbytery: the CCB found BCO 11-4 "implies geographical bounds" while BCO 13-1 does not limit "bounds" geographically. Bears on the constitutional premise of any geographic-boundary division like O62, which redraws bounds county-by-county. [page](../inquiries/ga10_1982__ci04.md)

### Prior overtures
- **[GA22 O27](../overtures/ga22_1994__o27.md)** (1994, Adopted (final)) — "Divide Tennessee Valley Presbytery to Form Nashville Presbytery." The single most on-point precedent: the prior division of *this very presbytery*, confirming GA's established practice of granting TVP's subdivision requests. [page](../markdown/ga22_1994.md#ga22-p65)
- **[GA41 O9](../overtures/ga41_2013__o9.md)** (2013, Adopted (final)) — "Form Tidewater Presbytery from James River Presbytery." The structural template O62 follows almost verbatim — same Guidelines criteria (≥10 churches / ≥1,000 communicants / ≥3 churches of ≥125), same Jan 1 effective date — and it correctly attributes those Guidelines to the *26th* GA. [page](../markdown/ga41_2013.md#ga41-p91)
- **[GA45 O19](../overtures/ga45_2017__o19.md)** (2017, Adopted (final)) — "Divide the Presbytery of the Southwest into Two Presbyteries." Recent precedent for granting a presbytery's request to split into two. [page](../markdown/ga45_2017.md#ga45-p673)
- **GA46 O22 / O23** (2018, Adopted (final)) — "Divide Metropolitan New York Presbytery into Two Presbyteries." Recent two-way division granted; O23 was the companion sent to CCB/OC for editorial review. [page](../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p715) · [O23 page](../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p717)
- **GA52 O36 / O46** (2025, O36 Adopted (final)) — "Change the Boundaries of Georgia Foothills and Metro Atlanta Presbyteries." ⭐ Most recent boundary action; shows GA53-era MNA practice of approving paired boundary overtures. [page](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1273) · [O46 page](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1297)

### Note
This is a non-provision (boundaries) overture, so the deterministic provision-join spine returned no hits; all bearing actions above were found by topical search of the OVERTURES / INQUIRIES indices. **Provenance caveat on O62's own grounds:** O62 attributes the "Guidelines for Dividing Presbyteries" to "the 41st General Assembly," but the Guidelines were in fact *approved by the 26th GA* (M26GA, 26-44, p.180; see [ga26_1998.md#ga26-p180](../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p180)), building on guidelines first set by the 16th GA (1988); the GA41 Tidewater overture (O9) cited them correctly as the 26th GA's and merely applied them. **Pruning:** the OVERTURES index lists dozens of routine boundary-adjustment/erection overtures (e.g., GA48 O8/O11/O42, GA49 O10, GA50 O1, GA51 O5/O8/O11/O12, GA52 O2/O17/O45); these are administrative county-shuffles, not subdivisions of an existing presbytery, so they are pruned as not bearing on the *division* question. Kept items are limited to (a) the prior division of TVP itself, (b) the closest structural template (GA41 Tidewater), and (c) recent two-way divisions plus the most recent GA52 boundary precedent for current practice. No judicial cases or RPR exceptions exist because presbytery boundaries are administrative matters handled by MNA, not justiciable provisions. (The prior pass cited GA40 O38, GA14 O9/O15, GA35 O17, GA36 O10, and "companion" overtures O10/O44/O70/O87 — none verified against the corpus and given without deep-links; they are dropped here.)
