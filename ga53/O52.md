# O52 — Add BCO 7-4 Prohibiting the Creation of Ecclesiastical Offices
**Targets:** BCO 7-4 (new) · **Source:** MS Valley · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-52_MSValley_7-4.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-52_MSValley_7-4.pdf)

**What it does:** Inserts a new BCO 7-4 that, while permitting courts to use qualified unordained men and women in a variety of ministry positions, bars church courts from creating ecclesiastical offices, vesting any part of the ordained-officer power in an unordained person, or intruding such persons upon the church or its members' consciences by popular election or by vows not mandated by the Word and the Constitution.

**Cites in its own grounds:** None (no prior cases, overtures, or inquiries cited by number). Grounds rest on WCF 1.6, BCO Preface I, BCO 3-1 and 3-2, BCO Preliminary Principle 7, and WCF 20.2.

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
- **[2009-25 / 2009-26 — Brown v. Northern California Presbytery; Session of Grace PC v. Northern California Presbytery](../cases/ga39_2011__2009-25_2009-26.md)** (M39GA/2011, sustained 20-0) — Directly on point: presbytery had adopted an abstract resolution permitting six diaconal "views/practices" (including unordained "deaconesses" and the "nobody ordained" view); the SJC annulled it, and the concurrence set the GUMP/Kniseley standard for when unordained diaconal practice crosses into a de facto office — the exact line O52 seeks to codify. [minutes](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p548)
- **[2007-13 — Kniseley v. Rocky Mountain Presbytery](../cases/ga37_2009__2007-13.md)** (M37GA/2009) — The standard-setting case on commissioning unordained diaconal assistants; bears on what counts as impermissibly vesting officer-power in unordained persons. [minutes](../markdown/ga37_2009.md#ga37-p555)
- **[2008-01 / 2008-10 — Crossroads Community Church Session & Grasso v. Philadelphia Presbytery](../cases/ga37_2009__2008-01_2008-10.md)** (M37GA/2009, denied) — Licensure/ordination of a man holding the "nobody ordained" diaconal view; part of the same diaconal-office line and shows the practical friction O52 addresses. [minutes](../markdown/ga37_2009.md#ga37-p554)

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **CCB 38-34 II.A** (M38GA/2010, in conflict) — Overture to prohibit deaconesses found in conflict because "deaconess" need not denote an ordained office; bears on the line between unordained service and a created office. [page](../inquiries/ga38_2010__ci02.md)
- **CCB 38-34 II.D** (M38GA/2010, in conflict) — Overture prohibiting commissioned/installed assistants to deacons found in conflict on the same grounds; directly relevant to "commissioning" unordained helpers. [page](../inquiries/ga38_2010__ci03.md)
- **CCB 38-34 II.E** (M38GA/2010, in conflict) — Overture appointing unordained persons to diaconal ministry found in conflict, in part because it implied an unordained office contrary to BCO 17-1 — the precise hazard O52 names. [page](../inquiries/ga38_2010__ci04.md)
- **CCB App. O II.H** (M46GA/2018, in conflict) — Grace Presbytery's deaconess/women-officer overture found in conflict; CCB held the session (not the congregation) is the appointing body requiring vows, invoking Preliminary Principle 7 — the same authorities and the popular-election/vows concern O52 raises. [page](../inquiries/ga46_2018__ci04.md)
- **CCB II.Q** (M47GA/2019, not in conflict) — Overture letting local sessions decide whether women may serve as deacons was found not in conflict; a counterpoint on how far courts may go in shaping unordained service. [page](../inquiries/ga47_2019__ci17.md)

### Note
The deterministic spine returned provisions=[BCO 7-4] with zero cases/inquiries/RPR and only two prior overtures — both PRUNED as incidental: GA23 O15 ("confine doctrinal requirements") and GA50 O9 (sexuality disqualification) cite the EXISTING BCO 7 / current 7-4 (officer qualifications), a different question from O52's office-creation prohibition. O52 inserts a NEW 7-4, so no past action cites this provision text; all bearing material is therefore TOPICAL — the diaconal-office / unordained-service line (cases, CCB advice, deaconess overtures, RPR). Also pruned: SJC 2023-22 (Suh — jurisdiction/membership, not office creation) and inquiry 12-53/ci09 (about a candidate's view on women deacons, not court-created office). No renumbering applies (7-4 is genuinely new). Companion GA53 overtures touching ch. 7 (titling/commissioning of unordained ministry) may cross-reference; O52's grounds are constitutional-principle based rather than case-based.
