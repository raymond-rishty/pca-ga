# O43 — Amend RAO 4-9 so that Coordinators of Committees are Elected to 4-year Terms
**Overtures Committee (2026) recommends:** Answer in the affirmative *(vote 73-53-5)*
**Targets:** RAO 4-9 · **Source:** Pacific Northwest Presbytery · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-43_PNW_RAO4-9.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-43_PNW_RAO4-9.pdf)

**What it does:** Amends RAO 4-9 so the four program Committees and the Administrative Committee nominate a coordinator/Stated Clerk for election to a four-year term (rather than annually), with men eligible to serve multiple successive terms if reelected. The effect is to replace annual reelection with staggered four-year terms beginning at GA53 (2026).

**Cites in its own grounds:** None — the rationale argues by analogy to AC (4-yr) and CDM/MNA/MTW/RUF (5-yr) member terms and to the agencies' presidents/EDs, but names no prior cases, overtures, or inquiries.

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **Supplemental III.1** (GA49/2022) — Whether Committees of Commissioners may nominate substitute program coordinators; CCB ruled a CoC may only approve or disapprove nominees for program coordinator, not nominate substitutes. Bears as a construction of how RAO 4-9 coordinator nomination/election operates, though it concerns the nomination mechanism, not term length. [page](../inquiries/ga49_2022__ci29.md)
- **Supplemental III.2** (GA49/2022) — Floor nomination procedure for the office of Stated Clerk; CCB advised floor nominations follow the same procedure as other contested offices and nominees must be examined by the TEC. Bears on the RAO 4-9 election/examination clause this overture leaves intact. [page](../inquiries/ga49_2022__ci30.md)
- **Supplemental II.2** (GA52/2025) — Floor nominations for Stated Clerk when the AC brings no nominee; CCB ruled floor nominations are permitted under RAO 3-4 even absent an AC nominee (Adopted 8-0-0). Recent construction of the RAO 4-9 nomination-by-committee scheme that this overture amends. ⭐ [page](../inquiries/ga52_2025__ci35.md)

### Note
The two prior overtures (GA39 O2, GA19 O43) are the controlling precedent — both proposed multi-year terms for RAO 4-9 coordinators/clerk and both were answered in the negative, making this the third bite at the same apple (this version omits the two-term cap both predecessors carried). Neither prior overture is GA48–52, so neither is starred. The three inquiries all cite RAO 4-9 but construe its *nomination / floor-nomination* mechanics rather than term length; they are kept as bearing-but-peripheral context, not as direct precedent on the change. Pruned from the topical search: GA12 O38 (examine coordinators before election), GA15 O37 (amend RAO Article III on coordinator/clerk selection), and GA18 O8 (keep the title "Coordinator") — all touch the coordinator/clerk election machinery but are not provision-exact to RAO 4-9 and address different questions (examination / Article III / title), so they are noted here rather than listed. Companion GA53 RAO 4-9 overtures to cross-check on the floor: O40/O41 (Savannah River), O73 (TE Hooper), and O77 (SEAL) — O77 in particular is nearly identical (four-year terms for coordinators and Stated Clerk), while O40 sets calendar-year terms and O41/O73 raise the election threshold. No judicial cases, CCB stand-alone advice, or RPR exceptions cite RAO 4-9. No renumbering applies (RAO 4-9 is stable; bco_renumberings covers BCO 24 only).
