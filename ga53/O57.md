# O57 — Amend BCO 12-7, 13-9, and 25-5 to Require Minutes of Congregational Meetings in Session Records
**Targets:** BCO 12-7, 13-9, 25-5 · **Source:** South Texas · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-57_SouthTexas_CongregationalMtgs.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-57_SouthTexas_CongregationalMtgs.pdf)
**What it does:** Amends BCO 12-7, 13-9.b, and 25-5 to make explicit that the minutes of congregational meetings are part of the records of the church Session — kept by the congregational clerk, sent to the Session, and submitted to Presbytery for annual review — codifying what the CCB has held to be true only "by implication."
**Cites in its own grounds:** SJC Case 2021-12 (Michelson v. Northwest Georgia Presbytery), M49GA 2022, 822–825 at 823 — quoted for the holding that a Presbytery "could take note of a Constitutional deficiency in a congregational meeting in their review of the records of the Session (note BCO 25-5, last sentence and BCO 13-9(b))"; and the CCB non-judicial reference that could only say congregational records "must be maintained" by implication (the GA52 2025 IV.B advice below). Grounds also cite BCO 25-11, 25, 20, 24.

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
- **[2021-12 — Michelson v. Northwest Georgia Presbytery](../cases/ga49_2022__2021-12.md)** (GA49/2022, dismissed) — The overture's own anchor case: holds a Presbytery may note a constitutional deficiency in a congregational meeting when reviewing Session records (BCO 25-5 last sentence + 13-9.b). Directly construes all three target provisions together. ⭐ [minutes](../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p839)
- **[2004-7 — Session of First Presbyterian Augusta v. Savannah River Presbytery](../markdown/ga33_2005.md#ga33-p140)** (GA33/2005, sustained) — Real-world friction over the conduct and minutes of a congregational meeting (Session adding docket items / recommending a course of action); the only spine case construing BCO 25-5 on congregational-meeting proceedings. [minutes](../markdown/ga33_2005.md#ga33-p140)
- **[2022-22 — Turner v. South Florida Presbytery](../cases/ga51_2024__2022-22.md)** (GA51/2024, not sustained) — Construes BCO 12-7 + 13-9 on the scope and adequacy of Presbytery review of Session records, the mechanism O57 would extend to congregational minutes. [minutes](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p733)
- **[2023-06/2023-08 — Baird et al. v. Grace Presbytery](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p796)** (GA51/2024, sustained) — Cites BCO 12-7 + 13-9 on the Presbytery's duty to review Session records and redress what is contrary to order. [minutes](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p796)

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **IV.B** (GA52/2025) — The non-judicial reference (Platte Valley) the overture cites: CCB ruled congregational meeting minutes ARE part of Session records subject to Presbytery review and must be submitted if required (BCO 25-5, 40, 41-3) — but only by implication, which is the gap O57 closes. [page](../inquiries/ga52_2025__ci32.md)
- **34-34 IV.C** (GA34/2006) — CCB advice on what Session records a Presbytery may review and require (language/translation), under BCO 12-7, 13-9b, 40-2; bears on the content and scope of the annual records review. [page](../inquiries/ga34_2006__ci04.md)
- **CI #17** (GA14/1986) — CCB advised a Presbytery must review ALL previously unreviewed Session records (BCO 13-9, 40-1..40-4), reinforcing the mandatory, comprehensive review O57 expands. [page](../inquiries/ga14_1986__ci19.md)

### Prior overtures
- **GA44 O38** (2016, Approved & ratified 2017) — Pacific Northwest Presbytery amended BCO 5-3 to require minute reporting for mission-church governments; the closest structural precedent for adding an explicit minute-keeping/reporting duty to the BCO. [page](../markdown/ga44_2016.md#ga44-p638)
- **GA51 O10** (2024, Answered in the negative) — Metro Atlanta Presbytery's attempt to amend BCO 13-9 (add Presbytery TE care); a recent, failed effort to amend the same provision, useful as a drafting/disposition cautionary precedent. ⭐ [page](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p1270)

### RPR exceptions
- **BCO 12-7** — 13 citations; repeated exceptions for Presbyteries failing to review/record Session minutes within the year (e.g., South Texas 18th/1990; Hills and Plains 50th/2023). Shows chronic real-world friction in the very review process O57 would extend to congregational minutes. [by provision](../index/RPR-BY-PROVISION.md)
- **BCO 13-9** — 33 citations; the largest block is "no record of review of records of church Sessions" (e.g., North Florida 52nd/2025; Korean Southwest 52nd/2025), confirming ongoing difficulty completing and documenting Session-records review. [by provision](../index/RPR-BY-PROVISION.md)

### Note
BCO 12-7, 13-9, and 25-5 are not renumbered (no entries in bco_renumberings.jsonl), so old-number searching is unnecessary. The spine is high-recall on BCO 13-9 — a provision enumerating nearly all of a Presbytery's powers — so the great majority of its ~50 case hits (church dissolution/dismissal, TE care, original jurisdiction, commissions, Korean-presbytery governance disputes, the Wilkins/Federal Vision cases) merely cite 13-9 in passing and were PRUNED as incidental; only actions touching review of Session/congregational records or the conduct/minutes of congregational meetings are kept (Michelson, First Pres Augusta, Turner, Baird). Likewise the prior-overture hits matched by title/body keyword on 13-9 concern dissolving/dismissing churches and TE care, not records — pruned except GA44 O38 (analogous minute-reporting amendment) and GA51 O10 (recent failed 13-9 amendment). The CCB advice items on BCO 7 / 8-7 / 17 / 41 (ordination, chaplains, venue) were pruned as unrelated. O57 is essentially the legislative sequel to the GA52 IV.B Platte Valley reference and the Michelson holding it quotes; cross-check any GA53 overture also amending BCO 25 or the records-review duty for a companion link.
