# O89 — Issue a Declaration of Thanksgiving for the USA on the 250th Anniversary
**Targets:** (statement) — no BCO/RAO amendment; grounds invoke BCO 14-6.k · **Source:** Fellowship Presbytery (to OC) · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/Overture-89_Fellowship_250th.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/Overture-89_Fellowship_250th.pdf)
**What it does:** Asks the 53rd GA to issue a formal "Declaration of Thanksgiving for the United States of America on the Occasion of the 250th Anniversary of the Nation's Founding" on July 4, 2026 — spreading the whereas-statements on the minutes and having the AC publish them electronically through byFaith.
**Cites in its own grounds:** No prior GA cases, overtures, or inquiries. Grounds rest on Scripture (Ps. 136:1; 1 Tim. 2:1-2; Ps. 76:10), WCF 5.7 / 20.2, the 1775 pastoral letter of the Synod of New York and Philadelphia, John Witherspoon's 1776 sermon, the U.S. Constitution's First Amendment, and **BCO 14-6.k** (GA's power "to recommend measures for the promotion of charity, truth and holiness through all the churches under its care").

- **GA48 O48** (2021, Adopted) — "Repudiate Anti-Asian Racism and Affirm the Dignity of Asian Americans": recent GA-adopted formal statement, illustrating the "spread-on-the-minutes" declaration mechanism O89 follows. ⭐ [p.1116](../markdown/ga48_2021.md#ga48-p1099), [p.1149](../markdown/ga48_2021.md#ga48-p1132)

### Note
**Companion GA53 triplet:** O89 is one of three materially identical 250th-anniversary thanksgiving-declaration overtures this Assembly — **O66 (Philadelphia)** and **O76 (Calvary)** are the other two — all to OC and all likely to be perfected/merged on the floor. O89 is the most heavily documented (extensive 1775 Synod pastoral-letter and Witherspoon citations).
The provision-exact spine returned nothing (statement overture, `provisions=[]`), so every bearing action here comes from topical search (concepts: thanksgiving, national/founding anniversary, GA-issued declaration/statement, day of prayer). Highest-value precedents: GA3 O20 (prior July-4/founding-anniversary act) and GA19 O41 (prior GA "thanksgiving" keyed to a civil event); GA21 O16 confirms the GA has used the same declarative mechanism toward the civil sphere; recent statement overtures (GA48 O45/O48, GA49 O26 ⭐) show the OC channel and the spread of outcomes (adopted vs. answered by reference). Pruned: 1 incidental BCO 14-6 inquiry (GA10, committee-channeling — not the 14-6.k substantive power). No judicial cases or RPR exceptions bear: a thanksgiving declaration is non-justiciable.
