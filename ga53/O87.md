# O87 — Change the Boundaries of Pittsburgh Presbytery and the Ohio Presbytery
**MNA recommends:** Answer by reference to [O10](O10.md)
**Assembly voted:** Answer by reference to [O10](O10.md)
**Targets:** (boundaries) · **Source:** Pittsburgh · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/Overture-87_Pittsburgh_Boundaries-5.15.26.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/Overture-87_Pittsburgh_Boundaries-5.15.26.pdf)

> *Referred by the Stated Clerk to MNA; not acted on by the Overtures Committee.*

**What it does:** Transfers all Ohio counties currently within Pittsburgh Presbytery's bounds (the region "east and south of and including the counties of Columbiana, Carroll, and Monroe") to Ohio Presbytery, since Ohio Presbytery has since been formed and in 2025 the only PCA church in those Ohio counties of Pittsburgh closed.

**Cites in its own grounds:** None by number — the grounds only quote Pittsburgh's existing official boundary description, note that Ohio Presbytery has since been formed, and recite the 2025 closure of the lone affected PCA church.

### Note
**Companion overture:** O87 (Pittsburgh) is the ceding-presbytery half of a paired filing with **O10** (Ohio Presbytery) — see [O10.md](./O10.md); both seek the identical transfer of Pittsburgh's Ohio counties to Ohio Presbytery, satisfying the standard requirement that both affected presbyteries concur. The single most-bearing precedent is **GA45 O20/O23 (2017)**, where these same two presbyteries did this exact Pittsburgh-to-Ohio reassignment with the identical two-overture concurrence structure; O87 finishes the job. This is a non-provision (boundaries) overture, so the deterministic provision-join returned no cases/inquiries/CCB advice/RPR (provisions=[]); every prior action above was found by topical search of OVERTURES.md (and the GA45 O20/O23 predecessor was missed by the looser first pass and the companion O10 file).
