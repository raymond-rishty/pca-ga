# O71 — Amend BCO 5-9.e, 9-2, and 9-7 to Allow Session to Directly Administer Diaconal Ministry
**Targets:** BCO 5-9.e, 9-2, 9-7 · **Source:** Northern California Presbytery · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Overture-71_NCal_DiaconalMinistry.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Overture-71_NCal_DiaconalMinistry.pdf)
**What it does:** Lets a Session, with three-quarters congregational ratification, directly administer diaconal ministry in lieu of electing deacons, and appoint unordained, non-elected lay volunteers (men and women) to assist — expressly forbidding that they be called deacon/deaconess or be ordained or installed as officers; conforms 5-9.e and 9-7 accordingly.
**Cites in its own grounds:** None (cites only Scripture — Titus 1:5, Phil 1:1, 1 Tim 3, Acts 6, Acts 20:17 — plus BCO 9-1 and a Ramsay / Morton Smith quotation; no prior cases, overtures, or inquiries).

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
- **[2009-25/2009-26 — Brown v. Northern California Presbytery / Session of Grace PC v. Northern California Presbytery](../cases/ga39_2011__2009-25_2009-26.md)** (GA39/2011, sustained) — Same presbytery as O71; the SJC adjudicated NorCal's permitted views on the diaconate and how diaconal ministry may be organized, directly construing BCO 9-2/9-7. Most on-point precedent. [minutes](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p550)
- **[2011-14 — Reese and Bech v. Philadelphia Presbytery](../cases/ga42_2014__2011-14.md)** (GA42/2014, sustained) — Construes the office of deacon under BCO 9-2 in the context of a candidate's refusal to ordain men as deacons and use of a "mercy" model — bears on whether diaconal function may be detached from the ordained office. [minutes](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p530)
- **[Case 2007-13 — Kniseley v. Rocky Mountain](../cases/ga37_2009__2007-13.md)** (GA37/2009, denied) — Addresses who may be a member of a "diaconate" and restricts the term, construing BCO 9-2/9-7 on diaconal membership and the elder/deacon two-office structure. [minutes](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p557)

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **38-34 II.E** (GA38/2010) — From Northern California Presbytery itself; CCB found an overture appointing unordained persons to diaconal ministry in conflict (it implied an unordained office contrary to BCO 17-1). The precise design O71 now attempts, and a prior NorCal attempt — O71's "no lay volunteer … called deacon/deaconess … not ordained or installed" wording appears engineered to answer this conflict. [page](../inquiries/ga38_2010__ci04.md)
- **38-34 II.A** (GA38/2010) — CCB found an overture prohibiting deaconesses in conflict because "deaconess" need not denote an ordained office; frames the 9-7 boundary O71 works within. [page](../inquiries/ga38_2010__ci02.md)
- **38-34 II.D** (GA38/2010) — CCB found an overture prohibiting commissioned/installed assistants to deacons in conflict on the same grounds; O71 likewise creates unordained assistants but forbids commissioning/installing them. [page](../inquiries/ga38_2010__ci03.md)
- **App. O II.H** (GA46/2018) — CCB found Grace Presbytery's deaconess/assistants overture in conflict, holding the Session (not the congregation) is the appointing body requiring vows; bears on O71's congregational-ratification mechanism and Session appointment of volunteers. [page](../inquiries/ga46_2018__ci04.md)
- **II.Q** (GA47/2019) — CCB found Metro New York's overture letting individual sessions decide locally whether women may serve as deacons NOT in conflict; closest favorable analog to O71's local-Session-discretion structure (BCO 9-3, 7-2). [page](../inquiries/ga47_2019__ci17.md)
