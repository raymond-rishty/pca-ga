# O4 — Docket a Prayer of Thanksgiving for Adult Professions of Faith
**Overtures Committee (2026) recommends:** Answer in the affirmative *(vote 129-0-0)*
**Targets:** (prayer — non-provision overture) · **Source:** Pacific Northwest Presbytery (to OC) · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/Overture-4_PNW_Thanksgiving.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/Overture-4_PNW_Thanksgiving.pdf)

**What it does:** Asks the 53rd GA to offer a prayer of thanksgiving to God during the Assembly in Louisville for the 5,656 adult professions of faith reported in the PCA Statistical Report; appends a per-presbytery table of churches reporting the most adult professions in 2024.

**Cites in its own grounds:** None (Scripture — Luke 15:6-7 — plus the PCA Statistical Report and the 52nd GA Commissioner Handbook pp. 307, 331 for the statistics; no prior GA cases, overtures, inquiries, or BCO provisions).

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
None found. (A docketed corporate prayer of thanksgiving raises no constitutional/judicial question; the deterministic spine returns no provision and there is no judicial action on point.)

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **CCB II.C** (GA47/2019, not in conflict; adopted by the CCB) — CCB advice on Overture 3 (Northern New England) amending RAO 10-3 to "encourage godly prudence in planning corporate prayer and worship at the General Assembly"; bears as the nearest constitutional treatment of corporate prayer/worship on the Assembly's own docket, though it concerns an RAO amendment rather than a one-time prayer resolution. ### Note
Non-provision (prayer) overture — the exact-provision spine returns provisions=[], so all bearing actions come from topical search of /workspace/index/OVERTURES.md and CCB-OVERTURE-ADVICE.md. The overture cites nothing in its own grounds. The single on-point precedent is GA19 O41 (a GA-adopted thanksgiving); GA15 O32 and GA8 O24 bear on dedicating Assembly time to corporate prayer/worship. Pruned the "day of prayer for an external cause" overtures (GA2 O1 missions; GA3 O20 fasting; GA14 O49 & GA15 O49 farm crisis; GA22 O25/O34 Korea; GA24 O39 elections) down to one representative (GA44 O32) — those direct prayer *outward by the churches* rather than offering thanksgiving *during the Assembly*, so they bear only on genre, not substance. No ⭐ recent (GA48–52) overtures exist on this topic; the most recent comparable is GA44 (2016). CCB II.C is the nearest constitutional touchpoint but is tangential (an RAO amendment, not a prayer resolution). Cross-link: O4 sits among the GA53 "prayer/thanksgiving" overtures — O50 (Day of Fasting and Prayer) and O66/O76/O89 (Declarations of Thanksgiving for the 250th anniversary) — none of which addresses adult professions of faith.
