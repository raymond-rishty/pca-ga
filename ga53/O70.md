# O70 — Expand Korean Northwest Boundaries to Include Alaska, Hawaii, Oregon, and Washington
**MNA recommends:** Answer in the affirmative
**Targets:** (boundaries — non-provision overture) · **Source:** Korean Northwest Presbytery (to MNA) · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Overture-70_KoreanNW_Boundaries.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Overture-70_KoreanNW_Boundaries.pdf)

> **Assembly voted:** answer in the affirmative
>
> *Referred by the Stated Clerk to MNA; not acted on by the Overtures Committee.*

**What it does:** Asks GA53 to add the states of Alaska, Hawaii, Oregon, and Washington to the boundaries of Korean Northwest Presbytery, effective immediately. The grounds note that the 9 Korean Language Presbyteries currently cover 41 states, leaving 9 states unassigned, and that although these presbyteries are "non-geographical" relative to the wider PCA they are geographical relative to one another.

**Cites in its own grounds:** The 10th General Assembly's continuation of Korean Language Presbyteries since 1982 (M10GA, p. 92-93); and BCO 13-2 Editorial Comment (for the "non-geographical" status of the language presbyteries). No prior judicial cases or specific prior overtures are cited.

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **CI 10-77** (GA10 / 1982) — Constitutionality of a non-geographical presbytery under BCO 11-4: the CCB found the BCO does not address non-geographical presbyteries, that BCO 11-4 implies geographical bounds while BCO 13-1 does not limit "bounds" geographically. This is the foundational ruling permitting the Korean Language Presbyteries that O70 relies on, and it frames exactly the "geographical vs. non-geographical bounds" tension O70 invokes. [page](../inquiries/ga10_1982__ci04.md)

### Prior overtures
- **[GA52 O2](../overtures/ga52_2025__o2.md)** (2025, Adopted final) — Expand Boundaries of South Florida Presbytery to Include Puerto Rico and US Virgin Islands. The most recent precedent for a presbytery expanding into new, previously-unassigned territory; adopted as final, showing the current GA's posture toward such extensions. ⭐ [p.1168](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1166), [p.1359](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1357)
- **[GA42 O25](../overtures/ga42_2014__o25.md)** (2014, Referred) — Expand Boundaries of Korean Southeastern Presbytery. The closest on-point precedent: a Korean Language Presbytery seeking to enlarge its geographic territory. Outcome (Referred, to MNA) shows such requests are routed through MNA rather than adopted on the floor. [p.81](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p79), [p.827](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p825), [p.888](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p886)
- **[GA42 O26](../overtures/ga42_2014__o26.md)** (2014, Referred) — Divide Korean Southwest Presbytery into Two Presbyteries. Companion 2014 action reorganizing Korean Language Presbytery geography; same referral disposition. [p.81](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p79), [p.828](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p826), [p.888](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p886)
- **[GA39 O5](../overtures/ga39_2011__o5.md)** (2011, Adopted final) — Divide Korean Eastern Presbytery to Create Korean Northeastern Presbytery. Shows the GA does adopt geographic reorganizations of the Korean Language Presbyteries (here creating the Korean Northeastern named in O70's whereas list). [p.80](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p78), [p.620](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p618), [p.679](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p677)
- **[GA10 O21](../overtures/ga10_1982__o21.md)** (1982, Adopted final) — Extend Siouxlands Presbytery Boundaries to Cover Five-State Region. A directly analogous mechanism — extending a presbytery's bounds across multiple states — adopted in the same Assembly that originated the Korean Language Presbyteries (M10GA, cited in O70's grounds). [p.49](../markdown/ga10_1982.md#ga10-p49)
