# Past Actions Bearing on the 90 Overtures to the 53rd PCA General Assembly (2026)

For each of the 90 overtures before the 53rd General Assembly, this document lists the **past actions
that bear on it**: SJC/CJB **judicial cases**, **constitutional inquiries** and CCB **overture/amendment
advice**, **prior overtures**, and **RPR exceptions of substance**. Each entry also captures what the
overture does and any prior action it cites in its own grounds.

**Basis.** Every citation is drawn either from the overture's own PDF (pcaga.org) or from a searchable
corpus of all PCA General Assembly minutes, **1973–2025 (GA1–GA52)** — the judicial-case index (647
SJC/CJB cases), the constitutional-inquiry catalogue, the CCB overture-advice catalogue, the overture
catalogue (~2,028 proposals with dispositions and ratification status), and the RPR exceptions catalogue
(5,941 threaded exceptions of substance, GA18–52). Each finding was cross-searched by **both** the
provision number and the underlying topic; ⭐ marks recent (GA48–52 / 2021–2025) overtures.

**How to read a citation.** Judicial cases are by docket number + parties (e.g. *2024-18 Harris v.
Wisconsin*); inquiries/CCB advice by GA + CCB section; overtures as **GA<N> O<n>** with outcome.
Outcomes for constitutional (BCO) amendments distinguish *Approved & ratified* (final) from *Approved
but not ratified* and *Answered in the negative* (defeated), because a BCO change is only final once
two-thirds of presbyteries ratify and the next GA affirms.

**Coverage of this report:** judicial cases found for 61/90 overtures · inquiries/CCB advice for 82/90 ·
prior overtures for 88/90 · RPR exceptions for 67/90. "None found" is stated honestly where a category
turned up nothing; it does not always mean nothing exists, only that nothing surfaced in this corpus.

**Caveats.** (1) The corpus ends at GA52 (2025); GA53 itself is the present Assembly. (2) BCO sections
were renumbered over the decades — older actions may cite an old number; topic searches mitigate this but
a few deep cuts may be missed. (3) Inquiry/overture *subject titles* are partly machine-generated (~97%
accurate). (4) One user-anticipated item — a constitutional inquiry holding the BCO "silent on who may
serve as clerk" (re O58) — was **not located** in the corpus despite targeted searching; O58 rests on
that asserted silence rather than on a citable ruling.

## Thematic clusters (overtures that share a subject and therefore much of their past-action history)

Many of the 90 overtures travel in families. Reading them together is useful because they draw on the
same cases, inquiries, and prior overtures:

- **Judicial process & discipline (BCO 31–46):** O3, O15, O16, O28, O29, O35, O54, O56, O65, O67, O69,
  O75, O85, O86 — appeals vs. complaints, dismissals, the record of the case, indictment timing, counsel.
- **The diaconate (deacons / women deacons / diaconal ministry):** O34, O36, O37, O38, O71 — the PCA's
  most-overtured topic, anchored on the women-in-office history and SJC *2009-25/26 (Brown/Grace v.
  N. Calif.)*.
- **Offices/titles for the unordained & commissioning:** O14, O48, O52, O74 (new BCO 17A) — closely tied
  to the diaconate cluster and to GA50 O26 (BCO 7-3, ratified 2024).
- **BCO 5 — church planting / mission churches:** O2, O17, O18, O19, O20, O51.
- **Consent of the congregation / who exercises authority:** O1, O13, O24, O39, O49, O55, O57, O72, O82, O84.
- **Ordination requirements (BCO 21):** O12, O25 (plus O42 on the 29-1 Standards reference).
- **RAO 4-9 — Stated Clerk / Coordinator terms & election thresholds:** O40, O41, O43, O73, O77.
- **RAO 8-4.i — floor nominations:** O8, O9, O45, O47.
- **RAO 4-21 — electronic meetings:** O78, O79 (and O11 on MNA data).
- **RPR rules & procedure:** O7, O33, O53.
- **AC / committee & agency structure:** O5, O6, O32, O68.
- **Presbytery boundaries:** O10 ⇄ O87 (the same Pittsburgh/Ohio change from both sides), O44, O62, O70.
- **Declarations / thanksgiving / prayer:** O4, O50, O61 (Danvers), O66 / O76 / O89 (250th anniversary).
- **Ad interim committees on cultural issues:** O80 (CRT) and O90 (Christian Nationalism).
- **The Lord's Supper:** O31 ⇄ O60 (who distributes the elements), O81 (wine + grape juice at GA).
