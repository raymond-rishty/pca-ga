# O37 — Amend BCO 9-3 to Allow Women to Serve as Ordained Deacons
**Targets:** BCO 9-3 (+ 12 conforming amendments: 5-9.c, 7-2, 17-3, 24-1, 24-6, 24-7, 24-8, 24-9, 24-10, 36-7, 37-1, 37-6) · **Source:** Pacific · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-37_Pacific_9-3.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-37_Pacific_9-3.pdf)

**What it does:** Strikes "men" from the deacon qualifications in BCO 9-3 (with 12 conforming amendments throughout the BCO) so that local Sessions may decide whether qualified women may be elected and ordained to the office of deacon; the office of elder remains restricted to men (7-2 amended to read "the office of elder is open to men only").

**Cites in its own grounds:** None — the grounds rest on Scripture (Rom. 16:1; 1 Tim. 3:11), complementarian and Reformed scholarship (Piper, Dever, Köstenberger, Schreiner, Calvin, Warfield, Strimple, Frame, Clowney), church history, WCF 20.2, and the practice of other NAPARC bodies. No prior PCA cases, overtures, or inquiries are cited.

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
- **[2009-25 — Brown v. Northern California Presbytery](../cases/ga39_2011__2009-25_2009-26.md)** (GA39/2011, sustained) — Construes BCO 9-3 directly: complaint sustained against a presbytery over deaconesses in a NorCal church. Bears squarely as judicial enforcement of the current "men"-only reading of 9-3 that O37 would overturn. [minutes](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p550)
- **[2009-26 — Session of Grace Presbyterian Church v. Northern California Presbytery](../cases/ga39_2011__2009-25_2009-26.md)** (GA39/2011, sustained) — Companion complaint on the same NorCal deaconess dispute under BCO 9-3; reinforces SJC enforcement of 9-3 against the practice O37 would legalize. [minutes](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p550)
- **[2007-13 — Kniseley v. Rocky Mountain](../cases/ga37_2009__2007-13.md)** (GA37/2009, denied) — Cited under 9-3; concerned a woman with an M.Div. who was not ordained. Bears more loosely (reception/ordination of a woman, not deaconess commissioning), but sits in the women-in-office frame O37 engages. [minutes](../markdown/ga37_2009.md#ga37-p150)

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **CI 12-53 (III,69)** (GA12/1984, advice given) — "Ordination of men who believe women may serve as deacons"; advised that holding the view (while abiding by BCO standards in practice) is not by itself grounds to deny ordination or reception. Directly bears on the doctrinal-latitude question behind O37 (cites BCO 7-2, 9-3, 21-5). [page](../inquiries/ga12_1984__ci09.md)
- **CCB II.Q** (GA47/2019, not in conflict) — On Overture 29 (Metro New York) proposing to amend multiple BCO provisions so individual sessions could decide locally whether women may serve as deacons; CCB found it not in conflict. This is the closest prior precedent to O37's "let local Sessions decide" mechanism (cites 5-9.c, 5-9.i(1), 7-2, 9-3, 17-3, 24, 25). [page](../inquiries/ga47_2019__ci17.md)
- **CCB II.P** (GA50/2023, not in conflict, 8-0-0) ⭐ — On Overture 23 (Mississippi Valley) amending BCO 8-2 and 9-3 to require officers to conform to biblical chastity in self-description; CCB found it not in conflict. Bears only as a recent CCB construction of the same provision's amendment mechanics, not on the women-deacon question (low-bearing). [page](../inquiries/ga50_2023__ci16.md)

### Prior overtures
- **GA36 O9** (2008, Answered in the negative) — "Erect Ad Interim Study Committee on Deaconesses" (Philadelphia). A defeated denominational attempt to open the women-deacon question; bears as a direct precursor to O37's aim. [page](../markdown/ga36_2008.md#ga36-p205)
- **GA19 O22 / GA20 O33** (1991–93, ratified) — "Amend BCO 24-5 to Permit Session to Ordain Elders & Deacons." Body+title match concerning ordination-*service* mechanics, not the gender qualification; low-bearing (see Note). [GA19 page](../markdown/ga19_1991.md#ga19-p58) · [GA20 page](../markdown/ga20_1992.md#ga20-p58)
- **GA33 O10** (2006, ratified) — "Amend BCO 24-1 to Require Bible Content Examination for Elders and Deacons." Incidental body+title match on "deacons"; does not bear on the women-deacon question (see Note). [page](../markdown/ga33_2005.md#ga33-p200)

### RPR exceptions
- **BCO 9-3** — 9 exceptions of record, the cluster directly bearing: [Korean Southern](../rpr/korean-southern.md) (30th/2002 raised, 31st/2003 unsatisfactory — "Ordination of women as deaconesses not provided for"), [Northern California](../rpr/northern-california.md) (35th/2007, 36th/2008, 40th/2012 — repeated "Deaconesses commissioned contrary to BCO 9-3"), [Philadelphia](../rpr/exc/philadelphia__028.md) (35th/2007), [Korean Southern](../rpr/exc/korean-southern__103.md) (40th/2012 — men and women appointed as deacons), and [Chesapeake](../rpr/chesapeake.md) (42nd/2014 — BCO 7-2 & 9-3 compliance). Documents sustained real-world friction: presbyteries permitting deaconesses against 9-3 — exactly the practice O37 would legalize. [by provision](../index/RPR-BY-PROVISION.md)

### Note
**Topical supplement — the provision-exact spine misses the main battleground.** The central PCA debate on women deacons ran through provisions *other than* 9-3, so the deterministic join did not surface it. The GA38 (2010) cluster is the key historical record and bears strongly on O37: **GA38 O7** "Reconsider Ordination of Women to the Office of Deacon" (Adopted, [p.386](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p384)); **GA38 O2** "Amend BCO 9-7 to Prohibit Deaconesses" ([p.388](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p386)); **GA38 O16** "Affirm the Permissibility of Unordained Deaconesses in the PCA" ([p.392](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p390)); **GA38 O25** "Reaffirm BCO Standards on Roles of Men and Women in Church Office" (Answered in the negative, [p.396](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p394)). Earlier: **GA19 O40** "Declare Ordination of Deaconesses a Violation of BCO" ([p.61](../markdown/ga19_1991.md#ga19-p59)) and **GA22 O8** "Encourage Godly Men & Women to Assist Deacons" ([p.65](../markdown/ga22_1994.md#ga22-p63)). Most directly, **GA46 O9** "Amend BCO 9-7 and Add BCO 24-11 Regarding Women Serving as Deaconesses" (Grace, approved → sent to presbyteries, [p.686](../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p684)) is the most recent prior amendment attempt on this question — but it pursued *unordained* deaconesses via 9-7/24-11, whereas O37 seeks *ordained* women deacons via 9-3, a more direct change than any prior PCA overture.
