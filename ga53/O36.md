# O36 — Add BCO 8-11 and 9-8 Regarding the Exercise of the Duties and Authority of Elder and Deacon
**Targets:** BCO 8-11 (new), 9-8 (new) · **Source:** Catawba Valley (to CCB, OC) · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Overture-36_CatawbaValley_8-11.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Overture-36_CatawbaValley_8-11.pdf)

**What it does:** Adds a new BCO 8-11 reserving the duties and authority of elders (leading worship, voting in Session, shepherding) to ordained elders, and a new BCO 9-8 reserving diaconal duties and authority (mercy-ministry leadership, voting in diaconal meetings, financial distributions, property oversight) to ordained deacons; both bar women and unordained men from performing those functions or being commissioned/appointed/installed in any manner implying parity with the ordained office. It also encourages presbyteries to review congregational practice for alignment.

**Cites in its own grounds:** 1 Tim 3:1–13; Titus 1:5–9; BCO 7-2 ("ordination… limited to qualified men"); and "recent General Assembly actions, including the affirmation of Overture 26 (2023)" — i.e., GA50 (2023) O26 "Amend BCO 7-3 to Prohibit Ecclesiastical Titles for Unordained People" (Approved & ratified 2024). No case or inquiry cited by number.

### Judicial cases (SJC/CJB)
- **[2009-25/2009-26 — Brown v. Northern California Presbytery / Session of Grace Presbyterian Church v. Northern California Presbytery](../cases/ga39_2011__2009-25_2009-26.md)** (39th/2011, sustained) — Directly construes the underlying question O36 targets: whether and how women/unordained persons may perform diaconal functions. The SJC annulled Northern California Presbytery's abstract resolution declaring six diaconal views permissible, holding no court may issue binding abstract decrees beyond its jurisdiction; a concurring opinion analyzed each view of the diaconate (appointed/elected deaconess, "equal partners," "nobody ordained," GUMP and Kniseley standards). Topical supplement — not in the provision-exact spine because O36 adds entirely new sections. [minutes](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p548)

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **38-34 II.E** (38th/2010, in conflict) — CCB found an overture appointing unordained persons (men and women) to diaconal ministry roles in conflict with the Constitution, including because it implied an unordained office contrary to BCO 17-1; lists BCO 9-8 among the provisions in tension. Bears directly on O36's aim to fence diaconal functions to the ordained office, and shows the CCB has scrutinized legislation in this exact area. [page](../inquiries/ga38_2010__ci04.md)

### Prior overtures
- **GA38 O10** (2010, Answered in the negative) — "Amend BCO 1-4, 4-2, 5-10, 7-2, 9-2, 9-7 and Add BCO 9-8 for Unordained Diaconal Ministry" (Northern California Presbytery). The closest prior attempt to legislate a new BCO 9-8 about unordained diaconal service — it moved the opposite direction (enabling unordained diaconal ministry) and the Assembly declined it. Most directly bearing precedent on the section O36 now seeks to add. [p.394](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p392), [p.409](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p407), [p.776](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p774)
