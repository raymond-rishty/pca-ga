# O51 — Amend BCO 5 to add a Section on 'Assisted Churches'
**Overtures Committee (2026) recommends:** Refer back *(vote 114-14-2)*
**Targets:** BCO 5 (new 5-11) · **Source:** MS Valley · **PDF:** [https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-51_MSValley_5-11.pdf](https://pcaga.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Overture-51_MSValley_5-11.pdf)

> *Referred back to Mississippi Valley Presbytery.*

**What it does:** Adds a new Section C to BCO Chapter 5 (paras 5-11.a–c) creating a third church category — the "Assisted Church": a church with only one ruling elder and no pastor that has been assigned a BCO 15-1 commission to serve as a temporary Session (cf. BCO 5-3.c). Presbytery must acknowledge the condition, choose among courses of action (train more ruling elders, or recommend dissolution/merger/reorganization under BCO 13-9.f), and review the church's status at least annually.

**Cites in its own grounds:** Cross-references BCO 15-1, BCO 5-3.c, and BCO 13-9.f in the proposed text, and appeals to the BCO Preliminary Principles. No prior case, overture, or inquiry cited by number.

### Constitutional inquiries / CCB advice
- **App. O II.E / 39-CI** (GA39/2011) — "Temporary governance for churches lacking ruling elders": CCB found a New Jersey overture on temporary governance for elder-less churches *may have been in conflict* with BCO Preliminary Principles 2 & 6 and BCO 12-1 if read as prescriptive. The single most on-point action — a prior attempt at exactly O51's idea, and a drafting-caution flag for O51's mandatory presbytery duties. [page](../inquiries/ga39_2011__ci01.md)
- **App. O II.G / 44-CI** (GA44/2016) — CCB found a mission-church minute-reporting overture in conflict with BCO 5-3.a because an evangelist, unlike a governing body, has no minutes to submit — construes the Chapter 5 temporary-government machinery O51 extends. [page](../inquiries/ga44_2016__ci02.md)
- **CI #15 / 14-86** (GA14/1986) — Whether a congregation may dissolve its session: CCB advised it cannot, but Presbytery may assume jurisdiction under BCO 13-9 — bears on Presbytery's authority over a church lacking a functioning governing body, the gap O51 fills. [page](../inquiries/ga14_1986__ci18.md)
- **16-77 III** (GA16/1988) — Membership status and session authority upon dissolution of a congregation — the downstream of O51's 5-11.b(2) dissolution/reorganization path. [page](../inquiries/ga16_1988__ci08.md)
- **II.H / 51-CI** (GA51/2024) — CCB found Overture 10 (add Presbytery care for TEs to BCO 13-9) not in conflict — recent CCB treatment of the BCO 13-9 care obligations O51 invokes via 13-9.f. ⭐ [page](../inquiries/ga51_2024__ci08.md)

The provision-exact join was empty because BCO 5-11 is a *new* section (no past action can cite a provision that does not yet exist), so this page rests on the topical supplement. Renumbering caveat: Chapter 5 was renumbered at GA38/2011 (new 5-5 inserted, sections shifted +1; see bco_renumberings.jsonl), so the GA41/GA42 "Add Sections 5-11, 5-12" overtures use post-2011 numbering and are genuine prior attempts to occupy the very slot O51 now seeks; GA38 O15 is the renumbering that fixed today's 5-11 endpoint. Pruning: the only cases surfaced concern multi-site particularization and inter-denominational discipline, not a session-less/declining church, and were dropped as incidental. Strongest bearing actions are GA39 App. O II.E (a prior near-identical idea, flagged as possibly in conflict) and the BCO 5-3.c RPR cluster (GA51–52).
