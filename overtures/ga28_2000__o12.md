# GA28 O12 — Amend BCO 12-5 to Prohibit Women from Preaching in Public Worship

**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 12 From Potomac Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Approved but not ratified  ·  **BCO:** 12-5

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 283](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p283)*

> *ITEM 1: add a new clause at BCO 12.5d...Defeated FOR 31 AGAINST 23*

“Prohibit Women from Preaching in Public Worship”

Whereas, questions have been raised across the church concerning the legitimate role of women in the PCA, and whether the constitution of the PCA is sufficiently explicit to guard against unbiblical practices with respect to the ministry of women in our churches; Therefore, Potomac Presbytery respectfully overtures the 28th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to proceed to the amendment of Book of Church Order to the following effect: add a new clause at BCO 12.5d., after the words “Bible study groups;”, as follows: to encourage the women in the church to hold meetings and give instruction appropriate to their calling (e.g. Titus 2:3-5), while prohibiting women from expounding or preaching the word of god a san ordinance of public worship (cf. BCO 4-4; 53-2), as this is clearly forbidden in Scripture (1 Tim. 2:11-12); Adopted by Potomac Presbytery at the 41st Stated Meeting, February 12, 2000. Attested by: /s/ Richard R. Larson, Stated Clerk S.
