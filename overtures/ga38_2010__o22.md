**Assembly:** 38th (2010)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 22 from Savannah River Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga38_2010 p. 313](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p313)*

---
