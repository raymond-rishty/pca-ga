# GA52 O18 — Direct RUF to Update Campus Ministry Manual for GA Approval

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 18 from the Gulf Coast Presbytery (to RUF)

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1204](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1204)*

---

“Direct RUF to Update “Campus Ministry Manual” for GA Approval” **Be it resolved** Gulf Coast Presbytery overtures the 52 [nd] General Assembly (2025) to direct the Permanent Committee of RUF, with the RUF Coordinator, to revise the 1979 “Campus Ministry Manual” in order to reflect the 2024 approved Affiliation Agreement and previously issued Assembly directives, and to present the same to the 54 [th] General Assembly (2027) through its Committee of Commissioners. This updated manual shall also include, but not be limited to, policies regarding: recruiting, assessing, hiring or promoting prospective TEs to the positions of Campus Minister and area Coordinator, starting new works and closing works (including for international and crosscultural works), yearly RUF training, Summer Conference, etc. **Rationale:** Reformed University Fellowship, since its inception, has expanded to over 195 works worldwide. Through much hard work and collaboration, RUF has recently passed through the General Assembly its most recent Affiliation Agreement. However, the last policy manual RUF presented for approval by the PCA was in 1979 (nearly half a century ago). _BCO_ 14-1.12 recognizes RUF is a permanent committee of the General Assembly. And according to _BCO_ 14-1 the General Assembly is the court for RUF. But its current structure and practices do not reflect the 1979 “Campus Ministry Manual.” Since the year 2000 (when RUF became a Permanent Committee of the PCA), no updates to its manual have been presented, though required by the General Assembly.
