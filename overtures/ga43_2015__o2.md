# GA43 O2 — Form Ad Interim Committee to Revise WCF 21-8 & WLC 117, 119 & WSC 60-61 on Sabbath

**Assembly:** 43rd (2015)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 2 from North Texas Presbytery (to OC, CCB, AC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga43_2015 p. 601](../markdown/ga43_2015.md#ga43-p601)*

---
