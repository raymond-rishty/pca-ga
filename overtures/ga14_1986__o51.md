# GA14 O51 — Amend BCO 14-2 to Base Presbytery Commissioner Allotment on Number of Churches

**Assembly:** 14th (1986)  ·  **Source:** Southwest Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Approved → sent to presbyteries; ratification not located  ·  **BCO:** 14-2

*Source: [ga14_1986 p. 48](../markdown/ga14_1986.md#ga14-p48)*

---

> *Overture 51 from Westminster Presbytery (to base allotment on number of churches) was answered 'See 14-31, 2, p. 106,' the same Ad Interim Committee recommendation that was recommitted rather than adopted. The 1986-1987 BCO voting record in ga15_1987 (lines 13486-13518) shows only BCO 45 and BCO 42/43 sent to presbyteries from the 14th GA; BCO 14-2 does not appear. No ratification vote was ever he*

Whereas the Ad Interim Committee on the General Assembly has worked diligently on the task assigned to it by the 12th General Assembly; and

Whereas we do not believe the issues assumed by the majority report are substantive to the degree that warrant their proposals; and

Whereas such drastic changes are not needed in the Presbyterian Church in America; Therefore Southwest Presbytery respectfully overtures the 14th General Assembly to dismiss the Ad Interim Committee on the General Assembly with thanks. Adopted at the Spring Stated Meeting of the Presbytery of the Southwest, April 24-25, 1986. Attest: Charles E. Turner, Stated Clerk Answered in the negative, 14-85, III, 1, p. 187.
