“Affirm the PCA’s Commitment to the Sanctity of Biblical Sexual Relationships”

**Whereas,** the Scripture is infallible and inerrant, the only rule of faith and practice, and the only touchstone of truth; and

**Whereas**, the Constitution of the Presbyterian Church in America “is subject to and subordinate to the Scriptures of the Old and New Testaments;” and

**Whereas,** every officer (teaching elders, ruling elders, and deacons) in the Presbyterian Church in America vows that they “believe the Scriptures of the Old and New Testaments, as originally given, to be the inerrant Word of God, the only infallible rule of faith and practice;” and

**Whereas**, all church courts in the Presbyterian Church in America have the power “to bear testimony against error in doctrine and immorality,” “to require obedience to the laws of Christ,” and “to cut off the contumacious and impenitent from the congregation of believers” ( _BCO_ 11-2); and

**Whereas**, every PCA Presbytery has power “to condemn erroneous opinions which injure the purity and peace of the Church” ( _BCO_ 13-9f); and

**Whereas**, the Revoice Conference, held at Memorial Presbyterian Church in St. Louis, Missouri on July 26-28, 2018, was for the purpose of “Supporting, encouraging, and empowering gay, lesbian, same-sex attracted and other LGBT+ Christians;” and
