# GA47 O12 — Amend RAO 8-4 Regarding Floor Nominations

**Assembly:** 47th (2019)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 12 from Northern New England Presbytery (to CCB, OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga47_2019 p. 618](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p618)*

“Amend _RAO_ 8-4 regarding Floor Nominations”

**Whereas**, subjection to the brethren is an oath taken by both Teaching and Ruling Elders in the PCA ( _BCO_ 21-5.6; 24-6.5) and such subjection begins with those brothers closest in relationship to said officer; and

**Whereas**, our form of government seeks to give deference to and maintain the authority of lower courts; and

**Whereas**, floor nomination of officers to fill open positions on the Permanent Committees, Agencies, and the Standing Judicial Commission of the General Assembly are, by definition, not made through such lower courts; and

**Whereas**, the rise of celebrity officers within Christ’s Church is a worrisome trend that can lead to polarization and division within Christ’s body (1 Corinthians 1:10-14); and floor nominations can serve as an avenue for the furtherance of this trend; and

**Whereas**, the undertaking of service within the Agencies and Committees of the General Assembly is one to be considered with deliberation and prayer; and

**Whereas**, the Nominating Committee is entrusted with the important task of evaluating and nominating

**Therefore be it resolved**, Northern New England Presbytery hereby overtures the 47th General Assembly to restrict floor nominations at the General Assembly for the Assembly Committees and Agencies to those whose names were submitted to the Nominating Committee according to _RAO_ 8-4.d and e, by adding a final sentence to _RAO_ 8-4.i as indicated below.
