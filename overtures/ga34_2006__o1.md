“Enlarge Boundaries of Covenant Presbytery”

**Be it resolved** that Covenant Presbytery overtures the 34 [th] General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to enlarge the boundaries of Covenant Presbytery by adding three counties in Missouri which are now part of Missouri presbytery: Jasper, McDonald and Newton. _Adopted by Covenant Presbytery at the stated meeting on May 24, 2005_ _Attested by /s/ Grover E.
