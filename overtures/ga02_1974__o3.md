# GA2 O3 — Affirm Members' Right to Hold Membership in Secret Societies

**Assembly:** 2nd (1974)  ·  **Source:** Rev. Eurie Hayes Smith, III et al  ·  **Disposition:** Other

*Source: [ga02_1974 p. 25](../markdown/ga02_1974.md#ga02-p25)*

---

FATHERS AND BRETHREN: To the Reverend General Assembly, First Presbyterian Church, Macon, Georgia Whereas certain branches of the Christian Church have limited or implied a limit to the free exercise of the conscience of its office bearers and membership by restricting them from secret associations and fraternities such as the Masonic order. Now therefore, we respectfully overture the Second General Assembly of the National Presbyterian Church to affirm the right of its office bearers and constituency to hold such membership in accordance with the Westminster Confession of Faith chapters 20:II, and 22:I, II, VIIa. Rev. Eurie Hayes Smith III Rev. Edwin P. Elliott, Jr. Junius W. Brown H. H. Stillions
