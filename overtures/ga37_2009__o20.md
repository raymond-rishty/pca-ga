**Assembly:** 37th (2009)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 20 from Northern California Presbytery (TO MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga37_2009 p. 241](../markdown/ga37_2009.md#ga37-p241)*

---
