# GA15 O11 — Amend BCO 19-2 to Require Examination of Candidates Against 1 Timothy 3 Qualifications

**Assembly:** 15th (1987)  ·  **Source:** Delmarva Presbytery  ·  **BCO:** 19-2

*Source: [ga15_1987 p. 48](../markdown/ga15_1987.md#ga15-p48)*

---
