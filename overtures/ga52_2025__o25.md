# GA52 O25 — Amend BCO 46-4 to Add Associate Membership for Honorably Retired TEs

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 25 from the Northwest Georgia Presbytery (to CCB, OC)  ·  **BCO:** 46-4

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1240](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1240)*

---

“Amend _BCO_ 46-4 to Add a Class of Associate Membership for Honorably Retired TEs” **Whereas** our current _BCO_ 46-4 defines an associate member as those believers temporarily residing in a location other than their permanent homes; **Whereas** the aim of this overture is pastoral and not for the purpose of changing our polity; or the needs of TEs called to works which are not to a local church. (Those differing calls as far as a defined relationship to a local church require a more complex study); **Whereas** ministers of the gospel who have served the church well and received Honorably Retired (HR) status may need ongoing pastoral care and vital relationships that local churches can give but is not practical for Presbytery Shepherding committees or Pastoral Care Committee to implement. The pastoral care of an HR minister would be a good application of I Timothy 5:10 coupled with the charge of John 13:14 and more suitable for deacons and elders of a local church to tend to their needs, Paul’s Partnership with the Philippian church was vital to his wellbeing and sanctification. (Phil.1:1-4); **Whereas** we already have a provision to assign ministers under discipline to a local church ( _BCO_ 46-8). We hold to two types of discipline, one being the regular preaching of the Word, and the other which is discipline for the unrepentant/repenting brother or sister.
