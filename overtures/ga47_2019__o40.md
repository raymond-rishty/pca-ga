“Overture for Sessions to Acknowledge and Support Women Leaders without Delay or More Divisiveness”

**Whereas**, the PCA has devoted many hours of debate, study, and discussion to the role of women in the church—and such does not seem to be yielding progress or resolution but rather much distraction from mission, along with much quarreling and divisiveness (1 Cor. 6:7); and

**Whereas**, a recent _Ad Interim_ Committee recommended commissioning women to serve other women and their ministry in the local church; and

**Whereas**, each local church may and should use the gifts of women as that local church sees fit, under the authority and norms of Scripture—and the Resolution below requires no higher court voting or constitutional amendment; and

**Whereas**, the PCA has an institution already in existence that could launch and nurture many gifts for ministry by our godly sisters, with no required _BCO_ changes or time-consuming debates; and

**Whereas**, most of our female members do not desire ordination as an aspiration or prerequisite to carry out gospel ministry; and

**Whereas**, rather than dividing and quarreling over this matter in the years to come; and

**Whereas**, _BCO_ 9-7 presently allows Sessions to recruit godly women in ministries in the local church under the authority and norms of Scripture; and
