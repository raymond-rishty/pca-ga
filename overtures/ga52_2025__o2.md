# GA52 O2 — Expand Boundaries of South Florida Presbytery

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 2 from South Florida Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1168](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1168)*

---

“Expand Boundaries of South Florida Presbytery”

**Whereas**, South Florida Presbytery is the geographically closest PCA presbytery to the Caribbean Sea,

**Whereas**, the United States has two inhabited territories in the Caribbean Sea, namely Puerto Rico and the US Virgin Islands, and

**Whereas**, Briarwood Presbyterian Church and Evangel Presbytery established PCA works in Puerto Rico, and

**Whereas**, Briarwood Presbyterian Church and Evangel Presbytery requested that South Florida Presbytery assume jurisdiction over the PCA works in Puerto Rico, and

**Whereas**, South Florida Presbytery received the PCA works in Puerto Rico, and

**Whereas**, South Florida Presbytery also received inquiries from PCA members living in the US Virgin Islands about establishing a PCA work there, and

**Whereas**, South Florida Presbytery already includes the Cayman Islands (a British territory in the Caribbean Sea),

**Now therefore be it resolved** that the South Florida Presbytery overture the 52 [nd] General Assembly to restructure the boundary of the South Florida Presbytery to include Puerto Rico and the US Virgin Islands, effective July 1, 2025. _Adopted by the South Florida Presbytery at its stated meeting, August 13,_ _2024._ _Attested by /s/ TE Robbie Crouse, stated clerk_
