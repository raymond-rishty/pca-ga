“Declare Full Preterism a Heresy” At its 32nd Stated Meeting on August 21, 1999, Heartland Presbytery took the following action: 99-79. Overture to General Assembly to adopt presbytery’s position paper on full preterism It was moved and seconded that General Assembly be overtured to adopt Heartland Presbytery’s position paper on full preterism. Adopted. I am attaching the resolution and the supporting position paper which were the basis for the above excerpted action Heartland Presbytery respectfully overtures the Presbyterian Church in America General Assembly to adopt this position paper as the General Assembly position on full preterism. Attested by: Is/ Lawrence N.
