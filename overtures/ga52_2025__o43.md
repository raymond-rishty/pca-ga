“Amend _RAO_ 4-11 regarding Data on Age and Ethnicity”

**Whereas** _RAO_ 4-11 authorizes the Administrative Committee to collect statistical information for the purpose of providing and publishing “guidelines for the Partnership Share Giving Program of the PCA, including the Ministry Ask” and for no other stated reason; and,

**Whereas** _RAO_ 4-11 specifies that said “guidelines shall include statistical information expressed both in per capita giving terms and in proportional (percentage) giving terms to demonstrate the giving necessary to attain full funding for budgets of the General Assembly Committees and Agencies” and nothing more; and,

**Whereas** the Office of the Stated Clerk, through a note contained ina box on the bottom of page 2 of the 2024 Annual Statistical Report Form, informed the congregations of the PCA that “two new line items” regarding “communicant member ethnicity and age” would appear “on the 2025 Annual Statistical Report Form” to be sent out for completion in 2026; and,
