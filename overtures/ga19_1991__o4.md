# GA19 O4 — Amend BCO 15-4 to Insert "Ordinarily" Allowing GA to Adjudicate Directly

**Assembly:** 19th (1991)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 4 From the Presbytery of New River

*Source: [ga19_1991 p. 75](../markdown/ga19_1991.md#ga19-p75)*

---

"Disclaimer on CE&P Bookstore Materials"

Whereas, the PCA is a confessional church seeking to uphold the truths found in the Constitutional documents of the church; and

Whereas, the CE&P Committee exists to serve the church and has a responsibility to uphold the Standards of the church;

Therefore be it resolved that we, the New River Presbytery, do overture the 19th General Assembly to adopt a resolution instructing the Permanent CE&P Committee to put a disclaimer into its sales materials, informing the buying public that not all of the materials advertised through CE&P are reflective of the views of the Reformed Faith or of the Standards of the PCA. The Language of the disclaimer is left in good faith to the discretion of the CE&P Committee or those empowered on its behalf. Adopted at the fall Stated Meeting of New River Presbytery on November 10, 1990. Attested by: /s/ Virgil B. Roberts Stated Clerk 12. That Overture 34 be answered in the negative. Adopted Grounds: CE/P has already initiated this process. (See minutes of Permanent Committee, Feb. 21-22, 1991).
