# GA51 O12 — Adjust Boundary Between Catawba Valley and Piedmont Triad Presbyteries

**Assembly:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 12 from Catawba Valley Presbytery (to MNA)

*Source: [ga51_2024 p. 1096](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p1096)*

---
