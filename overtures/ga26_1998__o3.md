# GA26 O3 — Amend RAO 4 by Adding New Section 18 Allowing Nominations from Committees and Agencies

**Assembly:** 26th (1998)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 3 From New River Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga26_1998 p. 192](../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p192)*

---

“Amend RAO 4, Adding New Section 18, Allowing Nominations From Committees and Agencies”

Whereas, Article VI of the Bylaws of the PCA permits the Boards of Trustees of Insurance, Annuities & Relief, Covenant College; Covenant Theological Seminary and the PCA Foundation to make requests to the Presbyteries to nominate specific men to their respective boards, and

Whereas, article vi of the bylaws of the PCA permits the boards of trustees of Covenant College, Covenant Theological Seminary and the PCA Foundation to submit to the General Assembly Nominating Committee letters of recommendation concerning particular nominees for their respective boards, and

Whereas, past actions of the general assemblies of the PCAin approving the procedures mentioned above presumes that these Boards have good and valid reasons to be given such permission, and

Whereas, the Bible clearly teaches that there should be no partiality shown in the work of the church (e.g. Deut. 1:17, 10:17; Mark 10:35-45; 1 Timothy 5:21), and

Whereas, the Book of Church Order (14-1-3) spells out this Biblical principal when it states: “The work of the Church as set forth in the Great Commission is one work, being implemented at the General Assembly level through equally essential committees” (emphasis added), and
