# GA30 O4 — Amend BCO 21-4 to Require Recording of Presbytery-Allowed Exceptions to Standards

**Assembly:** 30th (2002)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 4 from Ohio Valley Presbytery (to CCB & B&O)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 34-1

*Source: [ga30_2002 p. 240](../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p240)*

---

“Handling Exceptions to the Westminster Standards” Add two new paragraphs in BCO 21-4 where deemed appropriate by the Committee on Constitutional Business: “When Presbytery allows a man to hold an exception to the standards, it shall also determine the man may hold the exception in belief only, whether he may teach it, and whether he may practice it either in his private life or in his public ministry all so long as he does not disturb the peace and purity of the church.” “The Stated Clerk of Presbytery shall cause the man’s allowed exception(s) stated in his own words, along with the Presbytery’s action on the exception(s), to be recorded along with the ministerial obligation he is required to sign. This document will be attached to the minutes of Presbytery as an appendix.” 4.
