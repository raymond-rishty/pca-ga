# GA30 O34 — Counsel Assembly-Level Personnel Not to Participate in PPLN or Lose Posts

**Assembly:** 30th (2002)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 34 from Westminster Presbytery (to B&O)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga30_2002 p. 281](../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p281)*

---
