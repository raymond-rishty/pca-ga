“A Declaration Rejecting All Evolutionary Views of Adam’s Origin”

**Whereas**, questions about the origin and existence of Adam have become common within evangelicalism so that the June 2011 _Christianity Today_ featured an article entitled “The Search for the Historical Adam: Some scholars believe genome science casts doubt on the existence of the first man and woman.
