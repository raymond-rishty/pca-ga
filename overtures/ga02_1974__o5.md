# GA2 O5 — Remove GA Examining Committee Provisions Contrary to Presbyterian Polity

**Assembly:** 2nd (1974)  ·  **Source:** First Presbyterian Church, Belzoni, Mississippi  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga02_1974 p. 27](../markdown/ga02_1974.md#ga02-p27)*

---

FATHERS AND BRETHREN: The Reverend General Assembly, First Presterian Church, Macon, Georgia

Whereas, the Book of Church Order provides for the examination and certification of every officer and member in our denomination, and

Whereas, the Book of Church Order also constitutes an examining committee of the General Assembly which contradicts the above mentioned practice and violates Presbyterian polity principles.

Now, therefore, the Session of the First Presbyterian Church respectfully overtures the Second General Assembly to remove that Section (lines 22-29) on page 23 of the Book of Church Order and thereby return these duties to the appropriate lower court. Rev. Eurie Hayes Smith III, Moderator
