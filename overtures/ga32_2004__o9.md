**Assembly:** 32nd (2004)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 9 from Grace Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga32_2004 p. 168](../markdown/ga32_2004.md#ga32-p168)*

**Whereas,** synods and councils may err (WCF 31-34), and
