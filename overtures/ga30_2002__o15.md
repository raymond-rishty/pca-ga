# GA30 O15 — Amend BCO 24-1 to Replace "persons" With "men"

**Assembly:** 30th (2002)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 15 from Rocky Mountain Presbytery (to CCB & B&O)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 24-1

*Source: [ga30_2002 p. 262](../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p262)*

---

“Amend BCO 24-1 to Specify “men” Rather Than “persons” Whereas, the Bible makes clear that the office of elder is for men only (see I Tim 2:12, I Tim 3:2 and Titus 1:6 [husband of one wife]) and Whereas, the PCA has consistently and unashamedly held this view and Whereas, while there may be some Biblical and exegetical disagreement about the ordination of women to the office of deacon among some in the PCA; yet at present the office of deacon is also available only to men (see I Timothy 3:2 [husband of one wife]) and Whereas, the BCO 24-1, line one, says “persons” which is gender neutral rather than men; Therefore Rocky Mountain Presbytery, meeting on April 26, 2001, respectfully overtures the General Assembly to replace the word “persons” in line one of BCO 24-1, with word “men”. The new reading would then be “Every church shall elect men to the offices of ruling elder and deacon ...” 12.
