# GA26 O13 — Commend Dr. Paul Gilchrist for His Service as Stated Clerk

**Assembly:** 26th (1998)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 13 From Heartland Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga26_1998 p. 63](../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p63)*

---

“Commendation for Dr. Paul Gilchrist’s Service as Stated Clerk” Whereas, Dr Paul Gilchrist has served the Presbyterian Church in America for the past ten years as Stated Clerk, and Whereas, the 26th General Assembly will be pausing to praise God for His faithfulness during the 25 years since the denomination was formed, and Whereas, much of this growth is attributable to the leadership of our Stated Clerk, and Whereas, he has announced his desire to retire from this position at the close of the 26th General Assembly, Therefore be it resolved that the 26th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America give praise to God for Dr. Gilchrist’s service as Stated Clerk, and Be it Further resolved that the General Assembly ask Dr Gilchrist and his wife, Barbara, and any family members who may be present, to move to the platform as the Assembly gives praise to God for their service to the King of the Church. Adopted at the Spring Stated Meeting of Heartland Presbytery , on March 21, 1998 Attested by: /s/ Lawrence N. Lunceford, Stated Clerk
