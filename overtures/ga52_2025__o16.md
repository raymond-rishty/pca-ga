“Amend _BCO_ 36-5 to Conform the Language to _BCO_ 37-3 re Suspension from Office”

**Whereas** the censure of indefinite suspension is “administered to an accused who, upon conviction, remains impenitent” ( _BCO_ 30-1), and is administered “until he exhibits signs of repentance, or until by his conduct, the necessity of the greatest censure be made manifest” ( _BCO_ 30-3), and

**Whereas** with respect to church officers, “suspension from Sacraments shall always be accompanied by suspension from office. But suspension from office is not always necessarily accompanied with suspension from Sacraments” ( _BCO_ 30-3), and

**Whereas** the language stipulated in _BCO_ 36-5 to be used for the administration of the censure of indefinite suspension does not address the case when an officer is indefinitely suspended from his office but not from the sacraments as clearly a sit could, and

**Whereas** the language stipulated in _BCO_ 37-3 to be used for the restoration of one who has been indefinitely suspended more clearly addresses the case when an officer is indefinitely suspended from his office but not from the Sacraments, and

**Whereas** there is wisdom in bringing the language of _BCO_ 36-5 into conformity with the language of _BCO_ 37-3,

**Therefore, be it resolved** that _BCO_ 36-5 be amended by adding “/or” to the word “and” in “(and from the exercise of your office)” (underlining for additions):
