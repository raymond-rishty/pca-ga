# GA28 O15 — Amend BCO 56-5 to Replace 'Our Holy Religion' with 'The Holy Bible'

**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 15 From New Jersey Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Referred  ·  **BCO:** 56-5

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 285](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p285)*

---

“Revise BCO 56-5 Regarding ‘Our Holy Religion.’”

Whereas, the first paragraph of *The Directory for the Worship of God*, states that “BCO 56, 57 and 58 have been given full constitutional authority by the Eleventh General Assembly after being submitted to the Presbyteries and receiving the necessary two-thirds (2/3) approval of the Presbyteries,” and

Whereas, BCO 56-5 states, regarding the questions a minister is to ask when doing an infant baptism, “The minister shall then propose the following questions…”, and

Whereas, the word “shall” requires the minister to use the specific wording of the questions as recorded in BCO 56-5, and

Whereas, in question three the term “our holy religion” is used, and

Whereas, in modern English the term “religion” carries with it the idea of a man-made belief system in that there are numerous world religions, and

Whereas, Colossians 3:23 specifically uses the term “religion” in a negative sense referring to man-made religion, and

Whereas, the BCO 56-5 use of the term “our” also adds to the connection to Colossians 3:23 about religions as being of human origin, and

Whereas, the intent of BCO 56-5 is that of encouraging the teaching of the true doctrines given to the Church by the Lord Jesus as revealed in the Bible, Therefore, be it so ordered that the BCO be revised by the following action: Delete the phrase “our holy religion” and add the phrase “the holy Bible”, in BCO 56-5, so that question three will read, in part, “…that you will teach (him) the doctrines of the holy Bible, and…” Not adopted by the Presbytery of New Jersey. [RAO 10-10] Attested by: /s/ James A. Smith, Stated Clerk NOTE: Presbytery did, however, vote to suggest to the Assembly regarding OVERTURE 15 and 16 that the Assembly erect a committee to deal with language and style in the BCO. 7. That OVERTURE 16 from New Jersey Presbytery (“Revise BCO 56-5 Regarding ‘Means of God’s Appointment’”) be answered by referring the matter back to New Jersey Presbytery to perfect the form (e.g., RAO 10-11) and resubmit if they so desire. Adopted
