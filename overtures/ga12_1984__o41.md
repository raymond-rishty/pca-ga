# GA12 O41 — Schedule Extended Assembly Debate on Confessional Subscription

**Assembly:** 12th (1984)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of St. Louis  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga12_1984 p. 59](../markdown/ga12_1984.md#ga12-p59)*

---
