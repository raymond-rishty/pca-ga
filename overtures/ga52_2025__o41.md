# GA52 O41 — Direct AC to Collect & Report Each Congregation's Public Worship Service Times

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 41 from the Calvary Presbytery (to AC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1288](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1288)*

---

“Direct the AC to Collect & Report Each Congregation’s Public Worship Service Times” **Whereas** the worship of God, and particularly the corporate public worship of God, is vital to the Christian faith ( _WLC_ 117); and, **Whereas** proper observance of the Fourth Commandment requires due diligence and preparation ( _WLC_ 121); and **Whereas** due diligence and preparation requires knowing when particular congregations will gather for public worship; and, **Whereas** the charge of keeping the Sabbath is more specially directed to superiors (WLC 118); and **Whereas** information about public worship service times are at present occasionally unavailable and often tedious for individuals to collect through publicly accessible channels; and, **Therefore, be it resolved** that the 52 [nd] General Assembly direct the Administrative Committee to collect and report each congregation’s public worship service times (inclusive of all stated worship services) on the church directory map available at https://www.pcaac.org/churchdirectory/. **Therefore, be it further resolved** that the 52 [nd] General Assembly encourage the Administrative Committee to seek the assistance of the several Presbyteries in this task. _Adopted by Calvary Presbytery at its stated meeting of April 24, 2025._ _Attested by /s/ RE Melton L. Duncan, Stated Clerk_
