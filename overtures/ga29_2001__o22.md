# GA29 O22 — Divide Great Lakes Presbytery & Form Ohio Valley Presbytery

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 22 from Great Lakes Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 169](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p169)*

---

"Divide Great Lakes Presbytery and Form Ohio Valley Presbytery" Now Therefore, Be it Resolved, that Great Lakes Presbytery unanimously overtures the 29th General Assembly to form a new “Ohio Valley” Presbytery by: Establishing the boundary of the continuing Great Lakes Presbytery to include: All the state of Michigan; All of Indiana north and east of and including the counties of Porter, Jasper, Pulaski, Fulton, Wabash, Huntington, Wells, and Adams; and All of Ohio currently in Great Lakes Presbytery north and east of and including the counties of Mercer, Shelby, Logan, Union, Madison, Pickaway, Hocking, Vinton, and Meigs. Establishing the boundary of the new Ohio Valley Presbytery to include: All of Indiana that is currently in the Great Lakes Presbytery and is south and west of but not including the counties of Porter, Jasper, Pulaski, Fulton, Wabash, Huntington, Wells, and Adams; and All of Ohio south and west of but not including the counties of Mercer, Shelby, Logan, Union, Madison, Pickaway, Hocking, Vinton and Meigs. All of the counties of Kentucky currently in Great Lakes Presbytery. This action is to be effective July 1, 2001. Adopted unanimously at the Stated Meeting of the Great Lakes Presbytery held on October 13, 2000 12. That the General Assembly approve the 2002 budget of Reformed University Ministries, and note the budget including the affiliated campus ministries is provided to the General Assembly for its information; and further, that the Assembly note with thanksgiving the opportunities and challenges represented by the entire 2002 budget.
