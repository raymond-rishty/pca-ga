# GA6 O12 — Encourage Churches to Display the Denominational Name Publicly

**Assembly:** 6th (1978)  ·  **Source:** Warrior Presbytery

*Source: [ga06_1978 p. 36](../markdown/ga06_1978.md#ga06-p36)*

---

Whereas, God in His providence called into being the Presbyterian Church in America in December of 1973, and Whereas, God has called us to preach the distinctives of Reformed Christianity, and because we are a Presbyterian denomination in which Jesus Christ is preached in His fullness; Therefore, Warrior Presbytery overtures the Sixth General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America, to encourage our churches to use the denominational name on their signs, sta tionery, newspaper. Yellow pages, etc., so that the public may know our churches form a distinc tive and different branch of the Reformed Faith. Frederick S. Carr Stated Clerk 24 January 1978 Answered in the negative, §6-111, III. 18; p. 113.
