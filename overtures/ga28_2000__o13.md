The presbytery in which he is being called shall carefully investigate the circumstances under which he was divested. If the presbytery decides that the reason he was divested was simply a lack of call, it may proceed to give him the trials for ordination outlined in 21-4 without going through the normal candidate, licensure, and internship procedures.
