# GA26 O28 — Express Support for Overtures 16 and 17 Regarding Pacific Presbytery and Worship Practices

**Assembly:** 26th (1998)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 28 From the Presbytery of Grace  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference

*Source: [ga26_1998 p. 181](../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p181)*

“Expression of support for overtures 16 and 17 Re Enlarging Pacific Presbytery and Investigating Worship Practices”

Whereas, Grace Presbytery is interested in the work of the church-at-large, and

Whereas, we are part of a connectional church which seeks to spread into the entire United States of America; and

Whereas, questionable worship practices have apparently been introduced into various mission work sunder the sponsorship of the denominational Mission to North America (MNA) Committee, including the use of alternative rock music and film clips from secular movies; and

Whereas, at least one of the reasons why our denomination was formed in 1973 out of the Southern Presbyterian Church is because of the experimental worship that was being sponsored by the Southern Presbyterian home missions board, and

Whereas, the continuation of such worship practices in our mission works threatens denominational unity and is offensive to many in the church who contribute benevolence dollars to the MNA Committee, and

Whereas, Northern California Presbytery has overtured the General Assembly to expand its bounds to include most of Nevada and all of Utah so that no other presbytery could plant churches in that area; and

Whereas, this expansion of territory could have a detrimental effect on the efforts by the church as a whole in the planting of traditional churches in that vast area, and

Whereas, one of our sister presbyteries, Central Georgia, has adopted two overtures with regard to these matters; Now
