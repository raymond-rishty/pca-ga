# GA47 O46 — Reaffirm PCA's Commitment Against Abortion

**Assembly:** 47th (2019)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 46 from Hills and Plains Presbytery (OC)

*Source: [ga47_2019 p. 711](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p711)*

---

“Reaffirm PCA’s Commitment Regarding the Sanctity of Life and Our Stance Against Abortion in Particular”

**Whereas**, the Word of God indicates that all human beings are created in the image of God (Genesis 1:26, 27) and therefore have great worth in God’s sight, and

**Whereas**, God’s Word also indicates that human life is therefore of such value that to destroy that life for any reason other than that which God has prescribed, which is murder, is of such seriousness as to merit capital punishment (Genesis 9:5, 6), and

**Whereas**, God has clearly indicated in his Word that murder is a sin against him, according to the moral law applicable to all times and circumstances, as codified in the tablets of stone known as the Ten Commandments (Exodus 20:13; Deuteronomy 5:17), and

**Whereas**, the sin of abortion continues to be a common practice in our culture despite political and religious efforts to outlaw, restrict, and prevent it, and

**Whereas**, our _Confession of Faith_ indicates that it is proper and necessary for synods, “…by way of humble petition in cases extraordinary…” ( _WCF_ 31:4) to address matters which pertain to the civil magistrate, and

**Whereas**, both the legalization of the murder of a helpless class, resulting in the loss of more than 51 million lives, and the forced subsidization of such by individuals opposed to it, constitute such an extraordinary case,

**Therefore, be it resolved** that the General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America officially, and on the basis of the inspired and authoritative Word of God, declare the following: We reaffirm our denomination’s commitment on this pertinent, prevalent issue regarding the sanctity of life and our stance against abortion in particular. We commend our denominational overtures, particularly 1979 – Overture 7 - Revise Assembly’s statement on abortion 1980 – Overture 12 - Reaffirm original statement on abortion 1984 – Overture 14 - Adopt statement that a memorial service is Biblical and appropriate in cases of miscarriage 1989 – Overture 17 - Oppose research on transplantation of fetal tissue 1993 – Overture 25 - Protection of Life of the Unborn 1993 – Overture 41 - Exhort SBC to Continue Strong Stand Against Abortion 1999 – Overture 27 - Reaffirm the sanctity of life 2010 – Overture 28 - Sanctity of Life Resolution 2014 – Overture 43 - Expression of Support for Sanctity of Life and Marriage The content of these preceding overtures, which span years, and can be difficult to find, are compiled for easy reference in the Attachment to this overture, which may be found beginning on p. 718. _Adopted by Hills and Plains Presbytery at its stated meeting, May 4, 2019_ _Attested by /s/ RE Bill Boyd, stated clerk_
