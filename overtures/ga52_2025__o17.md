That **Overture 27** from Northwest Georgia Presbytery, “Erect Ad Interim Committee to Receive Suggestions for Permanent Committee and Agency Operations, Resources, and Expenses,” be answered in the **NEGATIVE** . Grounds: Pursuant to _RAO_ 9-1 ad interim committees may be erected solely to “handle particular matters of business as designated by the Assembly.” The ad interim committee proposed in the overture only provides generalized directives, not specific deliverables or outcomes, and does not speak to “particular matters of business.” The proposed committee would be instructed to solicit and receive recommendations with no criteria for the source of those recommendations, how they will be solicited, evaluated, or reported. It is also already the case that any presbytery, session, officer of the church, or congregant who has suggestions for a Permanent Committee or Agency is already welcomed to send suggestions to said Committee or Board. Further, per _RAO_ 4-11, the budget of MNA is reviewed and approved by the Permanent Committee and the Administrative Committee, whose members have been elected by the General Assembly. That budget is then reviewed by the MNA Committee of Commissioners, along with the annual audit (per _RAO_ 14-7) before being presented for approval by the General Assembly.
