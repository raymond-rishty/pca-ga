# GA15 O12 — Direct the Judicial Business Committee to Study Permitting Paedocommunion at Session Discretion

**Assembly:** 15th (1987)  ·  **Source:** Pacific Northwest Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Referred

*Source: [ga15_1987 p. 89](../markdown/ga15_1987.md#ga15-p89)*

---
