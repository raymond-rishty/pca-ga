# GA28 O25 — Urge U.N. and U.S. Action and Prayer Support for North Korean Refugees

**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 25 From Korean Southeastern Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 309](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p309)*

---

“Support North Korean Refugees” The Korean Southeastern Presbytery overtures the 28th General Assembly to: 1. Write an official letter to the Chairman of the United Nations requesting that the U.N. provide protection for the refugees escaping from North Korea and that refugee camps be established to provide shelter for them. 2. Write an official letter to the President of The United States requesting that the United States provide protection for the refugees escaping from North Korea and that refugee camps be established to provide shelter for them. 3. Set aside a special time of prayer during this General Assembly for the refugees who are escaping from North Korea. 4. Give permission to post a sign-up sheet for all commissioners who would like to continue supporting these refugees in prayer. 5. Give permission to send a letter to each local church requesting them to support these refugees by special prayer. Adopted by the Korean Southeastern Presbytery at its 29th Stated Meeting on April 3, 2000. Attested by: /s/ Dan Dongkyo Lee, Stated Clerk
