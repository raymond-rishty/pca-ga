**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 9 From the Presbytery of the Ascension  ·  **Disposition:** Referred  ·  **BCO:** 24-1

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 280](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p280)*
