# GA8 O19 — Invite the RPCES to join the PCA

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** Pacific Presbytery

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 37](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p37)*

---

Whereas, the Reformed Presbyterian Church, Evangelical Synod is a sister of the Presbyterian Church in America, holding the same doctrinal standards and practicing the same polity, and

Whereas, there is a need today fora united presbyterian voice for the historic christian faith, a viable alternative to theologically libera] Presbyterianism on the North American continent, and

Whereas, the Pacific Presbytery is one of three PCA presbyteries most affected by our Lord’s mandate to build His church within the same geographical boundaries as those within which the RPCES is at work, sometimes to the confusion of the world and even other parts of Christ's body. Therefore, the Pacific Presbytery overtures the Eighth General Assembly to humbly extend an invitation to the Reformed Presbyterian Church, Evangelical Synod to join the Presbyterian Church in America, thereby creating one church, one united witness in the nation and to the world and one fellowship as true brethren in Christ. Furthermore, should the Eighth General Assembly act upon the above overture in the affirmative, that the Pacific Presbytery be authorized to immediately discuss with the California, Pacific Northwest and Rocky Mountain Presbyteries of the RPCES our mutual concerns and possible redrawing of boundaries should our presbyteries be joined. Adopted by Pacific Presbytery January 26, 1980. Attest: William F. Woodhall Stated Clerk Answered by letter of invitation. See § 8-43, IV, 5; p. 80ff.
