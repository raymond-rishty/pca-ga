That the Assembly express its deepest appreciation for the hardworking staff in the Office of the Stated Clerk, especially for Mr. Ross E. Cook, Business Administrator, who will be retiring before the end of the year, for Mrs. Laurel De Bert, Administrative Assistant, for Chaplain J Robert Fiol, Assistant to the Stated Clerk, and for Mr. Jerry Kornegay, Director of the PCA Historical Center in St. Louis, who has resigned his position to move to Knoxville, Tennessee Adopted TE Bill Fox, Chairman of the Administrative Committee, presented a plaque to RE Ross Cook, AC Business Administrator, on behalf of the Assembly, due to his retirement later this year. 8 That the 26th General Assembly express its gratitude to the Lord for the good spirit of cooperation among the Coordinators, Directors, and Presidents of the committees and agencies, and pray that this spirit continues to grow Adopted 9. The Committee refers the following to the General Assembly Committee of commissioners for the pc .A Foundation: (See 26-65, III, 4 and 6, p. 220 For final disposition) That GA amend the Corporate Bylaws of the PCA, Article VI, Section 5, the first two paragraphs as follows: “The Board of Directors of Presbyterian Church in America Foundation, Inc., shall consist of ten (10) members: (a) Five (5) Six (6) members in classes elected through the standard nomination and election procedures (the “at large members”); and (b) one (1) member from each of the following permanent committees or board of trustees of the agencies: (1) Christian Education and Publications, {2-) Covenant College, (3- 2) Covenant Theological Seminary, (4 3) Mission to the World, and (# 4) Mission to North America (the “Appointed Members”). the chairman of the board of directors of Presbyterian Church in America Foundation, Inc. shall bean at large member. The at-large members shall serve for four (4) year terms, and shall be divided into four (4) classes, each class being as nearly equal in number as possible, so that the terms of approximately one-fourth of the at-large members shall expire each year. Directors shall be eligible for reelection to a secondfour-year term after which there must be a one-year interval before further election. 10. That the 26th GA accept the invitation from Evangel Presbytery to host the 30th General Assembly in June 2002 in the Birmingham area subject to available suitable facilities. Adopted 11. That the 26th GA respond to Overture 8 from Heritage Presbytery “Cease Publishing Private Information in Yearbook’’ by referring it to the AC with instructions to the Stated Clerk to review with legal counsel and other knowledgeable persons the questions of privacy raised, and to report to the October 1998 meeting of AC in order to implement any appropriate recommendations both for the AC and for other committees and agencies. Adopted
