# GA11 O13 — Amend BCO 25-3 to Reduce Congregational Meeting Quorum for Very Large Churches

**Assembly:** 11th (1983)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of Southern Florida  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 25-3

*Source: [ga11_1983 p. 55](../markdown/ga11_1983.md#ga11-p55)*

---

Whereas, the Book of Church Order 25-3 sets the quorum for congregational meetings; and Whereas, a different quorum is required for churches of more than one hundred resident communing members, (1/4 of membership for churches of less than 100; 1/6 for those over 100); and Whereas, churches with more than 2,000 members experience special difficulty in reaching the quorum required because of multiple services and other problems peculiar to very large churches; Therefore, the Presbytery of Southern Florida respectfully proposes that Chapter 25-3 of the Book of Church Order be amended to allow congregations of more than two thousand resident communing members to use a quorum of one eighth of their resident communing members. Answered in the negative §11-30, III, 16, p. 81. 10-75, III, 18, p. 102 Referred to Committee of Administration
