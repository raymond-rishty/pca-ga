# GA11 O32 — Expand New Jersey Presbytery Boundaries to Include the Entire State

**Assembly:** 11th (1983)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of New Jersey  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga11_1983 p. 51](../markdown/ga11_1983.md#ga11-p51)*

---

Whereas, it was moved and carried that the Presbytery of New Jersey of the Presbyterian Church in America petition the 1983 General Assembly to expand the boundaries of the Presbytery to include the entire State of New Jersey. Attest: JAMES A. SMITH, STATED CLERK No response to overture §11-61, III, 20, p. 125.
