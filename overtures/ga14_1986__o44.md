# GA14 O44 — Transfer Catawba County from Central to Western Carolina Presbytery

**Assembly:** 14th (1986)  ·  **Source:** Western Carolina Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga14_1986 p. 62](../markdown/ga14_1986.md#ga14-p62)*

---

We request (overture) the General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to redraw the boundaries of Central and Western Carolina Presbytery so as to include Catawba County in Western Carolina Presbytery. Approved March 15, 1986. Attest: Bill Laxton, Stated Clerk Answered in the affirmative, 14-43, III, 18, p. 117.
