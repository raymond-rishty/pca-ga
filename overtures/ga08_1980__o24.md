# GA8 O24 — Devote GA time daily to prayer and Bible study

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** Louisiana Presbytery

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 33](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p33)*

---

Whereas, the task of Christ's Elders is the ministry of His Word and Prayer (Acts 6:4) and Whereas, Christ is the sovereign king who rules and guides His Church (Ephesians 4:5), Therefore, we recommend that at least two hours per day be devoted each day of General Assembly to Bible study, prayer, and preaching. Adopted at Spring Stated Meeting of Louisiana Presbytery, March 15, 1980. Attest: BakerS. Smith, Stated Clerk Louisiana Presbytery Answered in the negative. § 8-103, III, 16, p. 123.
