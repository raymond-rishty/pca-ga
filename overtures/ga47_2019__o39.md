# GA47 O39 — Re-commend Previous PCA Statements on Homosexuality

**Assembly:** 47th (2019)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 39 from Southern New England Presbytery (to OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Out of order

*Source: [ga47_2019 p. 693](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p693)*

---

“Re-commend the Previous Statements and Study Reports of the PCA in Regard to Homosexuality”

**Whereas**, the Fifth General Assembly of the PCA in 1977 affirmed “the Biblical position for our denomination which states that: (1) The act of homosexuality is a sin according to God’s Word; (2) Churches should actively seek to lead the homosexual person to confession and repentance that he might find justification and sanctification in Jesus Christ, according to 1 Corinthians 6:11; and, (3) In light of the Biblical view of its sinfulness, a practicing homosexual continuing in this sin would not be a fit candidate for ordination or membership in the Presbyterian Church in America.” **(** _Minutes of the 5th_ _General Assembly_ _of the PCA_, 1977, pp. 67-68); and

**Whereas**, the PCA General Assembly has reaffirmed its position on the issue of homosexuality on subsequent occasions, including 1996, 1999, and 2018; and

**Whereas**, the 158th General Synod of the RPCES adopted a study report on homosexuality and commended their report, entitled “Pastoral Care for the Repentant Homosexual,” to their sessions and congregations as an aid for their ministry to those struggling with homosexuality” (158th General Synod _Minutes_, 4 July 1980, pp. 43-50; _Documents of Synod_, pp. 301-8 http://www.pcahistory.org/findingaids/rpces/docsynod/301.html); and

**Whereas**, the 9th General Assembly of the PCA in 1981 adopted the Ad Interim Committee report on Joining and Receiving, which included the statement: “In receiving these denominations, the Presbyterian Church in America recognizes the history of the respective denominations as part of her total history and receives their historical documents as valuable and significant material which will be used in the perfecting of the church” [ _Minutes of the 9th_ _General Assembly of the PCA_, p. 305].

**Therefore, be it resolved**, that the Southern New England Presbytery overture the 47th General Assembly of the PCA to re-commend the previous statements and study reports of the PCA, including our study report adopted by the 158th General Synod of the RPCES on homosexuality entitled “Pastoral Care for the Repentant Homosexual,” with the deletion of Sections III and IV, to our sessions and congregations as an aid for their ministry to those struggling with homosexuality.” [158th General Synod _Minutes_, 4 July 1980, pp. 43-50; _Documents of Synod_, pp. 301-308.
