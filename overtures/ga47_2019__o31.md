*Source: [ga47_2019 p. 662](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p662)*

“Form an Ad Interim Committee to Address Matters Related to Domestic Abuse, Domestic Oppression, and Sexual Assault” (Version 6)

**Be it resolved:** 1. That the 47th General Assembly create a seven-member Ad Interim Committee (AIC) tasked to accomplish the following. a. Prepare an annotated bibliography of recommended pastoral resources available on the nature of domestic abuse, domestic oppression, [15] and sexual assault, as well as pastoral resources for the care of victims of these sins; b. Explore these matters from an historical perspective, giving special consideration of how they have been conceived of and addressed within the Reformed tradition and provide a summary of their findings. c. Describe "best practices" and policy guidelines that could be helpful for elders, Sessions, Presbyteries, and agencies for protecting against these sins and for responding to them; d. If the Committee deems it prudent, it may include in the report any revisions or additions to _BCO_ Rules of Discipline that might more appropriately protect victims of these sins within a biblical process, which changes may be issued as an overture to a subsequent General Assembly by any Presbytery that would wish to do so; e. If the Committee deems it prudent, it may include in the report any statement the Committee might recommend for the GA to consider adopting regarding any PCA repentance related to these matters, which statement may be issued as an overture to a subsequent General Assembly by any Presbytery that would wish to do so. 2.
