# GA8 O21 — Prepare a Digest of Assembly Actions interpreting the BCO

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of Central Florida  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 32](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p32)*

---

Whereas, each year the Permanent Judicial Committee receives many requests to assist presbyteries and sessions in interpreting various portions of our Book of Church Order, and Whereas, the complaints and appeals in cases of judicial process with which the General Assembly has had to deal each year give evidence of the difficulties presbyteries and sessions have in making proper application of our Book of Church Order, now. Therefore, the Presbytery of Central Florida overtures the General Assembly to instruct the Stated Clerk to prepare a Digest of Assembly Actions taken which provide guidance in the interpretation and application of our Book of Church Order, this digest to be prepared in loose-leaf form, fully indexed and annotated as appropriate to its use, to be updated annually. Further, it is requested that the Stated Clerk be granted the services of an assistant for a term of one man-month in the initial preparation of this digest if, in his judgment, such assistance is necessary. Adopted at the meeting of Presbytery, January 15, 1980. Charles U. Ambler, Jr. Stated Clerk Answered in the affirmative. § 8-103, III, 15, p. 123.
