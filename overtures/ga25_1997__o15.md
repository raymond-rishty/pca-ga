# GA25 O15 — Appoint a Study Committee on the Role of Women in the Church

**Assembly:** 25th (1997)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 15 From the South Coast Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga25_1997 p. 175](../markdown/ga25_1997.md#ga25-p175)*

---
