# GA8 O8 — Amend BCO 26-11 on congregations withdrawing from the PCA

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of the Western Carolinas  ·  **Disposition:** Referred  ·  **BCO:** 26-11

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 40](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p40)*

---

Whereas, the Book of Church Order (BOCO), paragraph 26-11, provides explicitly for the withdrawal of congregations from the PCA ‘‘at any time for reasons which seem to it sufficient,'' and

Whereas, the BOCO does not set forth procedures for a congregation to take this important step, and

Whereas, such a significant action should only betaken by a congregation after a proper study of the matter, and in an orderly way. Therefore, the Presbytery of the Western Carolinas overtures the General Assembly to direct the Permanent Committee on Judicial Business to frame a possible amendment to Paragraph 26-11 of the BOCO to provide for an orderly procedure to handle this matter. Adopted at the Winter Stated meeting of the Western Carolinas Presbytery, January 15, 1980. A. Boyce Spooner Stated Clerk Answered in the affirmative, and referred to Sub-Committee on Judicial Business. §8-88,111, 15; p. 118.
