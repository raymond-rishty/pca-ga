# GA15 O34 — Reject the Majority Report's View of Church Authority in the Ad Interim Committee Report

**Assembly:** 15th (1987)  ·  **Source:** Northeast Presbytery

*Source: [ga15_1987 p. 41](../markdown/ga15_1987.md#ga15-p41)*

---

That Northeast Presbytery overture the General Assembly to express our strong disapproval of the view of church authority set forth by the Majority Report of the Ad-Interim Committee to Study and Make Recommendations as to Structure and Procedure, and to urge the General Assembly to reject that concept. Therefore, Northeast Presbytery hereby overtures the 15th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to 1. express our strong disapproval of the view of church authority set forth by the Majority Report of the Ad-Interim Committee to Study and Make Recommendations as to Structure and Procedure, and 2. to urge the General Assembly to reject that concept. Adopted at the May 8-9, 1987 Stated Meeting. Attest: Philip J. Adams, Stated Clerk Answered by reference to action on the Ad Interim Committee on General Assembly, 15-95, 111, 7, p. 197.
