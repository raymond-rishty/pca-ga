# GA8 O13 — Revise the plans for the 1982 General Assembly

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** the Session of Westminster Presbyterian Church, Fort Walton Beach, Florida  ·  **Disposition:** Approved → sent to presbyteries; ratification not located  ·  **BCO:** 25-10

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 32](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p32)*

---

> *That Overtures 5, 13 & 31 be answered in the affirmative and that the General Assembly accept the invitation to meet in Grand Rapids in 1982. Adopted. (ga08_1980.md, line 7007) — administrative action, not a BCO constitutional amendment; no ratification process applies.*

(Note: This Overture was presented to Gulf Coast Presbytery, but was not adopted by Presbytery.) Whereas, the Reformed Presbyterian Church. North America, the denomination owning and controlling Geneva College, Beaver, Pennsylvania, has committed to meet jointly with the other members of the North American Presbyterian and Reformed Council at Calvin College in Grand Rapids, Michigan in 1982; Whereas, the Presbyterian Church in America maintains fraternal relations with the other members meeting at Calvin College, Grand Rapids, Michigan in 1982; Whereas, we believe that to deliberately meet elsewhere might well give the impression of boycotting the North American Presbyterian and Reformed Council; Therefore, the Session of Westminster Presbyterian Church, Fort Walton Beach, Florida overtures the General Assembly to request the Committee on Administration to re-consider and revise the plans for the 1982 General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America so that said General Assembly meets concurrently with the other North American Presbyterian and Reformed Council members at Calvin College in Grand Rapids, Michigan in 1982. Adopted by the Session of Westminster Presbyterian Church, Fort Walton Beach, Florida on November 19. 1979. Attested: M.C. Hull Clerk of Session C. Don Darling Moderator of Session Answered in the affirmative. § 8-103, III, 2, p. 121.
