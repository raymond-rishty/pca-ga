# GA30 O17 — Amend RAO 7-4 to Require Random Matching of Floor Nominations

**Assembly:** 30th (2002)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 17 from Eastern Canada Presbytery (to B&O)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga30_2002 p. 241](../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p241)*

---

“Eliminate Floor Nominations Against Specific Nominees; Change to Random Contests”

Whereas it has been alleged that nominations from the floor of General Assembly are being placed in a party spirit, not just to put desirable candidates before the Assembly, but to prevent particular individuals who do not pass some litmus test from being selected for committees of the General Assembly; and

Whereas God’s people should avoid even the appearance of wrongdoing (1 Thess. 5:22; Mt 17:26-27; 1 Cor 10:32,33; 2 Cor 6:3); and

Whereas random matching of floor nominees to committee nominees for elections would both make it impossible to manipulate the vote by matching a popular candidate against one who was deemed unacceptable, and also make it clear to all that this was not happening; Therefore The Presbytery of Eastern Canada overtures the 30th General Assembly to amend RAO 7-4.i by adding “Nominations from the floor shall be matched by the Nominating Committee by lot, first to any slots for which the Committee presented no nominee, and then to one of the slots for which there is a single nominee (including those filled by nominations from the floor), then to slots for which there are more than one nominee.” 30-51 Theological Examining Committee By unanimous consent, the Assembly determined to take up this report before proceeding to the Special Order report of the Nominating Committee. Minutes of each meeting are filed with the Stated Clerk’s office. Examinations: 1. Dr. Nielson had no exceptions to the Westminster Confession and Catechisms. 2.
