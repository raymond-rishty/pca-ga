# GA8 O6 — Invite RPCES, OPC, and RPCNA to unite with the PCA

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** Warrior Presbytery

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 37](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p37)*

---

Whereas, the Presbyterian Church in America has had a committee meeting with representatives of the Reformed Presbyterian Church, Evangelical Synod, the Orthodox Presbyterian Church and the Reformed Presbyterian Church of North America, to talk about “ areas of agreement, difference and difficulties" in relation to organic union; and

Whereas, contacts with these other denominations have been warm, friendly and suggestive of a mutual interest for even closer relationships; Now Therefore Be It Resolved, that Warrior Presbytery does overture the General Assembly to extend to the Reformed Presbyterian Church, Evangelical Synod, the Orthodox Presbyterian Church and the Reformed Presbyterian Church of North America, a most cordial and fraternal invitation to unite with this Church in the spirit of the invitation of Numbers 10:29: "W e are journeying unto the place of which the Lord said, ‘I will give it you': Come thou with us and we will do thee good: for the Lord hath spoken good concerning Israel!" Attested: te fredrick S. Carr Stated Clerk 15 January 1980 Answered by letter of invitation. See § 8-43, IV, 5; p. 80ff.
