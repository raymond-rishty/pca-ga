# GA42 O35 — Affirm Overture 6 Regarding Child Protection in the PCA

**Assembly:** 42nd (2014)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 35 from Chicago Metro Presbytery (to OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference

*Source: [ga42_2014 p. 839](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p839)*

“Affirm Overture 6 Regarding Child Protection in the PCA”

**Whereas** Georgia Foothills Presbytery has sent an overture to the 42 [nd] General Assembly calling on the General Assembly to speak to the issue of child sexual abuse; and

**Whereas** Chicago Metro Presbytery believes that child sexual abuse is an issue to which the General Assembly should speak; and

**Whereas** Chicago Metro Presbytery endorses the substance of the overture from Georgia Foothills Presbytery on child sexual abuse;

**Therefore, be it resolved,** that Chicago Metro Presbytery urges the 42 [nd] General Assembly to answer in the affirmative the overture from Georgia Foothills Presbytery on child sexual abuse. _Approved by Chicago Metro Presbytery at its stated meeting, April 9, 2014_ _Attested by /s/ TE Nate Conrad, stated clerk_
