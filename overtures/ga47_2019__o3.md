# GA47 O3 — Amend RAO 10-3 on Godly Prudence in GA Corporate Worship

**Assembly:** 47th (2019)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 3 from Northern New England Presbytery (to AC, CCB)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga47_2019 p. 589](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p589)*

---

Externalism and hypocrisy stand condemned.
