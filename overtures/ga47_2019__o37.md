# GA47 O37 — Adopt Assembly Statement on Same-Sex Attraction

**Assembly:** 47th (2019)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 37 from James River Presbytery (to OC)

*Source: [ga47_2019 p. 689](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p689)*

---

“Assembly Statement on Same-sex Attraction” **Whereas**, there is tremendous cultural pressure in the United States in these days concerning homosexuality and sexual sin; and **Whereas**, there are many people inside and outside the church who struggle with same-sex attraction and gender confusion; and **Whereas**, the Revoice organization held a conference in 2018 which discussed how to improve the way the church ministers to people who struggle with same-sex attraction, encouraging them to uphold the Biblical sexual ethic; and **Whereas**, we acknowledge and affirm that Revoice showed love to people struggling with these sins; and **Whereas**, we acknowledge and affirm that the Church has not always shown love to people who struggle with these sins; and **Whereas**, the Presbyterian Church in America, as part of its duty under the Great Commission, desires to better minister to people who struggle with same-sex attraction; **Whereas**, the Church commends the faithfulness of Christians struggling with same-sex attraction who are seeking to remain faithful to a Biblical sexual ethic; **Therefore**, the 47th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America affirms that: 1. That all persons are born sinners justly deserving God’s wrath and condemnation and inherit both the guilt of our first parents as well as corruption. (Rom 5:12) 2. That outside of Christ, men and women are enslaved to corruption which enslaves the thinking of all persons and distorts God’s created order. (Eph 2:1-8) 3.
