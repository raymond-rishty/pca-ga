# GA1 O6 — Appoint Committee to Add Scripture Proof Texts to the BCO

**Assembly:** 1st (1973)  ·  **Source:** Covenant Presbytery

*Source: [ga01_1973 p. 21](../markdown/ga01_1973.md#ga01-p21)*

---

That a committee of scholars be appointed by the First General Assembly to study and to provide Scrip tural proof texts for each part of the denomination's Book of Church Order similar to the practice in the early years of the Presbyterian Church, U. S.
