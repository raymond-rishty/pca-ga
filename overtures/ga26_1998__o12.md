# GA26 O12 — Amend BCO 24-5 to Remove Obedience Language in Deacon Congregational Vows

**Assembly:** 26th (1998)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 12 From the Session of Bedford Presbyterian Church  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 24-5

*Source: [ga26_1998 p. 204](../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p204)*

“Amend BCO 24-5 re Congregational Vows respecting Deacons”

Whereas, BCO 7-2 rightly declares “The office of deacon is not one of rule, but rather of service both to the physical and spiritual needs of the people,” and

Whereas, this means that it is inappropriate to call the congregation to give obedience to the deacons, Therefore the Session of Bedford Presbyterian Church overtures the 26th General Assembly to add in the question to the congregation in BCO 24-5, after “obedience”, (for deacons substitute ‘and encouragement’ for ‘encouragement and obedience’)”.
