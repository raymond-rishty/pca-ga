# GA28 O17 — Amend BCO 57-5 to Add Elder Vow When Receiving New Members

**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** overture 17 From Philadelphia Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 57-5

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 286](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p286)*

---

“Add Vow for Elders when Receiving Members”

Whereas, elders are called upon to shepherd the flocks that the Chief Shepherd has placed under their care (1 Peter 5:1-5), and

Whereas, new church members come under the shepherding care and authority of the elders of the church that they join, and

Whereas, new church members are called upon to assent their submission to “the government and discipline of the church” represented by the elder- shepherds of the church (BCO 57-5, Question 5), and

Whereas, church membership represents not merely the commitment of the member to the authority of the church, but the commitment of the church to the care of new members; Therefore, we propose that the elders of the church express their commitment to shepherd their new members by adding this Question Six under BCO 57-5: 6. To the elders of the church (standing): Do you, as elders and shepherds of this congregation, commit yourselves to provide spiritual care, nurture, and discipline to these who have submitted themselves to your God-given authority, recognizing that they are now among the sheep for whom you will give an account to the Chief Shepherd? Adopted November 13, 1999, by the presbytery of philadelphia for presentation to the 28th General Assembly, 2000, in Tampa, Florida Attested by: /s/ Frank D Moser, Stated Clerk
