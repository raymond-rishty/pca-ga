# GA29 O37 — Divide Potomac Presbytery and Form New Chesapeake Presbytery

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 37 From Potomac Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 177](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p177)*

---

“Divide Potomac Presbytery and Form Chesapeake Presbytery.” Now Therefore, Be it Resolved, that Potomac Presbytery overtures the 29th General Assembly to form – effective January 1, 2002 – a new “Chesapeake” Presbytery by: 1. establishing the boundary of the continuing potomac presbytery to include: • the city of w ashington, D.C. • the maryland counties of Calvert, Charles, Frederick, Montgomery, Prince George’s, Saint Mary’s, and Washington. • the west virginia counties of Berkeley, Jefferson, and Morgan. • the virginia counties of Alexandria, Arlington, Clarke, Fairfax, Fauquier, Frederick, Prince William, and Loudoun, including the cities within their borders. 2. establishing the boundary of the new chesapeake presbytery to include: • the city of baltimore and the maryland counties of Baltimore, Carroll, Harford, Howard, Anne Arundel; and Kent Island in Queen A nne’s County. approved at the called meeting of potomac presbytery held on march 31, 2001. adopted at the stated meeting of potomac presbytery held on may 15, 2001. Attested by: Richard R. Larson, Stated Clerk
