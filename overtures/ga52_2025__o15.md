> *SENT DOWN TO PRESBYTERIES: Proposed amendments to BCO: ... 36-4; 36-5; 36-8 and 42-2; 41-3; 57-2 ... THE NEXT MEETING OF THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY: June 22-26, 2026, Louisville, Kentucky*

“Amend _BCO_ 36-4 to Add Language for the Administration of Definite Suspension from Office” **Whereas** the censure of definite suspension from office is “administered to an accused who, upon conviction, satisfies the court as to his repentance and makes such restitution as is appropriate” ( _BCO_ 30-1), and **Whereas** the censure of definite suspension from office is “administered when the credit of religion, the honor of Christ, and the good of the delinquent demand it, even though the delinquent has given satisfaction to the court” ( _BCO_ 30-3), and **Whereas** _BCO_ 36-4 gives no specific language by which the censure of definite suspension from office is to be administered and the offending brother is to be addressed, necessitating Courts to determine for themselves how to communicate the censure, and **Whereas** _BCO_ 37-1 currently contains language to declare to a brother when he is “restored by the court at the end of the term of his suspension,” **Therefore, be it resolved** that _BCO_ 36-4 be amended by adding the following language (underlining for additions): **36-4.** Definite suspension from office should be administered in the presence of the court alone or in open session of the court, as it may deem best, and public announcement thereof shall be at the court’s discretion.
