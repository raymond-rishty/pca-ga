"Concur with Mid-America on Presbytery Boundaries" M&amp;P that the North Texas Presbytery inform the 27th General Assembly that it concurs with the overture of Mid-America Presbytery to include the Oklahoma Counties of Love, Carter, Murray, Garvin, McClain, Cleveland and Oklahoma in the NTP."
