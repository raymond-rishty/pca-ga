# GA10 O3 — Amend BCO 11-3 to Allow Presbyteries to Elect a Moderator for One Year

**Assembly:** 10th (1982)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of Pacific  ·  **Disposition:** Approved & ratified (1983)  ·  **Ratified**  ·  **BCO:** 11-3

*Source: [ga10_1982 p. 45](../markdown/ga10_1982.md#ga10-p45)*

---

> *Item 1: Amend 10-3 by replacing the present second sentence with the following: 'The Moderator of the Presbytery may be elected at each stated meeting of the court, or for a period of time up to one year. The Moderator of the General Assembly shall be chosen at each stated meeting.' Presbytery votes were as follows: Affirmative 30 – Negative 2 – Not reporting 4*

Be It Resolved that the Book of Church Order Chapter 11-3 be amended so that the second sentence shall read: The Moderator of the Presbytery and of the General Assembly shall be chosen at each stated meeting of these courts, except that presbyteries may, if they desire, elect a Moderator to serve for a term of one year. Taken from the September 25, 1980, Minutes of Pacific Presbytery Meeting. WILLIAM F. WOODHALL, STATED CLERK (Received too late for consideration by the Ninth General Assembly.) Answered in the affirmative as amended §10-75, III, 13, p. 102.
