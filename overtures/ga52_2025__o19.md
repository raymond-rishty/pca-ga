# GA52 O19 — Amend BCO 57-5 to Revise the Membership Vows

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 19 from the Eastern Carolina Presbytery (to CCB, OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 57-5

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1205](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1205)*

---

“Amend _BCO_ 57-5 to Revise the Membership Vows”

**Whereas** our current five membership vows have been unchanged for many years without review;

**Whereas** our current five membership vows have some antiquated language that must be explained;

**Whereas** our current five membership vows are inherited from other Reformed denominations, and we are an “ _ecclesia reformata, semper_ _reformanda_ ” denomination;

**Whereas** our current five membership vows mention nothing of the love of God for sinners;

**Whereas** our current five membership vows give no indication of what human beings are saved unto, just what we are saved from;

**Therefore, be it resolved** that the General Assembly amend _BCO_ 57-5 to revise our membership vows to include 2 addenda and one language update (underlining for additions; strikethrough for deletions): Add an entire new first vow for membership and renumber the existing vows. **1.** **Do you agree that you are made in the image of God,** **designed by God to glorify God and enjoy Him forever?** Insert the words “both beloved and” into the former vow #1. **12. Do you acknowledge yourselves to be both beloved and** **sinners in the sight of God, justly deserving His** **displeasure, and without hope save in His sovereign** **mercy?** Change the word “study” to “promote” in the final vow. **56. Do you submit yourselves to the government and** **discipline of the Church, and promise to study promote** **its purity and peace?** So that the amended vows would read as follows: **1.** **Do you agree that you are made in the image of God,** **designed by God to glorify God and enjoy Him forever?** **2.** **Do you acknowledge yourselves to be both beloved and** **sinners in the sight of God, justly deserving His** **displeasure, and without hope save in His sovereign** **mercy?** **3.** **Do you believe in the Lord Jesus Christ as the Son of** **God, and Savior of sinners, and do you receive and rest** **upon Him alone for salvation as He is offered in the** **Gospel?** **4.** **Do you now resolve and promise, in humble reliance** **upon the grace of the Holy Spirit, that you will endeavor** **to live as becomes the followers of Christ?** **5.** **Do you promise to support the Church in its worship** **and work to the best of your ability?** **6.** **Do you submit yourselves to the government and** **discipline of the Church, and promise to promote its** **purity and peace?** _Adopted by the Eastern Carolina Presbytery at its stated meeting, January_ _25, 2025_ _Attested by TE Wes Martin, stated clerk_
