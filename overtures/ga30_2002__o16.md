# GA30 O16 — Add Montana to the Geographical Bounds of Rocky Mountain Presbytery

**Assembly:** 30th (2002)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 16 from Rocky Mountain Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Approved → sent to presbyteries; ratification not located  ·  **BCO:** 24-1

*Source: [ga30_2002 p. 186](../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p186)*

---

> *The 30th GA answered the overture to amend BCO 24-1 to replace 'persons' with 'men' in the negative (ga30_2002 recommendation 11: 'BCO 24-1 to Specify "men" Rather Than "persons") be answered in the negative'). The amendment was never sent to the presbyteries, so no ratification record exists in ga31_2003 or ga32_2004.*

“Redefine the Geographical Boundaries of Rocky Mountain Presbytery to Include the State of Montana”

Whereas, Rocky Mountain Presbytery is already overseeing churches and missions in the great state of Montana; and

Whereas, we are establishing additional churches in the state of Montana; and

Whereas, the state of Montana is not in any other PCA Presbytery; Therefore, Rocky Mountain Presbytery respectfully overtures the General Assembly to add the state of Montana to the geographical bounds of Rocky Mountain Presbytery. 16. That Overture 23 from the New York State Presbytery to redraw New York State Presbytery Boundary be answered in the affirmative. Adopted [The substance of Communication 5 (text below) was dealt with, though not explicitly, by Assembly action on Overture 23.]
