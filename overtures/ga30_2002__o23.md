# GA30 O23 — Redraw Western Boundary Between New York State and Ascension Presbyteries

**Assembly:** 30th (2002)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 23 from New York State Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga30_2002 p. 187](../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p187)*

---

“Redraw New York State Presbytery Boundary”

Whereas, the current western boundary of the New York State Presbytery includes central New York east and including Monroe, Ontario, Livingston and Steuben counties; and,

Whereas, the current eastern boundary of the Ascension Presbytery includes western New York west and including Orleans, Genesee, Wyoming, and Allegany counties; and,

Whereas, the Presbyterian Church of Wellsville and its teaching elder desires to leave the Ascension Presbytery and affiliate with New York State Presbytery; and

Whereas, the Presbyterian Church of Wellsville is located in Allegany county which is currently within the boundary of the Ascension Presbytery; and,

Whereas, the New York State Presbytery desires to receive the Presbyterian Church of Wellsville and its teaching elder; and

Whereas, the New York State Presbytery desires to initiate and oversee the planting of healthy and reproducing PCA churches in every major city in upstate New York including Buffalo; and Therefore, Be It Resolved that the New York State Presbytery hereby overtures the 30th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to redraw the western boundary of New York State Presbytery and the northeastern New York boundary of Ascension Presbytery, to wit, that the counties currently belonging to the Ascension Presbytery which are in New York State be transferred to New York State Presbytery, thus having all the churches and counties within the geographical bounds of New York State, excepting those churches and counties belonging to Metropolitan New York Presbytery, within
