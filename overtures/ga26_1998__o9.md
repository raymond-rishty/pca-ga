# GA26 O9 — Commend and Thank Dr. Paul Gilchrist for Service as Stated Clerk

**Assembly:** 26th (1998)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 9 From Potomac Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga26_1998 p. 63](../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p63)*

---

“Commendation and Thanks for Dr Paul Gilchrist’s Exemplary Service as Stated Clerk” Whereas, Teaching Elder, Dr Paul Rowland Gilchrist has served the Presbyterian Church in America as its Stated Clerk since June 1988, and Whereas, the Presbyterian Church in America has benefited greatly from his service in this Office and, Whereas, since his ordination as a teaching elder in October 1956, he has selflessly served the Church of Jesus Christ with distinction, dedication, and devotion to the glory of God, often well beyond the extent to which he has been called, and Whereas, Dr. Gilchrist is retiring from the Office of the Stated Clerk in June 1998, Be It Resolved that the General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America in Saint Louis, Missouri, June 30, 1998 commends and extends its heartfelt thanks to Dr Paul Rowland Gilchrist for his exemplary work and witness in the Kingdom of the Lord Jesus Christ. Adopted at the 33rd Stated Meeting of Potomac Presbytery on February 14, 1998. Attested by: Richard R. Larson, Stated Clerk
