**Assembly:** 38th (2010)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 6 from Evangel Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga38_2010 p. 334](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p334)*

---
