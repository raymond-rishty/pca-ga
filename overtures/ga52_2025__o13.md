# GA52 O13 — Amend BCO 25-1 to Specify Only Members in Good Standing May Vote

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 13 from the Great Lakes Presbytery (to CCB, OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 25-1

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1197](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1197)*

---

“Amend _BCO_ 25-1 to Specify Only Members in Good Standing May Vote”

**Whereas** _BCO_ 24-3 states that “All communing members in good and regular standing, but no others, are entitled to vote.”

**Whereas** _BCO_ 25-1 states that all communing members are entitled to vote and does not qualify that only members in good and regular standing may vote, it is inconsistent with the _BCO_ .

**Be it resolved** that _BCO_ 25-1 be amended to specify which communing members are entitled to vote (underlining for additions; strikethrough for deletions): **25-1.** The congregation consists of all the communing members of a particular church, and they only members in good and regular standing are entitled to vote. So that the amended paragraph would read: **25-1.** The congregation consists of all the communing members of a particular church, and only members in good and regular standing are entitled to vote. _Adopted by the Great Lakes Presbytery at its stated meeting, January 11, 2025._ _Attested by Ryan Potter, stated clerk_
