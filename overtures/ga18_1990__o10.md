# GA18 O10 — Amend the Eastern Canada Presbytery Boundary Description

**Assembly:** 18th (1990)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of Eastern Canada requests that its boundaries, which now include all of Ontario, be limited to an area south of an imaginary line between the town of Tobermory, at the tip of a peninsula stretching north into Lake Huron, and the town of Gananoque which is just north east of the northeastern tip of Lake Ontario. The line is superimposed as an arrow on the map above.

*Source: [ga18_1990 p. 415](../markdown/ga18_1990.md#ga18-p415)*

This is a very indefinite boundary and one that can hardly be fixed with any accuracy.
