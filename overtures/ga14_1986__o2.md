# GA14 O2 — Amend BCO to Require Final Ordination Exams on the Full Presbytery Floor

**Assembly:** 14th (1986)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of Western Carolina  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference

*Source: [ga14_1986 p. 50](../markdown/ga14_1986.md#ga14-p50)*

---

That we accept the ruling of the General Assembly Judicial Business Committee concerning matters properly executed by a commission, but that we believe it is a dangerous precedent to conduct the final examination for candidacy, licensure or ordination other than on the floor of presbytery, and Therefore we overture the General Assembly to amend the Book of Church Order to make it clear that the final examination for candidacy, licensure and ordination must be conducted by the presbytery as a whole and not be delegated to a commission. Adopted at the Stated Meeting of Western Carolina Presbytery on April 20, 1985. Attest: W. Donald Munson, Jr. Stated Clerk Answered by response to Overture 4. See 14-52, 23, p. 126.
