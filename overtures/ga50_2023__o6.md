# GA50 O6 — Amend BCO 13-6, 21-4.b & 24-1 to Require Criminal Background Checks

**Assembly:** 50th (2023)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 6 from South Texas Presbytery (to CCB, OC)  ·  **BCO:** 13-6

*Source: [ga50_2023 p. 983](../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p983)*

---
