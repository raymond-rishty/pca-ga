# GA13 O41 — Expand Louisiana Presbytery Bounds to All Louisiana Parishes

**Assembly:** 13th (1985)  ·  **Source:** Louisiana Presbytery

*Source: [ga13_1985 p. 54](../markdown/ga13_1985.md#ga13-p54)*

---

The Louisiana Presbytery respectfully overtures the Thirteenth General Assembly to include all the Parishes (Counties) in the State of Louisiana in the boundary of Louisiana Presbytery. Adopted at the Twentieth Stated Meeting of Louisiana Presbytery, October 13, 1984. Attest: Baker S. Smith, Stated Clerk See 13-48, III, 33, p. 116.
