**Assembly:** 46th (2018)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 4 from Calvary Presbytery (to CCB, OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga46_2018 p. 676](../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p676)*

“Amend _RAO_ 9-4 to Require Three REs on Each Ad Interim Committee

**Whereas**, the Constitution of the Presbyterian Church in America (“PCA”) provides that “the ordinary and perpetual classes of office in the Church are elders and deacons,” and further provides, “Within the class of elder are the two orders of teaching elders and ruling elders,” and further provides, “The elders jointly have the government and spiritual oversight of the Church, including teaching.” ( _BCO_ 7-2); and

**Whereas**, the Constitution of the PCA implements this principle of “parity” between ruling elders and teaching elders with respect to the government and spiritual oversight of the church by providing for minimum levels of ruling elder participation in all courts of the church ( _BCO_ 12-1, 13-4, 145); and

**Whereas**, the Constitution of the PCA further affirms that the principle of ruling and teaching elder parity applies with special force in the General Assembly by providing that “The [Assembly’s] committees are to be established on the basis of an equal number between teaching and ruling elders.” ( _BCO_ 14-1); and
