# GA38 O26 — Move Wilkes County from Western Carolina to Piedmont Triad Presbytery

**Assembly:** 38th (2010)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 26 from Piedmont Triad Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference

*Source: [ga38_2010 p. 317](../markdown/ga38_2010.md#ga38-p317)*

---
