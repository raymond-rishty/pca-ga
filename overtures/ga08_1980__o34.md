# GA8 O34 — Erect a presbytery in eastern PA and southern NJ

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** Mid-Atlantic Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 44](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p44)*

---

Mid-Atlantic Presbytery, meeting in its 22nd Stated Meeting held May 9-10. 1980 in Baltimore. Maryland, in response to the requests of a majority of the churches involved, respectfully overtures the Eighth General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to erect a Presbytery in Eastern Pennsylvania and Southern New Jersey, the boundaries to include the counties of Philadelphia. Delaware. Chester. Montgom ery. Bucks. Lehigh. Berks. Lancaster. York, Dauphin. Lebanon, Schuylkill, Perry, Cumberland. Franklin. Adams and North Hampton in Pennsylvania; the counties of Mercer, Ocean. Burlington, Camden, Atlantic, Gloucester, Salem, Cumberland and Cape May in New Jersey; and the county of Newcastle in Delaware. We respectfully request that the new Presbytery be directed to convene and be constituted on September 19, 1980, at 7:30 p mint he fairfield presbyterian Church, Fairton, New Jersey, and that the membership of the Presbytery be composed of all of the Presbyterian Church in America organized churches within the bounds set forth above, plus the West Springfield Covenant Community Church. West Springfield. Massachusetts; the Pastors of these churches, and any other Presbyterian Church in America Teaching Elders who have indicated their desire in writing to their respective Presbyteries to be a member of the New Presbytery and who do not reside within the bounds of any other Presbytery of the Presbyterian Church in America.
