# GA13 O5 — Amend BCO 19-5 by Replacing "Intern" with "Licentiate"

**Assembly:** 13th (1985)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of the Ascension  ·  **BCO:** 19-5

*Source: [ga13_1985 p. 46](../markdown/ga13_1985.md#ga13-p46)*

---

Whereas, BCO 19-5 seems to deal with "interns" and the paragraphs on internship begin in 19-7, thus leading to confusion; and

Whereas, the transfer of interns is discussed in full in 19-11; and

Whereas, 19-5 seems to be meaning to discuss licentiates, since the moving of licensure is discussed nowhere else in the BCO;

Therefore, be it resolved that BCO 19-5 be changed by replacing the word “intern” with the word “licentiate”. Adopted at the Stated Meeting of September 9-10, 1983. Attest: Frank D. Moser, Stated Clerk Answered in the negative, 13-45, III, 27, p. 105.
