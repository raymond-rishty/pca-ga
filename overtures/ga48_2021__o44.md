# GA48 O44 — Transfer Berks County, Pennsylvania to Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery

**Assembly:** 48th (2021)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 44 from Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference

*Source: [ga48_2021 p. 1106](../markdown/ga48_2021.md#ga48-p1106)*

---

“Transfer Berks County, Pennsylvania, to Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery”

**Whereas**, the Susquehanna Valley Presbytery has requested the General Assembly to remove Berks County, Pennsylvania from the territory of Susquehanna Valley Presbytery; and

**Whereas**, Berks County is adjacent to the present territory of Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery, and Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery is willing to accept a transfer of Berks County to the territory of Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery;

**Now therefore**, Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery requests the PCA General Assembly to transfer Berks County to the territory of Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery, effective July 2, 2021. _Adopted by Philadelphia Metro West Presbytery at its stated meeting, May 8,_ _2021_ _Attested by /s/ RE Paul A. Rich, stated clerk_
