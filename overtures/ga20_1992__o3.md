# GA20 O3 — Place Committees of Commissioners on Two-Year Terms

**Assembly:** 20th (1992)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 3 From Missouri Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga20_1992 p. 101](../markdown/ga20_1992.md#ga20-p101)*

---

"Have the Committee of Commissioners Serve a 2-Year Term"

Whereas, the founding fathers of the Presbyterian Church in America saw the wisdom of creating Committees of Commissioners to oversee the work of the Permanent Committees, and

Whereas, this task has become more necessary and time consuming as we have grown larger, and

Whereas, the Nominating Committee operates very well on a 2-year term in Classes, Therefore Be It Resolved that the General Assembly take the necessary steps to place all the Committees of Commissioners on a 2-year term in Classes. Adopted for the Session of the New Port Presbyterian Church by Missouri Presbytery and sent without comment on the 17th day of April 1991. Attested by: /s/ Albert F. Moginot, Jr. Stated Clerk
