“Amend _BCO_ 23-1 on Procedures for Dissolving Various Pastoral Relations” Central Indiana Presbytery hereby overtures General Assembly to amend _BCO_ 23-1 as follows in order to clarify the procedures for the dissolution of the various pastoral relations: 23-1. When any minister shall tender the resignation of his pastoral charge to his Presbytery, the Presbytery shall cite the church to appear by its commissioners, to show cause why the Presbytery should or should not accept the resignation. If the church fails to appear, or if its reasons for retaining its pastor be deemed insufficient, his resignation shall be accepted and the pastoral relation dissolved.
