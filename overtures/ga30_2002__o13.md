# GA30 O13 — Revise Boundaries of Proposed Blue Ridge Presbytery

**Assembly:** 30th (2002)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 13 from Session of Faith Reformed Presbyterian  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga30_2002 p. 185](../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p185)*

---

Church (to MNA) “Revise Boundaries of Proposed Blue Ridge Presbytery” Overture To New River Presbytery and the 30th General Assembly of the PCA from Faith Reformed Presbyterian Church, Fairmont, WV

Whereas the New River Presbytery is in the process of a major change in that the churches located in Virginia have proposed the formation of a separate Presbytery as the Presbytery of Blue Ridge - and

Whereas Allegheny and Garrett counties in Maryland are within the bounds of the Pittsburgh Presbytery - and

Whereas the present churches in the northern part of West Virginia are much closer to the churches in the Pittsburgh Presbytery [than] they are to all the other churches in the New River Presbytery - and

Whereas the closer proximity to these churches in the Pittsburgh Presbytery would give greater opportunity for fellowship and shared activities such as missions conferences, church planting efforts and participation in Presbytery meetings and Presbytery Committee responsibilities - and

Whereas the travel time to attend Pittsburgh Presbytery meetings would be at least an hour less than required to attend meetings of the New River Presbytery in the Charleston area Therefore Be It Resolved that the northern most counties of West Virginia, namely, Preston, Marion, Monongalia, Wetzel (and those in the northern pan handle), Marshall, Ohio, Brook and Hancock be included within the boundary of the Pittsburgh Presbytery - and Be It Further Resolved that the present churches within these counties: Christ Community, Faith Reformed and Trinity be transferred to the Pittsburgh Presbytery and that any future churches in these counties that may be developed shall be under the jurisdiction of the Pittsburgh Presbytery - and Therefore by action of the Session of Faith Reformed Presbyterian Church, Fairmont WV, meeting at their Stated Meeting, December 19, 2001 hereby overtures the New River Presbytery, and the 30th General Assembly of the PCA that effective September 1, 2002 that the above listed counties and churches be made part of the Pittsburgh Presbytery of the PCA. 15. That Overture 16 from the Rocky Mountain Presbytery to redefine the Geographical Boundaries of Rocky Mountain Presbytery to include the State of Montana be answered in the affirmative. Adopted
