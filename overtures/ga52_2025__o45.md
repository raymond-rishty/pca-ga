# GA52 O45 — Change the Boundaries of Covenant & Hills & Plains Presbyteries

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 45 from the Hills and Plains Presbytery (to MNA)

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1297](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1297)*

---

“Change the Boundaries of Covenant and Hills & Plains Presbyteries” _**OVERTURE 21 WITHDRAWN BY COVENANT PRESBYTERY**_

**Whereas**, the general guidelines for dividing Presbyteries approved by the 41 [st] General Assembly of the PCA state “Presbytery boundaries should be such that her member churches have a common commitment to the region within their boundaries and a deep commitment to their shared responsibility to cover the region with the Gospel through evangelism and church planting.”

**Whereas**, the same general guidelines for dividing presbyteries further state “A Presbytery should have regional cohesiveness. The Presbytery should have a geographical spread that is as reasonable as possible to facilitate planting churches, encouraging fellowship, participating together in shared ministry and conducting the necessary business of the Presbytery.”

**Whereas**, the 16 [th] General Assembly of the PCA adopted guidelines for Presbytery boundaries stating, “A presbytery should have a radius of 2 1/2 hours maximum driving distance.”

**Whereas**, Sebastian County, Arkansas, where Covenant Presbyterian Church of Fort Smith is located on the farthest western border of Covenant Presbytery and is adjacent to the border of Hills & Plains Presbytery, and

**Whereas**, the Session of Covenant Presbyterian Church of Fort Smith wants to regularly attend Presbytery meetings but finds it difficult at times due to extended travel, and

**Whereas**, the locations of the stated meetings of Hills & Plains Presbytery are much closer to Fort Smith than the stated meetings of Covenant Presbytery, and

**Whereas**, the Session of Covenant Presbyterian Church of Fort Smith, Arkansas has petitioned Covenant Presbytery and Hills & Plains Presbytery to so act,

**Now therefore, be it resolved** that Hills and Plains Presbytery, in concurrence with the Session of Covenant Presbyterian Church of Fort Smith and Covenant Presbytery, overtures the 2025 General Assembly to transfer Sebastian, Scott, Crawford, Franklin, Johnson, Logan, and Polk counties from the geographic bounds of Covenant Presbytery to the geographic bounds of Hills and Plains Presbytery, and that Covenant Presbyterian Church of Fort Smith and her Teaching Elder(s) be received into the membership of Hills and Plains Presbytery. _Adopted by the Hills and Plains Presbytery at its stated meeting, May 6,_ _2025_ _Attested by /s/ TE Aaron Raines, stated clerk_
