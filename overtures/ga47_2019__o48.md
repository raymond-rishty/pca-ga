# GA47 O48 — Make Stronger Statement Regarding Abortion

**Assembly:** 47th (2019)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 48 from the Session of Christ the King (to OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga47_2019 p. 714](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p714)*

---

Presbyterian Church, Norman, Oklahoma “Make a Stronger Statement regarding Abortion” (Rejected by Hills and Plains Presbytery at its stated meeting May 4, 2019 [ _RAO_ 11-10])

**Whereas**, the Word of God indicates that all human beings are created in the image of God (Genesis 1:26, 27) and consequently have great worth in God’s sight, and

**Whereas**, God’s Word also indicates that human life is therefore of such value that to destroy that life for any reason other than that which God has prescribed, which is murder, is of such seriousness as to merit capital punishment (Genesis 9:5, 6), and

**Whereas**, God has clearly indicated in His Word that murder is a sin against Him, according to the moral law applicable to all times and circumstances, as codified in the tablets of stone known as the Ten Commandments (Exodus 20:13; Deuteronomy 5:17), and

**Whereas**, the Supreme Court decision known as Roe v. Wade, which struck down laws prohibiting the practice of abortion of unborn children and legalized abortion in every state in the United States of America, was predicated upon a fraudulent case involving false testimony with respect to the circumstances surrounding it, which circumstances thus wielded powerfully emotional and insidiously persuasive, yet mythical, import, and

**Whereas**, the fraudulent nature of the entire case was irrefutably verified by testimony subsequent to the ruling by the plaintiff in the case, Norma McCorvey, the so-called Jane Roe, such that crucial alleged facts testified to in court regarding her need for an abortion and injuries suffered by her due to her inability to obtain one were untrue (as documented in the books _I_ _Am Roe: My Life, Roe V. Wade, and Freedom_ _of_ Choice and _Won by Love_ and the documentary film “I Was Wrong”), and

**Whereas**, Ms. McCorvey spent much of the rest of her life seeking to correct the record and advocate the pro-life cause, and

**Whereas**, in the course of such advocacy, Ms. McCorvey testified to the manipulation and use of her by ideological radicals intent on promoting the legalization of abortion by any means necessary, and

**Whereas**, her obviously relevant and groundbreaking testimony has been largely ignored by both the mainstream media and our culture at large, such that Roe v. Wade, as legal opinion, is unjustifiably and ignorantly revered by many, and

**Whereas**, the practice of abortion and its promotion have long been linked to the secular humanistic ideology undergirding the practice of eugenics, which came to expression in the death camps of Nazi Germany, and which was advocated in the writings of the founder (Margaret Sanger, who publicly advocated the forced sterilization, abortion, and elimination of whole races and classes of people) of the most prolific dispenser of abortion today, Planned Parenthood, and

**Whereas**, recent video documentary evidence (made public by the Center for Medical Progress in 2015, and validated by the investigation and ruling of the United States Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals in January, 2019) has demonstrated that Planned Parenthood has sold body parts obtained through abortion to those wishing to harvest human organs for a variety of purposes, in violation of law, and

**Whereas**, certain lawmakers within the United States government have persisted and now again succeeded in forcing American taxpayers, against their desires and beliefs, in multiple ways, to fund and undergird these wicked practices through government subsidization of Planned Parenthood and other less prominent abortion providers, and

**Whereas**, medical science has advanced to the point of demonstrating, beyond a shadow of a doubt, that the fetus within a mother’s womb is a living organism, distinct from the mother’s body (possessing distinct DNA, etc.), and

**Whereas**, the notion that a living being changes into a different type of organism by passing through a birth canal is untenable, both logically and scientifically, and

**Whereas**, God’s Word refers to life in the womb as having personal and human existence (e.g., Psalm 139:15, 16) and metes out capital punishment for causing the death of an unborn child, confirming its status as a human being created in the image of God (Exodus 21:22-25), and

**Whereas**, the oft-cited cases of rape and incest do not justify the practice of abortion, since the unborn child bears no responsibility for those sins and is the one who is punished by losing his life through abortion, though innocent, which is a gross injustice and which adds sin to sin, and

**Whereas**, there are no such things as “unwanted children,” adoption agencies having waiting lists of years consisting of couples who desperately want children and would gladly take those who are being aborted, and

**Whereas**, the support services for pregnant women, including the unwed, are so prolific as to render any woman contemplating an abortion utterly unjustified, if not dishonest, in basing her decision on a lack of support or ability to raise the child, and

**Whereas**, since Roe v. Wade, there have been more than 51 million murders of human beings through abortion in the United States (an extremely conservative figure, according to a comparison of information from the Centers for Disease Control and the Guttmacher Institute), the overwhelming majority of which have been perpetrated in order to avoid responsibility, undergird promiscuity, assert sinful autonomy, or for other depraved purposes (based on information obtained through surveys by the Guttmacher Institute), and

**Whereas**, according to statistics available to the public, in the state of New York there are now more abortions than live births, and

**Whereas**, God’s Word continually commands His people to do such as to “open your mouth for the mute, for the rights of all who are destitute; open your mouth, judge righteously, defend the rights of the poor and needy” (Proverbs 31:8, 9), and is full of such admonitions to speak for and defend those who cannot speak for or defend themselves, and

**Whereas**, the foregoing demonstrates that the unborn are most certainly a quintessential example of such a category of persons, and

**Whereas**, the recent bill passed by the New York legislature and signed into law by its governor effectively legalizes not only abortion up until the moment of birth, but also infanticide after birth at the sole discretion of the mother, an act which was given a standing ovation by those present, and

**Whereas**, actions such as these are beyond ghastly and are indicated in Scripture to invite the severe judgment of God upon societies and nations which permit such atrocities, and

**Whereas**, our _Confession of Faith_ indicates that it is proper and necessary for synods, “…by way of humble petition in cases extraordinary…” ( _WCF_ 31:4) to address matters which pertain to the civil magistrate, and

**Whereas**, both the legalization of the murder of a helpless class, resulting in the loss of more than 51 million lives, and the forced subsidization of such by individuals opposed to it, constitute such an extraordinary case by any reasonable and honest definition, and

**Whereas**, elders in the Church of the Lord Jesus Christ are shepherds and watchmen, given the responsibility to warn its people of the solemn judgment of God when their sin demands such, and

**Whereas**, the Scriptures declare that a failure to discharge this responsibility faithfully, as in the case of the watchmen and shepherds of Israel, disposes those shepherds and watchmen themselves to the awesome judgment of God (Isaiah 56:9-12; Ezekiel 33:1-9; 34:1-10); **Therefore**,

be it resolved that the Session of Christ the King Presbyterian Church, Norman, Oklahoma, hereby overture the 47 [th] General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America and request that it officially, and on the basis of the inspired and authoritative Word of God, declare the following: First, that all who participate in the practice of abortion, including mothers, for any reason other than to save the physical life of the mother, are guilty of complicity in heinous sin, and subject to the severe judgment of God unless they repent and seek His forgiveness, Second, that all such above persons are not beyond the grace of God, and that forgiveness and healing are abundantly available to such as will humble themselves in godly sorrow leading to sincere repentance and submission to the revealed will of God in Scripture, Third, that sexual activity and pregnancy are inextricably linked according to the design of God the Creator, rendering individuals engaged in intentional sexual activity responsible for all of its results and consequences, and Fourth, that a woman’s body, in addition to being biologically distinct from an unborn baby, is moreover not solely her own, to be regarded and utilized purely in accordance with her own autonomous discretion, but rather ultimately belongs to God the Creator, and is to be utilized strictly in accordance with His revealed will, and Fifth, that all professing Christians, especially those who labor in civil government, are obligated to oppose the practice of abortion with all the vehemence befitting the severity and urgency of the issue, Sixth, that professing Christians who labor in civil government, who also advocate and approve of the practice of abortion, should be disciplined to the limit of church censure until and unless they repent of such wickedness, and Seventh, that the civil authorities in the United States of America, at all levels, are hereby warned that they are inviting the temporal and awesome judgment of God upon our nation in allowing this atrocity to continue, and that they are urged to repent and exercise their God-given function of restraining evil by protecting the rights of all people, especially the powerless, and including the unborn, and Eighth, that the churches of the Presbyterian Church in America are urged to consider the inclusion of ministries to abortion clinics and support of and cooperation with pro-life pregnancy resource agencies as part of their diaconal programs, and Ninth, that the churches of the Presbyterian Church in America are urged to redouble their efforts in biblical instruction on sex, sexuality, marriage, and family, and on God’s will concerning the interrelation of and participation in all of these. **Grounds:** 1. The previous overtures and statements approved by the General Assembly, while salutary, do not speak explicitly and strongly enough to the biblical specifics surrounding the issue. 2. These previous overtures contain nothing affirming and declaring the responsibility of the Church to discipline those who are sinning in this area. 3. These previous overtures include little regarding the responsibility of Christian political leaders to oppose abortion, or regarding their sin in being complicit in approving or promoting it through various legislation, and their consequent liability to church discipline. Inasmuch as there are many professing Christians in the political arena who regard their political activity and commitments as unaccountable to church discipline and who thus feel confident in participating in the promotion of abortion on demand and its funding with no spiritual consequences, this needs to be addressed explicitly and officially by the Church, and such attention is long overdue. 4. The scientific, historical, and moral mythology surrounding abortion and its justification, including the deceptive and false context of the Roe V. Wade decision, and the nature and history of Planned Parenthood, needs to be officially and comprehensively addressed in the rationale for the Church’s position. This has not been adequately accomplished in previous overtures, and is the reason for the extensive “Whereas” section of the proposed overture. 5.
