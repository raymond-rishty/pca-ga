# GA30 O30 — Amend BCO 34-1 to Require Ten Percent of Presbyteries to Trigger Original Jurisdiction

**Assembly:** 30th (2002)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 30 from Philadelphia Presbytery (to CCB & B&O)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference  ·  **BCO:** 34-1

*Source: [ga30_2002 p. 219](../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p219)*

---

and Iowa Presbytery “Require Ten Percent of Presbyteries to Assume Original Jurisdiction Over a Minister in BCO 34-1 - Version 2”

Whereas, the grassroots history of the Presbyterian Church in America indicates her desire to act in unity with the consensus of her churches; and

Whereas, the present wording of Book of Church Order 34-1 allows only two presbyteries to require the General Assembly to decide whether to assume original jurisdiction in judicial process against a minister in another Presbytery; and

Whereas, this standard can allow presbyteries of a particular perspective, and potentially quite few churches, to challenge the ordinary rights of a Presbytery to determine its own membership according to the men and circumstances best known by the local Presbytery; and

Whereas, this standard, in effect, can allow two presbyteries and a few churches, to bring charges against one or many presbyters across Presbytery boundaries and flood the General Assembly with charges that express the concerns of very few; and

Whereas, such concerns should not be allowed to challenge the ordinary rights of any Presbytery to determine its own membership unless there is a wide consensus in the church that a Presbytery has failed to act properly; Therefore, to ensure that a small minority of presbyteries or churches does not exercise inordinate influence over other churches, presbyteries and the General Assembly in pursuing the discipline of ministers; and to ensure that there is a significant indication of a likely consensus in the denomination that the GA must assume original jurisdiction in such a case for the sake of purity and testimony of the church,

Therefore be it resolved Philadelphia Presbytery / Iowa Presbytery overtures the General Assembly to amend the Book of Church Order by changing the words “two other” in BCO 34-1 to “at least ten percent of all the presbyteries.” 2.
