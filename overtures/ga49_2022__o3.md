# GA49 O3 — Withdraw from the National Association of Evangelicals

**Assembly:** 49th (2022)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 3 from Pee Dee Presbytery (to IRC)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga49_2022 p. 1304](../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p1304)*

---
