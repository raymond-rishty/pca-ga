# GA1 O1 — Approve the Gulf Coast Presbytery Boundary Change

**Assembly:** 1st (1973)  ·  **Source:** LF Coast Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga01_1973 p. 19](../markdown/ga01_1973.md#ga01-p19)*

---

"That the first General Assembly of the Continuing Presbyterian Church leave the boundaries of the presbyteries fluid through 1974 and to consult with the presbyteries concerning their boundaries before any changes are planned."
