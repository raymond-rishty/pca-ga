# GA28 O16 — Amend BCO 56-5 to Replace Archaic Phrase 'Of God's Appointment'

**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 16 From New Jersey Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Referred  ·  **BCO:** 56-5

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 286](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p286)*

---

“Revise BCO 56-5 Regarding “Means of God’s Appointment”

Whereas, the first paragraph of the directory for the worship of God, states that “BCO 56, 57 and 58 have been given full constitutional authority by the Eleventh General Assembly after being submitted to the Presbyteries and receiving the necessary two-thirds (2/3) approval of the Presbyteries,” and

Whereas, BCO 56-5 states, regarding the questions a minister is to ask when doing an infant baptism, “The minister shall then propose the following questions. . .”, and

Whereas, the word “shall” requires the minister to use the specific wording of the questions as recorded in BCO 56-5, and

Whereas, in BCO 56-5, question three contains the archaic phrase “o f God’s appointment”, Therefore, be it so ordered that the BCO be revised by the following action: Delete the phrase “o f God’s appointment” and add the phrase “appointed by God” in BCO 56-5, so that question three will read, in part, . and that you will strive, by all the means appointed by God, to bring (him) up in the nurture and admonition of the Lord?” not adopted by the presbytery of new Jersey. [RAO 10-10] Attested by: /s/ James A. Smith, Stated Clerk 8. That OVERTURE 17 from Philadelphia Presbytery (“Add Vow for Elders when Receiving Members”) be answered in the negative. Adopted Grounds: such avow is already affirmed by the elders of the church (cf. BCO 21-5.7. -5 .8 ; 24-5.4).
