# GA34 O26 — Adopt Missouri Presbytery Report on Federal Vision Theology

**Assembly:** 34th (2006)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 26 from Missouri Presbytery (to B&O)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga34_2006 p. 212](../markdown/ga34_2006.md#ga34-p212)*

---
