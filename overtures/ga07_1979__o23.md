# GA7 O23 — Honor Pre-1978 Donor Designations for the Five-in-Five Capital Funds Campaign

**Assembly:** 7th (1979)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of New River  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 58-5

*Source: [ga07_1979 p. 38](../markdown/ga07_1979.md#ga07-p38)*

---

That the Book of Church Order, paragraph 58-5, be changed in Question 2 to read as follows: 2. “Do you believe in the Lord Jesus Christ as the eternal Son of God, and only Saviour of sinners, and do you receive and rest upon Him alone for salvation as He is offered in the Gospel?” **The Rationale:** The rationale for such a change lies in the felt need to express our church vows more specifically in harmony with our biblical beliefs and in clear agreement with the Westminster Confession of Faith, chapter 8, “Of Christ The Mediator.” Also, such clarification is needed to express precisely our belief in our Lord’s Diety as the eternal Son of God, apart from the several cultic groups (e.g. Mormons) who call Him the Son of God, without any biblical understanding attached to their wording. In summary, the addition of these two words will clearly denote our Lord’s eternal Deity and that “there is salvation in none other.” Passed by New River Presbytery in Session at South Ruffner Presbyterian Church, Charleston, West Virginia, this date, March 10, 1979: Attest: James N. Gay Moderator R. E. Kresge Clerk Answered in the negative. § 7-41, III, 25; p. 105.
