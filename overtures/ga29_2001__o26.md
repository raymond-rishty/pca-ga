# GA29 O26 — Affirm that Women May Not Preach or Teach in Corporate Worship

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 26 From New York State Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 171](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p171)*

---

“Redraw New York State and Ascension Presbytery Boundaries” Whereas, the current western boundary of the New York State Presbytery includes central New York east and including Wayne, Ontario and Steuben counties; and, Whereas, the current eastern boundary of the Ascension Presbytery includes western New York west and including Monroe, Livingston and Allegany counties; and Whereas, the city of Rochester located in Monroe County and within the boundary of Ascension Presbytery has no PCA church; and Whereas, there are several families who have expressed interest in the past several years in forming a core group for the purpose of starting a PCA church in the Rochester area; and Whereas, there are some families who travel from the Rochester area to New Life Presbyterian Church in Ithaca, NY, a mission church in the New York State Presbytery, for worship; and Whereas, these same families have indicated a desire to affiliate with the New York State Presbytery; and Whereas, the New York State Presbytery would like to initiate and oversee this developing PCA work in the Rochester area as part of its vision to plant healthy and reproducing PCA churches in every major city in upstate New York; Therefore, Be It Resolved that the New York State Presbytery hereby overtures the 29th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to redraw the western boundary of New York State Presbytery and the northeastern New York boundary of Ascension Presbytery, to wit, that the counties east of and including Monroe and Livingston be transferred to New York State Presbytery, and furthermore,
