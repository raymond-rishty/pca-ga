“Commendation and Thanks for Dr Paul Gilchrist’s Exemplary Service as Stated Clerk” 26-18, III, 6, p. 60,62 COMMUNICATIONS COMMUNICATION 1 From NAP ARC re CRC (to IRC) 26-22, III, 7, p 73 COMMUNICATION 2 From the Christian Reformed Church in North America (IRC) 26-22, III, 13, p. 91 COMMUNICATION 3 From Presbyterian Evangelistic Fellowship Dear Dr Gilchrist: I am thankful for your work and dedication on the behalf of the PCA. I sent out the following letter to all the Stated Clerks of the Presbyteries. I asked them to include this letter in the docket for their upcoming meetings and if they could read this letter at the next Presbytery meeting I want to wish the Presbyterian Church in America happy 25th anniversary in 1998 We want to thank God for His blessing on the PCA and the pastors, elders, and churches in your Presbytery. Let us rejoice and give God the glory for all the boards and agencies of the PCA. We celebrate Christ the King, who is the head of the PCA As Christ said in Matthew 16:18: “ .. I will build my church; and the gates of hell shall not prevail against it ” Our organization, Presbyterian Evangelistic Fellowship, will be celebrating its 40th anniversary in 1998 The founder of PEF, William E. Hill, PEF’s second executive director, Ben Wilkinson, and its preaching evangelists found a hunger among Presbyterians for a denomination that would uphold Scripture as the inspired Word of God, to the Reformed faith, and to the Great Commission PEF played a vital role in the formation of the PCA.
