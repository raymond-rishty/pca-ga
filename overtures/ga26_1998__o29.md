I sent out the following letter to all the Stated Clerks of the Presbyteries. I asked them to include this letter in the docket for their upcoming meetings and if they could read this letter at the next Presbytery meeting I want to wish the Presbyterian Church in America happy 25th anniversary in 1998 We want to thank God for His blessing on the PCA and the pastors, elders, and churches in your Presbytery. Let us rejoice and give God the glory for all the boards and agencies of the PCA. We celebrate Christ the King, who is the head of the PCA As Christ said in Matthew 16:18: “ .. I will build my church; and the gates of hell shall not prevail against it ” Our organization, Presbyterian Evangelistic Fellowship, will be celebrating its 40th anniversary in 1998 The founder of PEF, William E. Hill, PEF’s second executive director, Ben Wilkinson, and its preaching evangelists found a hunger among Presbyterians for a denomination that would uphold Scripture as the inspired Word of God, to the Reformed faith, and to the Great Commission PEF played a vital role in the formation of the PCA. Bill Hill, James Baird, and Ben Wilkinson, representing PEF along with representatives of three additional bodies became a group of twelve within the PCUS to form what was to be known as the Steering Committee for a Continuing Church. With our commitment to the Great Commission, PEF sensed the need for overseas evangelism and formed the Executive Committee for Overseas Evangelism (ECOE). ECOE’s twenty one missionaries, candidates and staff, became the initial nucleus for what is now Mission to the World. With these missionaries came a $100,000 financial gift from PEF to establish this newly formed mission agency. Not only in the foreign field, but also for domestic missions, PEF provided a framework in which the new denomination could begin its program. A structure known as Mission to the United States was established. When it was turned over intact to the new denomination, it had helped establish 35 congregations in the PC A The transfer included 12 organizing pastors and $40,000. After 40 years, PEF is still training, assisting, and practicing Biblical evangelism anywhere, anytime, with anyone. Presently, we are doing Biblical evangelism in the US and 20 countries. We cooperate with agencies of Presbyterian and Reformed Churches that promote Biblical evangelism. We would be glad to come to your churches to help train your congregation in evangelism. PEF can provide resources like pamphlets and tracts on evangelism Please pray with us as we are asking God to raise up 35 new PEF missionaries in 1998. Also, pray for us as we go into the 21st century for the glory of God and the salvation of souls. if we can be of assistance to you and your church in preaching, evangelistic and revival services, mission’s conferences or training in evangelism, please contact PEF Once again, happy 25th anniversary Presbyterian Church in America . In Christ, /s/ Dr. A1 Herrington, Executive Director Acts 1:8 COMMUNICATION 4 VACATED 26-55, III, 4, p. 198 COMMUNICATION 5 From the Lutheran Church-Missouri Synod (to IRC) “Greetings” 1333 South Kirkwood Road St. Louis, MO 63122-7295 May 18, 1998 Dear Dr Gilchrist: Greetings to you and peace in the name of our risen Lord and Savior Jesus Christ thank you very much for your kind letter of april 28, inviting me to attend your 26th General Assembly as a fraternal correspondent.
