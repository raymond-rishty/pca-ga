# GA8 O9 — Establish a theological library at Ridge Haven

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of the Western Carolinas  ·  **Disposition:** Referred

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 36](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p36)*

---

Whereas, the General Assembly has approved in the plans for Ridge Haven the possible inclusion of a theological center on the grounds, and

Whereas, the usefulness of Ridge Haven for the church at large will be enhanced by the establishment of a theological library to be developed and maintained there. Therefore, the Presbytery of the Western Carolinas overtures the General Assembly to direct the Committee on Christian Education to provide for the establishment of a theological library at Ridge Haven, and to receive donations for such. The Committee on Christian Education shall consult with the Board of Directors of Ridge Haven as to the housing and custody of such a library " The Presbytery voted to adopt for overture to the General Assembly January 15, 1980. A. Boyce Spooner Stated Clerk Referred to the Committee for Christian Education and Publications to report back to the Ninth General Assembly. § 8-29, III, 25; p. 73.
