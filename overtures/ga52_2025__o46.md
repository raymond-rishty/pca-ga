# GA52 O46 — Change the Boundaries of Georgia Foothills & Metro Atlanta Presbyteries

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 46 from the Metro Atlanta Presbytery (to MNA)

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1299](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1299)*

---

“Change the Boundaries of Georgia Foothills and Metro Atlanta Presbyteries”

**Whereas** Lumpkin County, GA is the farthest northern point of Metro Atlanta Presbytery; and

**Whereas** it lies geographically in the northeast Georgia mountains, near many churches within the current boundary of Georgia Foothills Presbytery; and

**Whereas** Georgia Foothills Presbytery desires to start an RUF campus ministry at University of North Georgia located in the city of Dahlonega in Lumpkin County; and

**Whereas** the Session of Creekstone Church, the only PCA church located in Lumpkin County, is agreeable to come into Georgia Foothills Presbytery; and

**Whereas** the minutes of the 46 [th] General Assembly established the boundary of Georgia Foothills Presbytery to be the Georgia counties of Hall, Barrow, Jackson, Clarke, Oconee, Habersham, and Union counties; also the counties of Oglethorpe, Elbert, Madison, Hart, Franklin, Banks, White, Stephens, Rabun, and Towns; and Gwinnett county north of the defined boundaries, from east to west, of Highway 316, then Sugarloaf Parkway to Peachtree Industrial Boulevard, then an imaginary line westward from the intersection of Peachtree Industrial Boulevard and Sugarloaf Parkway to the Chattahoochee River; and

**Whereas** the minutes of the 46 [th] General Assembly established the boundary of Metro Atlanta Presbytery to be the Georgia counties of Dekalb, Fulton, Clayton, Cobb (East of I-75), Dawson, Lumpkin, Coweta, Heard, Spalding, Troup, Merriweather, Pike, Fayette, Forsyth, Rockdale, Newton, Henry, and Walton; and Gwinnett county south of the defined boundaries, from east to west, of Highway 316, then Sugarloaf Parkway to Peachtree Industrial Boulevard, then an imaginary line westward from the intersection of Peachtree Industrial Boulevard and Sugarloaf Parkway to the Chattahoochee River;

**Therefore be it resolved** that Metro Atlanta Presbytery overtures the 52 [nd] General Assembly as follows: - That Lumpkin County be transferred from Metro Atlanta Presbytery to Georgia Foothills Presbytery; - By adding Lumpkin County to the list of Georgia counties included within the boundary of Georgia Foothills Presbytery; and - By removing Lumpkin County from the list of Georgia counties included within the boundary of Metro Atlanta Presbytery. _Adopted by the Metro Atlanta Presbytery at its stated meeting, May 6, 2025_ _Attested by TE Randy Schlichting, stated clerk_
