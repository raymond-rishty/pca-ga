“Establish a Study Committee on Domestic Violence and Sexual Assault” (Version 7)

**Whereas**, the sins of domestic abuse and sexual assault are expressly forbidden (2 Timothy 3:2-5; Jude 7; Exodus 21:15; Deuteronomy 22:2527; also _WLC_ 135 and 139); and

**Whereas**, these sins are especially heinous because they are “against the express letter of the law,” “break many commandments,” “admit of no reparation,” and often involve various other aggravations ( _WLC_ 151); and

**Whereas**, these sins are grievously prevalent in our society; [17] and

**Whereas**, these sins often produce in the victims a complex response of denial along with immense fear and shame that persist with varying severity throughout life (e.g., 2 Samuel 13:1-22); and
