“Add to BCO 26-1 Procedure for Dealing with Alleged Conflicts Between Scripture and the Constitution of the PCA”

Whereas, the 29th General Assembly found itself in the position that its rules compelled it to distribute in its permanent records material harmful to the reputation of certain people and groups; and

Whereas, the Constitutional Business Committee reported to the 29th General Assembly that we had no option but to follow BCO, even if it appeared to conflict with Scripture, with the only remedy being to amend BCO after the fact to avoid such action in the future; and

Whereas, this was not a matter of judicial process against these people; and
