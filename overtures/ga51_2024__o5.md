# GA51 O5 — Adjust Piedmont Triad & Catawba Valley Presbytery Boundaries

**Assembly:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 5 from Piedmont Triad Presbytery (to MNA)

*Source: [ga51_2024 p. 1080](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p1080)*

---
