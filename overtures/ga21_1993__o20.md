# GA21 O20 — Amend BCO 21-4(d) to Permit Ordination Sermons Heard by Commission

**Assembly:** 21st (1993)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 20 From Covenant Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Approved & ratified (1994)  ·  **Ratified**  ·  **BCO:** 21-4

*Source: [ga21_1993 p. 136](../markdown/ga21_1993.md#ga21-p136)*

---

> *ITEM 4. That BCO 21-4,d be amended as follows...The intention is to allow this practice to continue, but not require it. Adopted VOTE: FOR 37 AGAINST 12*

"Amend BCO 21-4, d To Permit Ordination Sermons to be Heard By Commission"

Whereas, often presbyteries are faced with many TE ordination candidates at a meeting in which they all must preach a sermon before the presbytery,

Whereas, this may be facilitated by allowing several men to preach before several commissions simultaneously,

Whereas, many presbyteries have used this procedure without any discernible deficiencies; and

Whereas, there has arisen some confusion and differences of interpretation of the present language of the Book of Church Order as to whether this is permitted;

Therefore be it resolved to clear up the present confusion and differences as to whether this is permitted by amending the Book of Church Order 21- 4,d., by adding the words "or committee thereof, upon 3/4 vote." The intention is to allow this practice to continue, but not require it. Adopted by Covenant Presbytery at its stated meeting on March 2,1993. Attested by: /s/ Robert L. Penny Stated Clerk
