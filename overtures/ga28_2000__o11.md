# GA28 O11 — (untitled overture)

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 259](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p259)*

From the Presbytery of Mississippi Valley “Authorize Formation of a Provisional Presbytery in Russia”

**Whereas**, TE Blake Purcell is a member of Mississippi Valley Presbytery, and

**Whereas**, Mississippi Valley Presbytery has authorized TE Blake Purcell to serve out of bounds as an evangelist in St. Petersburg, Russia, and

**Whereas**, Mississippi Valley Presbytery has entrusted to TE Blake Purcell (BCO 8-6) "the power to organize churches, and, until there is a Session in the church so organized, to instruct, examine, ordain, and install ruling elders and deacons therein, and to receive or dismiss members;" **Therefore, Be it Resolved** that Mississippi Valley Presbytery overture the 28th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America, according to BCO 15-6, to exercise its "power to commit to a commission, consisting of not less than three elders, the task of forming a provisional Presbytery in a foreign country [Russia] where there exists no compatible indigenous presbyterian and reformed church. Such a commission shall have authority to act as the Presbytery in all matters pertaining to the establishment and ordering of a national church and shall report annually to the General Assembly. The commission shall be dissolved when there are at least three national teaching elders and three organized churches under its care, and these shall then constitute a separate national church." Adopted at the Winter Stated Meeting of The Presbytery of the Mississippi Valley on February 15, 2000. Attested by: /s/ Roger G. Collins, Stated Clerk 10. That **Personal Resolution #4** from TE Jeffrey Black be answered in the negative. The reason for this is that TE Jeffrey Black and MTW have talked and TE Black is satisfied with their response. The fund drive has been discontinued, MTW has properly disposed of all funds and the Committee of Commissioners commends MTW for acting with complete integrity and good prudence in this matter. *Adopted* **Personal Resolution #4** from TE Jeffrey Black “The Mission to the World Committee make to this General Assembly a full disclosure of the fund drive to raise one million dollars for the ministry of the Rev. Franco Maggiotto in Italy, specifically addressing whether or not this fund drive is continuing and if not, what will be done with the $500,000 already raised by designated gifts publicly reported by MTW in the early spring of this year.”
