# GA52 O21 — Change the Boundaries of Covenant and Hills & Plains Presbyteries

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 21 from the Covenant Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Withdrawn

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1207](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1207)*

---

“Change the Boundaries of Covenant and Hills & Plains Presbyteries” _**WITHDRAWN BY COVENANT PRESBYTERY**_ **Whereas** Sebastian County, Arkansas, where Covenant Presbyterian Church of Fort Smith is located is on farthest western border of Covenant Presbytery and is adjacent to the border of Hills & Plains Presbytery; **Whereas** the Session of Covenant Presbyterian Church of Fort Smith wants to regularly attend Presbytery meetings but finds it difficult at times due to extended travel; **Whereas** the locations of the stated meetings of Hills & Plains Presbytery are much closer to Fort Smith than the stated meetings of Covenant Presbytery; **Therefore, be it resolved** that Covenant Presbytery, in concurrence with the Session of Covenant Presbyterian Church of Fort Smith and Hills and Plains Presbytery, overtures the 2025 General Assembly to transfer Sebastion, Scott, Crawford, Franklin, Johnson, Logan, and Polk counties from the geographic bounds of Covenant Presbytery to the geographic bounds of Hills & Plains Presbytery. _Adopted by Covenant Presbytery at its stated meeting, February 4, 2025_ _Attested by TE Robert Browning, stated clerk_
