“Amend _BCO_ 30-4 to Require a New Case with Process for Elevation of Indefinite Suspension to Excommunication”

**Be it resolved** that _BCO_ 30-4 be amended as follows (underlining for additions): **30-4.** Excommunication is the excision of an offender from the communion of the Church. This censure is to be inflicted only on account of gross crime or heresy and when the offender shows himself incorrigible and contumacious. Elevating indefinite suspension from the Sacraments to excommunication shall require a new case of process establishing that the matter is a gross crime or heresy and that the offender has shown himself incorrigible and contumacious. The design of this censure is to operate on the offender as a means of reclaiming him, to deliver the church from the scandal of his offense, and to inspire all with fear by the example of his discipline. So that _BCO_ 30-4 as amended would read: **30-4.** Excommunication is the excision of an offender from the communion of the Church.
