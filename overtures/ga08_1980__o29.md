# GA8 O29 — Amend BCO 15-1 on committee alternates

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** Covenant Presbytery  ·  **BCO:** 15-1

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 42](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p42)*

---

Covenant Presbytery respectfully overtures the Eighth General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to amend the last sentence of chapter 15-1, paragraph 11 of the Book of Church Order to read as follows: " each alternate should attend each meeting and fill any vacancies at the meeting with full voting privileges." Adopted at the 28th Stated Meeting of Covenant Presbytery, April 15, 1980. Attest: Robert S Hayes, Stated Clerk Answered in the affirmative. § 8-88, III, 13; p. 118. To the Committee of Commissioners on Mission to the United States
