# GA15 O30 — Amend BCO 19-6 to Drop Unlicensed Candidates from Presbytery Rolls

**Assembly:** 15th (1987)  ·  **Source:** Philadelphia Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Approved & ratified (1988)  ·  **Ratified**  ·  **BCO:** 19-6

*Source: [ga15_1987 p. 54](../markdown/ga15_1987.md#ga15-p54)*

---

It is our recommendation that the phrase, "A person who fails to renew his licensure before its expiration date will be dropped from the rolls of Presbytery" be added as a final sentence in BCO 19-6. Adopted at the March 14, 1987 Stated Meeting of the Philadelphia Presbytery. Attest: Iain Crichton, Stated Clerk Adopted as amended. See 15-83, III, 36, p. 178 for amended text.
