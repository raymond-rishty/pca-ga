# GA23 O9 — Amend RAO 3-2 and 14-2-h-2 to Require Memorials for Teaching Elders

**Assembly:** 23rd (1995)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 9 From Central Carolina Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga23_1995 p. 215](../markdown/ga23_1995.md#ga23-p215)*

---

"Amend RAO 3-2 and 14-2-h-2 To Require Memorials for Teaching Elders" Whereas, is it appropriate and biblical to remember the good works of believers, as an example to others (1 Timothy 1:3, Rev. 14:13). Therefore, Central Carolina Presbytery humbly overtures the General Assembly to amend the Rules of Assembly Operations by adding to 3-2 (Responsibilities of the Stated Clerk) the following: "U. At the Opening Services of General Assembly, he shall read the names of Teaching Elders who have passed away during the year and schedule a memorial moment in their memory. He shall record their names in the minutes of General Assembly together with a brief summary of their ministry, as provided by their member presbytery." Also, Central Carolina Presbytery humbly overtures the General Assembly to amend the Rules of Assembly Operations by adding to 14-2-h-2 (Guidelines for Keeping Presbytery Minutes) the following: "Record shall also be made of Teaching and Ruling Elders who have passed away during the year, with a brief summary of their ministry, as provided by their member presbytery." Adopted by Central Carolina Presbytery. Attested by: /s/ David M. Frierson, Stated Clerk 5. That the Registration fee for the General Assembly be increased from $100 per commissioner to $115 per commissioner for the 24th General Assembly only. Adopted Grounds: The Administrative Committee is receiving mandates from the General Assembly which are not being funded.
