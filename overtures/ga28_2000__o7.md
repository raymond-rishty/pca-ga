# GA28 O7 — Add BCO 16-4 Prohibiting Teaching of Exceptions Harmful to the Church

**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 7 from presbytery of north florida  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 16-4

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 292](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p292)*

---

“Decry contentious practices of the ‘Presbyterian and Reformed News’” Whereas, the Presbyterian and Reformed News, in an apparent disregard for the wisdom of Proverbs 6:16-19, [There are six things which the Lord hates, Yes, seven which are an abomination to Him: haughty eyes, a lying tongue, and hands that shed innocent blood, a heart that devises wicked plans, feet that run rapidly to evil, a false witness who utters lies, and one who spreads strife among brothers, (NASB)] has printed stories and information that has created much confusion and fomented division within the PCA, and Whereas, the Presbyterian and Reformed News has printed statements even though the person or persons have asked not to be quoted (Spring 1998 issue, p. 5), and Whereas, the Presbyterian and Reformed News has printed statements about a member of North Florida Presbytery that called into question his reputation, integrity and Christian character (New Bulletin #27-1, June 14-15, 1999), and Whereas, the Presbyterian and Reformed News has printed summaries of the minutes of North Florida Presbytery, Therefore Be it Resolved, that the Presbytery of North Florida overtures the Presbytery of the Northeast to instruct the Editor(s) of the Presbyterian and Reformed News that Christian charity demands that they more seriously consider and humbly respect the requests of Christian brothers and Presbyteries.
