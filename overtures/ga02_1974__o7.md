# GA2 O7 — Suspend BCO Section 27-2 for Not Less Than Five Years

**Assembly:** 2nd (1974)  ·  **Source:** Adopted by Warrior Presbytery at its Stated Meeting of May 21,

*Source: [ga02_1974 p. 27](../markdown/ga02_1974.md#ga02-p27)*

---

1974 Out of sincere concern for the well being of the National Presbyterian Church and a desire to promote the peace and tranquility of the Church; Warrior Presbytery respectfully overtures the Second General Assembly of the National Presbyterian Church that:

Whereas: There are issues which have not been decided which directly affect basic elements of the Book of Church Order such as: 1. Paragraph 7-1 of the form of Government. 2. The dropping of the inactive roll. 3. The question of rotation of officers. 4. The concept of total and complete parity of ruling and teaching elders to include ruling elders as moderators of the sessions and to officiate at the sacrament of Holy Communion. 5. The vital concern that the Book of Worship reflect Reformed Worship more thoroughly. __ 6. The consideration of the denomination’s whole attitude on church discipiine. 7. The controversy about evangelism.

Whereas: There are many who hold contrary opinions on these matters.

Whereas: There are many items of personal concern with differing elements in the Book of Church Order which should be heard and considered fully by the General Assembly.

Whereas: This section 27-2 of the form of Government if enacted would severely limit the active discussion of these vital areas and apply pressure on the individuals and groups concerned to receive a hearing on their suggestions and recommendations at this assembly. Be It Resolved: That section 27-2 of the form of Government of the Book of Church Order be suspended for a period of not less than five years.
