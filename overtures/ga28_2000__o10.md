# GA28 O10 — Amend BCO 53-6 to Restrict Preaching to Qualified Men Only

**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 10 from the presbytery of mississippi valley  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 53-6

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 284](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p284)*

---

“Restrict Preaching or Exhorting to Qualified Men Only” Whereas, the Westminster Confession of Faith, Chapter XXI, paragraph 5 discusses the elements of corporate worship, and “sound preaching. . .” is an element of corporate worship, and Whereas, god ordained men to bethe spiritual leaders of israel (Ex. 18: 17-26), and Whereas, the priesthood of israel was composed only of the sons of aaron (Ex. 28: 1-4), and Whereas, the apostles of Jesus, used of god to establish the new covenant Church for our Lord, were all men (Mt. 10: 1-4), and Whereas, the Bible forbids women to teach or exercise authority over men (I Tim. 2: 11-15), and Whereas, Larger Catechism, question 158 states: “.. .the word of g odis to be preached only by such as are sufficiently gifted and also duly approved and called to that office,” and Whereas, God forbids ordained office to women (I Tim. 3: 2,12, Titus 1: 6), and Whereas, the entire bible teaches the principle of covenant heads hip of men over women (I Cor. 11: 3), a principle which is usurped when women exhort or instruct men in the church, Let it Therefore Be Resolved that Mississippi Valley Presbytery overture the 28th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to replace Book of Church Order paragraph 53-6, which currently reads: “No persons hall be invited to preach in any of the churches under our care without the consent of the Session, ” with the following: “Only qualified men with the consent of the sessions hall be invited to preach/exhort in any of the churches under our care. ” adopted at t hew interstate d meeting of the pres
