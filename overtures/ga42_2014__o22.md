# GA42 O22 — Establish Study Committee Regarding BCO 21-5, Third Ordination Vow

**Assembly:** 42nd (2014)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 22 from Philadelphia Presbytery (to AC, OC)  ·  **BCO:** 21-5

*Source: [ga42_2014 p. 825](../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p825)*

---
