# GA28 O8 — Direct Interchurch Relations Committee to Investigate EPC's Reception of Suspended PCA Elder

**Assembly:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 8 From Illiana Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Referred

*Source: [ga28_2000 p. 66](../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p66)*

---

"Evangelical Presbyterian Church Receiving Suspended PCA Teaching Elder"

Whereas, Illiana Presbytery imposed a censure of indefinite suspension on TE David W. Baer in 1998, and

Whereas, Illiana Presbytery’s action of censure was communicated in the Spring of 1999 to TE Corbett Heimburger, Chairman of the Evangelical Presbyterian Church’s Mid-America Presbytery’s Candidates and Credentials Committee, and

Whereas, TE Heimburger indicated that the EPC would not receive TE Baer as long as he was under discipline in the PCA, and

Whereas, that censure has not been lifted by Illiana Presbytery but has been left in force by a Subcommittee of the General Assembly’s Standing Judicial Commission which declined the appeal of TE Baer in June of 1999, and

Whereas, the EPC Mid-America Presbytery examined and voted to receive TE Baer into their fellowship on October 23, 1999 despite his still being under censure in the PCA, and

Whereas, the EPC Mid-America Presbytery at the same meeting approved the appointment of TE Baer as organizing pastor of Trinity Fellowship Church, composed of people who had left TE Baer’s former PCA Church, Westminster PCA, in Godfrey, Illinois, and

Whereas, the EPC Mid-America Presbytery rejected the Illiana Presbytery’s Censure and the SJC Subcommittee’s decision as being in error, Therefore, Illiana Presbytery respectfully overtures the General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to take this matter into advisement in regard to current or future relations with the EPC and to assign the Interchurch Relations Committee to investigate this action by the EPC and to recommend an appropriate response and/or actions to be taken by PCA churches that have relations with EPC denominational churches in their area. Adopted at the Winter Stated Meeting of Illiana Presbytery on January 15, 2000. Attested by: /s/ Dale L. Eisenreich, Stated Clerk 6. That the Permanent Committee of the IRC investigate and clarify the relationship between the PCA and the World Fellowship of Reformed Churches (WFRC) and report their findings to the 29th GA.
