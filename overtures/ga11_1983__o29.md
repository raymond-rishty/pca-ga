# GA11 O29 — Establish a Committee to Refine Inconsistencies in the Book of Church Order

**Assembly:** 11th (1983)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of St. Louis  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga11_1983 p. 48](../markdown/ga11_1983.md#ga11-p48)*

---

Whereas, there is need to refine the Book of Church Order in regard to inconsistencies, apparently vague statements, and grammatical and typographical corrections,

Therefore, be it resolved that the Eleventh General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America, meeting in Norfolk, Virginia, June 13-17, 1983, establish a committee to assist the Stated Clerk of the General Assembly in refining the Book of Church Order, such committee to serve for two years. Attest: ALBERT F. MOGINOT, JR., STATED CLERK Answered in the negative §11-36, III, 57, p. 101.
