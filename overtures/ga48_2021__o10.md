**Assembly:** 48th (2021)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 10 from the Presbytery of the Ascension (to CCB, OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga48_2021 p. 984](../markdown/ga48_2021.md#ga48-p984)*
