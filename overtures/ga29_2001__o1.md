# GA29 O1 — Amend BCO 14-1(12) to Increase Committee Size and Extend Terms to Five Years

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 1 from Missouri Presbytery (to CCB &amp; B&amp;O)  ·  **Disposition:** Approved & ratified (2002)  ·  **Ratified**  ·  **BCO:** 14-1

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 190](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p190)*

---

“Amend BCO 14-1(12), Increase Number on Committees, Extend Terms to Five Years”

Whereas, the founders of our denomination, in order to minimize the concentration of power in the hierarchy of our denomination, created the structure of our General Assembly Committees and Agencies so that Committee members would be elected, in classes, for 4-year terms and are only eligible for re-election after a year has elapsed,

Whereas, the responsibility and complexity of our Program Committees have grown as our denomination has grown,

Whereas, it is very difficult for newly elected Committee members to function effectively until their 4-year term is almost half over,

Whereas, this frequent turnover of Committee members coupled with the increased size and complexity of Committee operations tends to place more power in the hands of the permanent staff which is opposed to the intent of the founders of our denomination,

Whereas, the proposed change provides for greater continuity in our Program Committees while still providing for the regular rotation of Committee members, Therefore Be it Resolved that BCO paragraph 14-1(12) in the second to the last paragraph be revised as follows: “Committee on Christian Education and Publications, Committee on Mission to North America, Committee on Mission to the World, and Committee on Reformed University Ministries shall consist of fifteen (15) men divided into five classes of three men each, with two men being TEs and one RE or two men being REs and one TE on alternate years on odd years and four men each on even years, elected to serve five-year terms. Committees on Christian Education and Publications, Mission to North America, Mission to the World and Reformed University Ministries shall have one ruling elder and one teaching elder as alternates to fill any vacancy that may occur during the year.”

Be it further resolved, that the first class of three (3) men to serve a five year term shall begin in 2003, should the 29th General Assembly approve this Overture, the consent of two-thirds (2/3) of the Presbyteries is given and the 30th General Assembly approves the same; and that each year thereafter there shall be an additional class of three (3) men who shall serve a five year term. Adopted by Missouri Presbytery on April 18, 2000. Attested by: /s/ David R. Stain, Stated Clerk 2. That Overture 2 from New Jersey Presbytery (“Amend BCO 58-5 – Use Unleavened Bread in Communion”) be answered in the negative. Grounds: There are no Scriptural grounds sufficient to mandate the exclusive use of unleavened bread in Communion. Adopted
