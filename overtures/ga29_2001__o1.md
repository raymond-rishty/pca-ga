# GA29 O1 — Amend BCO 14-1(12) to Increase Committee Size and Extend Terms to Five Years

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 1 from Missouri Presbytery (to CCB &amp; B&amp;O)  ·  **Disposition:** Approved & ratified (2002)  ·  **Ratified**  ·  **BCO:** 14-1

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 190](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p190)*

---

“Amend BCO 14-1(12), Increase Number on Committees, Extend Terms to Five Years” Whereas, the founders of our denomination, in order to minimize the concentration of power in the hierarchy of our denomination, created the structure of our General Assembly Committees and Agencies so that Committee members would be elected, in classes, for 4-year terms and are only eligible for re-election after a year has elapsed, Whereas, the responsibility and complexity of our Program Committees have grown as our denomination has grown, Whereas, it is very difficult for newly elected Committee members to function effectively until their 4-year term is almost half over, Whereas, this frequent turnover of Committee members coupled with the increased size and complexity of Committee operations tends to place more power in the hands of the permanent staff which is opposed to the intent of the founders of our denomination, Whereas, the proposed change provides for greater continuity in our Program Committees while still providing for the regular rotation of Committee members, Therefore Be it Resolved that BCO paragraph 14-1(12) in the second to the last paragraph be revised as follows: “Committee on Christian Education and Publications, Committee on Mission to North America, Committee on Mission to the World, and Committee on Reformed University Ministries shall consist of fifteen (15) men divided into five classes of three men each, with two men being TEs and one RE or two men being REs and one TE on alternate years on odd years and four men each on even years, elected to serve five-year terms
