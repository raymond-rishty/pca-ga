# GA29 O38 — Erect a New Presbytery from Remaining Westminster Presbytery Churches

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** overture 38 From Westminster Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 183](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p183)*

---

“Erect New Presbytery” westminster presbytery hereby notifies the General Assembly o fits intention to withdraw from the Presbyterian Church in America, effective at the January 2002 stated meeting of Presbytery; and the General Assembly be overtured to take the following action: That the General Assembly, under the powers granted to it under the PCA Constitution to erect new presbyteries and to unite and divide presbyteries per their request, hereby established a new presbytery, in accordance with the following criteria: those churches and ministers which have certified to the stated clerk of Westminster Presbytery by the January 2002 stated meeting of said presbytery that they wish to remain in Westminster Presbytery, shall be constituted as Westminster Presbytery; while those churches and ministers which have not certified to the stated clerk of westminster presbytery by the january 2002 stated meeting of said presbytery that they wish to remain in westminster Presbytery, shall be constituted as a new Presbytery, effective that date, with the same boundaries as the present Westminster Presbytery. Adopted by Westminster Presbytery on May 15, 2001. Attested by: Is/ D.
