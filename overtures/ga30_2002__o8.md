“Revise Boundaries of James River Presbytery”

Whereas, having been appraised by formal overture of the desire of several of the churches in our Presbytery located in the western area of Virginia desiring to unite with the newly forming Presbytery of the Blue Ridge, and

Whereas, having reviewed the proposed overture from New River Presbytery asking the General Assembly to establish this new Presbytery, and

Whereas, having reviewed the proposed new boundaries of James River Presbytery contained in this proposed overture from New River Presbytery,

Therefore be it resolved that James River Presbytery, voting at Hopewell, Virginia in the One Hundred and Fifth Stated Meeting of January 19, 2002 does hereby approve of the following change to our boundaries effective September 1, 2002: Revised boundaries of James River Presbytery will be as follows: “All of Virginia south and east of and including the following counties, except for the counties of Accomac and Northampton on the Eastern Shore: Northumberland, Westmoreland, King George, Stafford, Spotsylvania, Hanover, Goochland, Cumberland, Buckingham, Prince Edward, Charlotte, and Halifax.” 10. Lee to another term on the Presbyterian and Reformed Joint Commission on Chaplains and Military Personnel (PRJC) as members of the Class of 2006. Adopted 11.
