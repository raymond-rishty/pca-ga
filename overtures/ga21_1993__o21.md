# GA21 O21 — Rebuke MNA for Promoting Lord's Day Superbowl Evangelism Activities

**Assembly:** 21st (1993)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 21 From the Presbytery of Eastern Canada

*Source: [ga21_1993 p. 101](../markdown/ga21_1993.md#ga21-p101)*

---
