# GA26 O10 — Expand Northern California Presbytery Boundaries to Include Nevada Counties and Utah

**Assembly:** 26th (1998)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 10 From Northern California Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Referred

*Source: [ga26_1998 p. 172](../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p172)*

---

“Change boundaries of northern california presbytery to include Additional Counties in Nevada and Utah”

Whereas, the Presbytery of Northern California has shown itself both aggressive and effective in church planting, adding 10 churches and missions from 1991 through 1996 and over 1,000 members, and

Whereas, three of the Northern California Presbytery’s new missions in the last four years a RE in the state of Utah, which is outside the boundaries of any presbytery, and

Whereas, the boundaries of northern california presbytery already include the nevada counties of Douglas, Carson City, Storey, and Lyon and the nevada counties of Washoe, Churchill, Eureka, White Pine, Elko, Humboldt, Lander and Pershing are naturally connected by Interstate 80 and US Route 50,

Therefore be it resolved that the 26th General Assembly ADD THE STATE OF UTAH AND THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES IN THE STATE OF NEVADA: Washoe, Churchill, Eureka, White Pine, Elko, Humboldt, Lander and Pershing to the boundaries of the Northern California Presbytery. adopted at the stated meeting of northern california presbytery on february 11, 1998. Attested by: /s/ Arthur R. Schick, Stated Clerk
