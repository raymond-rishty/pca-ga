# GA29 O34 — Transfer Pulaski and Carroll Counties from Westminster to New River Presbytery

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 34 From Westminster Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 176](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p176)*

---

*(to MNA)* “Revise Boundaries of Westminster Presbytery”

Whereas, the counties of Pulaski and Carroll lie geographically in the New River Valley and at the extreme northeastern end of Westminster Presbytery, closer to all the churches in New River than to those in the entire southern cluster in Westminster; and

Whereas, in the formative studies of potential boundaries for Westminster and New River Presbyteries, significant consideration was given to the possibility of our two Counties being assigned to New River; and

Whereas, the geographical and cultural issues involving our two churches and those in New River Presbytery have led to very positive ministry and outreach sharing, particularly between the Virginia congregations of that Presbytery and ourselves; and

Whereas, New River Presbytery has indicated a desire to receive those two Counties with a notation that adding our two Counties would strengthen their presbytery without unduly weakening a stronger Westminster; and

Whereas, New River Presbytery has formed an ad-hoc committee to explore with some of the churches in James River Presbytery, and our two churches, the possibility of asking the General Assembly in the future to form a new Presbytery in the west-central area of Virginia; Now

Therefore, be it resolved that Westminster Presbytery herewith overtures the 29th General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America to redraw the boundaries of Westminster and New River Presbyteries by transferring Pulaski County and that portion of Carroll County that lies east of Interstate 77 in the Commonwealth of Virginia, and the ministers and churches therein, from Westminster to New River Presbytery, effective September 30, 2001; and

Be it further resolved that the Walthall-Wood Scholarship Fund assets be transferred to the New River Presbytery with the approval of this change of boundary since they are closely tied to the Draper’s Valley Presbyterian Church of Draper, Virginia. Adopted by Westminster Presbytery on April 21, 2001. Attested by: /s/ D. Steven Meyerhoff, Stated Clerk NOTE: New River Presbytery, acting in its 81st Stated Meeting on March 20, 2001, took the following action in regards to a proposed Overture of Westminster Presbytery requesting boundary changes: It was M/S/C to concur with that portion of a proposed overture by Westminster Presbytery dealing with the transfer of Pulaski and Carroll Counties in Virginia and/or the churches and Teaching Elders residing therein to New River Presbytery. Attested by: /s/ Don Clements, Stated Clerk
