**Assembly:** 47th (2019)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 2 from Northwest Georgia Presbytery (to CCB, CTS, OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga47_2019 p. 586](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p586)*
