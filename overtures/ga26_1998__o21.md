# GA26 O21 — Amend BCO 57-5 Adding New Membership Vow on Means of Grace

**Assembly:** 26th (1998)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 21 From the Presbytery of the Ascension  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 57-5

*Source: [ga26_1998 p. 207](../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p207)*

---

“Amend BCO 57-5 by adding New Question for Members” Whereas, many in our churches neglect to avail themselves of opportunities to grow in our Lord Jesus Christ, Whereas, many fail to make Sunday School, Bible studies, special classes, etc. a high priority on their schedules, Whereas, many haven ot created a heritage for their children of family bible study, devotions, and worship in their homes, Whereas, Paul’s admonition to Timothy to “Be diligent to present yourself approved to God as a workman who does not need to be ashamed, handling accurately the word of truth.” (2 Timothy 2:15) is applicable to all of us, laity as well as pastors, Whereas, inclusion of the suggested question below would provide added opportunity for leaders to explain to those joining the church, their responsibilities and privileges with respect to the various means of grace available through the ministry of the church and home, Be it resolved to add to the questions in BCO 57-5, a question which in substance used to appear in the old presbyterian Book of Church Order - namely “Do you promise to make diligent use of all the means of grace which Christ, through His Church, has provided?” It is suggested that this question be numbered question 5, and the current Question 5 be renumbered as Question 6. 13 That Overture 24 from the Session of New Life Presbyterian Church be answered in the negative (see also Committee on Constitutional Business, p 230).
