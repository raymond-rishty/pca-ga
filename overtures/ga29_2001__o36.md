# GA29 O36 — Allow North Hills PCA Salisbury to Join Triad Presbytery Outside Its Geographic Bounds

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 36 From Central Carolina Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 177](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p177)*

---

“North Hills PCA in Salisbury, NC, Seeking Admission into Newly-formed Triad Presbytery”

Whereas, North Hills Presbyterian Church exists for the Glory of God and the furtherance of His Kingdom on Earth;

Whereas, North Hills Presbyterian Church was admitted to the Central Carolina Presbytery in October of 1998 and has been blessed by its association with Central Carolina Presbytery and the Presbyterian Church in America;

Whereas, North Hills Presbyterian Church is located in Rowan County, a county that adjoins the proposed boundaries of the Triad Presbytery;

Whereas, North Hills Presbyterian Church lies in the extreme north of Rowan County next to the proposed boundary of the Triad Presbytery;

Whereas, North Hills Presbyterian Church has a strong affinity to the Triad geographical region as well as to the churches in the new presbytery;

Whereas, this proximity and affinity has already resulted in filial and ministry ties to the churches in the new presbytery;

Whereas, official connection with the Triad Presbytery would strengthen North Hills Presbyterian Church, further its mission, and encourage its members to love and good deeds; Now Therefore North Hills Presbyterian Church hereby petitions and overtures the Central Carolina Presbytery and the General Assembly that it be allowed to join the newly-formed Triad Presbytery outside its geographical bounds effective July 1, 2001. Adopted by the Moderator of Session, North Hills Presbyterian Church and the Clerk of Session, North Hills Presbyterian Church /s/ Jerry C. Currin, Moderator and /s/ M. J. Smith, III, Clerk of Session
