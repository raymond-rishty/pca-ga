# GA52 O14 — Amend BCO 38-1 re Court Interaction with an Offended Party

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 14 from the Great Lakes Presbytery (to CCB, OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 38-1

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1197](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1197)*

---

“Amend _BCO_ 38-1 to Indicate Ways a Court May Interact with an Offended Party”

**Whereas** _BCO_ 38-1 requires that “in any instances… pertinent to the offense against him or her;” and

**Whereas** informing offended person(s), inviting offended person(s) to provide the court comment, and encouraging offended person(s) to enlist the help of an advisor in preparing such comments are all the responsibility of the court; and

**Whereas** compliance with these responsibilities ordinarily requires multiple meetings of the court; and

**Whereas** in some circumstances it may be preferable for a designated individual to interact with an offended person rather than the court itself;

**Be it resolved** that _BCO_ 38-1 be amended by inserting the following language (underlining for additions; strikethrough for deletions): In any instances involving a personal offense ( _BCO_ 29-3), the court Moderator of the court or his designee shall attempt to inform the offended person(s) of that part of the Confession the court deems pertinent to the offense against him or her. The court Moderator of the court or his designee shall invite the offended person to provide the court comment on the Confession prior to final approval of the Confession by the confessor and the court. The court Moderator of the court or his designee shall encourage the offended person to enlist the help of an advisor in preparing any such comments.
