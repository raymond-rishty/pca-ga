# GA10 O8 — Study the Desirability of Publishing a New PCA Hymnal

**Assembly:** 10th (1982)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of Mississippi Valley  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga10_1982 p. 53](../markdown/ga10_1982.md#ga10-p53)*

---

The Presbytery overtures the General Assembly to instruct the Committee on Christian Education to appoint a special committee to study the desirability of the PCA publishing a new hymnal that adequately meets the worship needs of our denomination. THOMAS A. COOK, STATED CLERK Answered in the negative §10-31, III, 19, p. 66.
