# GA14 O21 — Reject the Proposed Denominational Logo

**Assembly:** 14th (1986)  ·  **Source:** Westminster Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Other

*Source: [ga14_1986 p. 45](../markdown/ga14_1986.md#ga14-p45)*

---

Be it resolved that the Fourteenth General Assembly not accept the Proposed Logo presented to the Thirteenth General Assembly. Adopted at the Winter Stated Meeting of Westminster Presbytery, January 18, 1986. Attest: Larry E. Ball, Stated Clerk *Answered in the affirmative as the logo was tabled, 14-74, III, 13, p. 171.*
