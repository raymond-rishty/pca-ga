# GA8 O30 — Direct MNA not to recruit from other denominations

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** Warrior Presbytery

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 44](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p44)*

---

Warrior Presbytery respectfully overtures the Eighth General Assembly to instruct the Committee on Mission to the United States to desist from inviting malcontents in other denominations to turn to the Presbyterian Church in America (as seems indicated by advertisements in such magazines as the April 4, 1980 Christianity Today. Volume 24, Number 7. Page 30) and return to the positive promotion of the Presbyterian Church in America in the cause of Christ Adopted at the April 15. 1980 Stated Meeting of Warrior Presbytery. Allan Story Recording Clerk Answered in the negative. § 8-50, III, 9; p. 86.
