“Commend Covenant College for Forfeiting Championship Tennis Match Scheduled on a Lord’s Day”

**Whereas** the Word of God clearly instructs men to observe the Sabbath Day, to keep it holy, and to set aside this one day out of seven as being consecrated to the Lord (Genesis 2:1-3; Exodus 20:8-11; Isaiah 58:13-14); ~~and~~ ~~**Whereas**~~ ~~the Sabbath Day has fallen into widespread profanation~~ ~~and disuse in American society today, and various athletic,~~ ~~commercial, and social enterprises and activities have all but~~ ~~obliterated our former national reverence for this holy day;~~
