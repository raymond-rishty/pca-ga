**Assembly:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 3 from Pee Dee Presbytery (to CCB, OC)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 53

*Source: [ga51_2024 p. 1069](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p1069)*
