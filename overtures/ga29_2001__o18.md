# GA29 O18 — Publish Corrected Hardback Edition of Westminster Standards

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 18 From Heritage Presbytery (to AC)  ·  **Disposition:** Other

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 255](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p255)*

---

“Publish Hardback Version and Newly Edited Versions of Westminster Standards”

Whereas, the Westminster Standard [sic] (hereafter called Standards) [Standards] are [part of] the Constitution of the Presbyterian Church in America (hereafter called the PCA); and

Whereas, the Standards [Standards] are the most complete and accurate of any doctrinal statement available to the church today; and

Whereas, the Standards [Standards] ought to be used widely without distraction; and

Whereas, the current published PCA version of the Standards [Standards], 1990, contain[s] numerous typographical errors, omissions and other mistakes both in the text and in the citations which are a distraction to those who use it; and

Whereas, this same published version of the Standard [sic] [Standards] is available mostly in a paper bound edition that does not remain intact with much serious use; Therefore, Let It Be Resolved, that the P C A [sic] is instructed [sic] to publish a corrected [sic] edition of the version of the Standards [Standards] in a durable, and reasonably priced edition. Approved by Heritage Presbytery on January 27, 2001. Attested by: /s/ Bruce Howes, Stated Clerk 5.
