# GA14 O10 — Change Oklahoma Presbytery Bounds to Include Kansas and Rename It Mid-America Presbytery

**Assembly:** 14th (1986)  ·  **Source:** Oklahoma Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Referred

*Source: [ga14_1986 p. 56](../markdown/ga14_1986.md#ga14-p56)*

---

On October 11, 1985, in its 9th Stated Meeting, Oklahoma Presbytery unanimously agreed to petition the 1986 General Assembly to change the boundary of Oklahoma Presbytery to include all of Kansas, and to change the name to Mid-America Presbytery. Attest: Robert E. Hays, Stated Clerk Referred to the proposed subcommittee on boundaries, 14-43, III, 20, p. 117.
