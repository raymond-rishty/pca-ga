# GA24 O16 — Amend BCO 46-5 by Adopting Alternative A

**Assembly:** 24th (1996)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 16 From Westminster Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference  ·  **BCO:** 46-5

*Source: [ga24_1996 p. 304](../markdown/ga24_1996.md#ga24-p304)*

---

“Amend BCO 46-5 by Substituting Alternative A”

Whereas, Westminster Presbytery has debated the “Items Sent Down to Presbyteries By The 23rd General Assembly for Consideration RE. BCO 46-5”,

Whereas, Westminster Presbytery has adopted Amendment A on page 222 and 223 of the minutes of the 23rd General Assembly, as representing a change in the Book of Church Order which would reflect our views on the matters of church membership, Therefore, Westminster Presbytery overtures the 24th General Assembly to adopt Amendment A on page 222 and 223 of the minutes of the 23rd General Assembly as a proposed amendment to the Book of Church Order. Adopted by Westminster Presbytery at the stated meeting on January 13,1996. Attested by: Is/ Larry E. Ball, Stated Ball
