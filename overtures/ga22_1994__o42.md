**Assembly:** 22nd (1994)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 42 From the Presbytery of Southern Florida  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga22_1994 p. 212](../markdown/ga22_1994.md#ga22-p212)*
