# GA24 O10 — Amend BCO 40 & 39-1 to Delete "and control"

**Assembly:** 24th (1996)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 10 From Heartland Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Approved → sent to presbyteries; ratification not located  ·  **BCO:** 40-3

*Source: [ga24_1996 p. 290](../markdown/ga24_1996.md#ga24-p290)*

---

> *That Overture 10 from Heartland Presbytery be answered in the negative. Adopted — the overture to amend BCO 40 to delete 'and control' was rejected at the 24th GA and never sent down to presbyteries.*

“Amend BCO 40 to Delete ‘and control.’”

Whereas, the committee on review of presbytery records (CRPR) has been charged with the responsibilities outlined in BCO, Chapter 40; and

Whereas, in recent years, the CRPR (with the approval of General Assembly) has interpreted this responsibility to review the minutes (Records) of each presbytery;

Therefore, be it resolved that Heartland Presbytery overture the 24th General Assembly (June 1996) to change the Book of Church Order to read as follows: A. Chapter 40 title: General Review B. Section 40-3: Strike the words “and control,” so that it reads, . . Proceedings in judicial case, however, shall not be dealt with under review when notice of appeal or com plaint.. C. Change Chapter 39-1, 1 by striking the words “and control” so that it reads: “ 1. Review” D. Make any additional editorial changes as may be needed in other sections of the BCO and RAO (Rules for Assembly Operation). Approved at the 21st stated meeting of heartland Presbytery, November 19, 1995. Attested by: Is/ Lawrence N. Lunceford, Stated Clerk 8. That Overture 22 from Western Carolina Presbytery be answered by reference to the Assembly's action on Personal Resolution 3 (1995) and Overture 10 (1995) [see under Recommendation 20] page 312. Adopted
