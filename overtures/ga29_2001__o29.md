# GA29 O29 — Direct MNA to Report Administrative Fees to General Assembly

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 29 From TE Andrew Lee of Northern California Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 173](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p173)*

“Direct MNA to Report Administrative Fees”

Whereas, in October 1997 the GA MNA Committee approved a policy: (a) to limit the maximum dollar amount (or percentage), of "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [project overhead/administrative fees] taken from funds raised for church planting projects; and (b) to disclose to select donors the existence and amounts of such costs as a percentage of donations; and

Whereas, prior to October 1997, i.e., without aforementioned Committee-approved policy, GA MNA Staff prepared and administered church planting project budgets that included "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [project overhead/administrative fees]; and

Whereas, GA MNA heretofore has not been a member of the Evangelical Council for Financial Accountability (ECFA; www.ecfa.org) as has another PCA Committee (Mission to the World) and PCA Agency (Covenant Theological Seminary) - organizations that are also involved in fundraising (Romans 12: 17b). (It should be noted that while GA MNA's financial records are audited annually by an independent firm, and all records are reviewed by normal General Assembly processes, ECFA membership is helpful both in terms of accountability and in increasing the confidence of donors); and
