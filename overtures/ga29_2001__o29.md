# GA29 O29 — Direct MNA to Report Administrative Fees to General Assembly

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 29 From TE Andrew Lee of Northern California Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 173](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p173)*

---

“Direct MNA to Report Administrative Fees”

Whereas, in October 1997 the GA MNA Committee approved a policy: (a) to limit the maximum dollar amount (or percentage), of "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [project overhead/administrative fees] taken from funds raised for church planting projects; and (b) to disclose to select donors the existence and amounts of such costs as a percentage of donations; and

Whereas, prior to October 1997, i.e., without aforementioned Committee-approved policy, GA MNA Staff prepared and administered church planting project budgets that included "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [project overhead/administrative fees]; and

Whereas, GA MNA heretofore has not been a member of the Evangelical Council for Financial Accountability (ECFA; www.ecfa.org) as has another PCA Committee (Mission to the World) and PCA Agency (Covenant Theological Seminary) - organizations that are also involved in fundraising (Romans 12: 17b). (It should be noted that while GA MNA's financial records are audited annually by an independent firm, and all records are reviewed by normal General Assembly processes, ECFA membership is helpful both in terms of accountability and in increasing the confidence of donors); and

Whereas, GA MNA as a Committee of the PCA is accountable to the General Assembly; Therefore, Be It Resolved that the General Assembly have GA MNA report to the General Assembly on the following: 1. Written policy and limits that GA MNA Staff used to determine "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [and/or similar overhead/administrative fees] in preparing and administering church planting project funds in the absence of the October 1997 Committee-approved policy; 2. Written policy and practices GA MNA Staff followed to disclose "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [and/or similar overhead/ administrative fees] to donors and church planters in the absence of the October 1997 Committee-approved policy; 3. "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [and/or similar overhead/administrative fees] in: (a) dollar amounts; (b) as a percentage of; donations (cf. guidelines in the October 1997 Committee policy); (c) timing as to when such fees were taken from church plant funds with respect to project's budget plan; and (d) the implicit cost of capital that "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [and/or similar overhead/administrative fees] imputed to each project when netted against funding covenants. Information for items 3(a) through 3(d) to be provided for all GA MNA project budgets not covered by the October 1997 Committee policy beginning from January 1990 through implementation of said Committee policy; 4. Recommended actions, if any, that should be taken for all projects in item #3 above if it is determined that "Field Assistance and Project Development Costs" [and/or similar overhead/administrative fees] were (a) lower relative to the guidelines set by the Committee's October 1997 policy; or (b) higher relative to the guidelines set by the Committee's October 1997 policy; and 5. GA MNA's current position regarding its membership in the ECFA. NOTE: This overture was presented to, and rejected by, Northern California Presbytery at the Spring 2000 Stated Meeting. Overture requested by TE Andrew Lee. Rejection recorded in meeting's minutes approved by Presbytery at the Fall 2000 Stated Meeting. Attested by: /s/ TE Andrew Lee
