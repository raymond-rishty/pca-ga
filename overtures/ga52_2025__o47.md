# GA52 O47 — Erect Ad Interim Committee on Christian Nationalism

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 47 from the Great Lakes Presbytery (to OC, AC)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1300](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1300)*

---

“Erect Ad Interim Committee on Christian Nationalism **Whereas**, there is a question whether those that hold to the Historic _WCF_ (1647) concerning the magistrate ( _WCF_ 23-3) need to take an exception because of differences from the version of the _WCF_ adopted by the PCA (1788); and **Whereas**, some in the PCA have labeled the historic _WCF_ ’s views concerning the magistrate as a form of Christian Nationalism; and **Whereas**, some also hold to a version of Christian Nationalism that they describe as Ethno-Nationalism, or label themselves as Heritage Americans; and **Whereas**, those that hold these teachings profess conformity to the Reformed Tradition; and **Whereas**, these have caused confusion, division, and dissension even among the congregants of PCA churches and affected PCA pastors and officers; and **Whereas**, church courts have the responsibility to determine questions of doctrine seriously, and in general to maintain truth and righteousness, condemning erroneous opinions which tend to bring injury to the eace, purity, or progress of the Church ( _BCO_ 11-4); **Therefore, be it resolved** that the 52 [nd] PCA General Assembly erect and convene an Ad Interim Study Committee on Christian Nationalism ( _RAO_ 9-1), to study the relationship between Christian Nationalism, Ethno-Nationalism, and related teachings.
