# GA52 O47 — Erect Ad Interim Committee on Christian Nationalism

**Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 47 from the Great Lakes Presbytery (to OC, AC)  ·  **Disposition:** Adopted (final)

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 1300](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p1300)*

---

“Erect Ad Interim Committee on Christian Nationalism

**Whereas**, there is a question whether those that hold to the Historic _WCF_ (1647) concerning the magistrate ( _WCF_ 23-3) need to take an exception because of differences from the version of the _WCF_ adopted by the PCA (1788); and

**Whereas**, some in the PCA have labeled the historic _WCF_ ’s views concerning the magistrate as a form of Christian Nationalism; and

**Whereas**, some also hold to a version of Christian Nationalism that they describe as Ethno-Nationalism, or label themselves as Heritage Americans; and

**Whereas**, those that hold these teachings profess conformity to the Reformed Tradition; and

**Whereas**, these have caused confusion, division, and dissension even among the congregants of PCA churches and affected PCA pastors and officers; and

**Whereas**, church courts have the responsibility to determine questions of doctrine seriously, and in general to maintain truth and righteousness, condemning erroneous opinions which tend to bring injury to the eace, purity, or progress of the Church ( _BCO_ 11-4);

**Therefore, be it resolved** that the 52 [nd] PCA General Assembly erect and convene an Ad Interim Study Committee on Christian Nationalism ( _RAO_ 9-1), to study the relationship between Christian Nationalism, Ethno-Nationalism, and related teachings. Further, the committee shall advise on whether these teachings and formulations are in conformity with the system of doctrine taught in the _Westminster_ _Standards_ or where they may diverge from the system of doctrine. **Further**, the committee shall write a report that gives pastoral guidance when addressing congregations, new members, and future officers of the PCA.

**Be it further resolved** that the General Assembly’s Moderator be empowered as a commission to populate the ad interim committee with three (3) Teaching Elders and four (4) Ruling Elders.

**Be it further resolved** that the budget for the study committee be set at $10,000/year and that funds be derived from gifts to the AC designated for that purpose, with the General Assembly appealing to the various Presbyteries to fund this initiative via such gifts ( _RAO_ 9-3).

**Be it further resolved** that the Great Lakes Presbytery is committed to offer a $1,000 gift to assist in funding the work of this Ad Interim Study Committee. _Adopted by the Great Lakes Presbytery at its stated meeting, May 3, 2025_ _Attested by TE Ryan Potter, stated clerk_
