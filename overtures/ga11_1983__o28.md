# GA11 O28 — Submit Future BCO Revisions Individually Rather Than in Groups

**Assembly:** 11th (1983)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of Central Florida  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative

*Source: [ga11_1983 p. 48](../markdown/ga11_1983.md#ga11-p48)*

---

Whereas, the submittal by General Assembly of amendments to the Book of Church Order to the presbyteries for action, need to be considered on their own merit, and Whereas, the grouping of two or more paragraphs from different chapters tends to cause ambiguity and confusion. Therefore, the Presbytery of Central Florida overtures the Eleventh General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in America, in the interest of both simplicity and clarity, to submit future revisions to the Book of Church Order individually, not topically or in groups. Attest: A. CARLTON HEIL, STATED CLERK Answered in the negative §11-36, III, 56, p. 101.
