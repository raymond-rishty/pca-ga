# GA40 O40 — Expand Southeast Louisiana Presbytery Upon Dissolution of Louisiana Presbytery

**Assembly:** 40th (2012)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 40 from Southeast Louisiana Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference

*Source: [ga40_2012 p. 747](../markdown/ga40_2012.md#ga40-p747)*

---
