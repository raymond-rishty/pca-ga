# GA6 O7 — Study the Relationship Between Dispensationalism and Covenant Theology

**Assembly:** 6th (1978)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of North Georgia  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference

*Source: [ga06_1978 p. 32](../markdown/ga06_1978.md#ga06-p32)*

---

The North Georgia Presbytery respectfully overtures the General Assembly to set up an Ad Interim Committee for the purpose of studying the relationship between dispensationalism and covenant theology. Adopted by North Georgia Presbytery at its stated meeting, January 21, 1978. Attest: Robert G. Valentine Stated Clerk Answered by reference to the Committee for Christian Education, §6-63, II. A. p. 80.
