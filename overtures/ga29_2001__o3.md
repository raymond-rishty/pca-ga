# GA29 O3 — Amend BCO 20-2 to Include Senior Pastor on Associate Search Committee

**Assembly:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 3 from Susquehanna Valley Presbytery (to CCB &amp; B&amp;O)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered in the negative  ·  **BCO:** 20-2

*Source: [ga29_2001 p. 192](../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p192)*

---

“Amend BCO 20-2 to Include Pastor in Search Committee for Assistant or Associate Pastor” Whereas, the *Book of Church Order* does not address the matter of searching for an assistant or associate pastor, and Whereas, for the peace and unity of the church it is vital that there be harmony among the pastoral staff of a church, and Whereas, any new assistant or associate pastor must be agreeable to and compatible with the senior pastor, Therefore be it resolved that the following be added to the BCO 20-2: “When a search committee for an associate pastor is formed by a congregation, the senior pastor shall automatically be a member of the committee. Before proposing a candidate to the congregation, the committee shall assure itself that the candidate is acceptable to and has the full support of the senior pastor. “In the case of a call to an assistant pastor, Session may act as the search committee or it may appoint church members as a search committee to bring a candidate for Session approval. In either case, the senior pastor shall be a member of the search committee.” Adopted by the Susquehanna Valley Presbytery on September 16, 2000. Attested by: D. F. Rundle, Stated Clerk 4. That Overture 4 be answered in the negative. Adopted as amended
