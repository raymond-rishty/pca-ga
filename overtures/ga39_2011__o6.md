# GA39 O6 — Concur with Central Carolina Presbytery Request to Form a New Presbytery

**Assembly:** 39th (2011)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 6 from Western Carolina Presbytery (to MNA)  ·  **Disposition:** Answered by reference

*Source: [ga39_2011 p. 621](../markdown/ga39_2011.md#ga39-p621)*

---

“Concur with Central Carolina Presbytery Request to Form a New Presbytery”

**Whereas,** Central Carolina Presbytery has overtured the 39 [th] General Assembly to form a new presbytery that would include Alexander and Catawba counties, which counties are currently within the boundaries of Western Carolina Presbytery; and

**Whereas,** Western Carolina Presbytery concurs with Central Carolina Presbytery in this regard;

**Therefore, be it resolved** that Western Carolina Presbytery hereby overtures the 39 [th] General Assembly to include Alexander and Catawba counties within the boundaries of such a new presbytery, effective July 1, 2011. _Adopted by the Western Carolina Presbytery at its stated meeting, February 26, 2011_ _Attested by /s/ TE Skip Gillikin, stated clerk_
