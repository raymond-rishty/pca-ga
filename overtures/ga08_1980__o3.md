# GA8 O3 — Amend BCO 25-6 through 25-9 via BCO 27-2

**Assembly:** 8th (1980)  ·  **Source:** the Presbytery of Southern Florida  ·  **Disposition:** Approved → sent to presbyteries; ratification not located  ·  **BCO:** 25-6, 27-2

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 39](../markdown/ga08_1980.md#ga08-p39)*

---

> *Answered in the negative. § 8-88, III, 12; p. 118. (ga08_1980.md, line 2085) — confirmed: '12. That Overture 3 be answered in the negative based upon previous actions of the General Assembly.' (line 6829); proposal never sent to presbyteries.*

Whereas, it appears that the First General Assembly, when formulating the current wording of paragraphs 25-6 through 25-9 of the Book of Church Order, intended to allow, though not to recommend, limited (rotational) terms of active service for Ruling Elders and Deacons (see Recommendation No. 2, Report of Permanent Sub-Committee on Judicial Business to the Sixth General Assembly, page 188, Minutes of the Sixth General Assembly), and

Whereas, many individuals within our denomination, in particular churches both using and not using limited terms, doubt or question whether the current wording of the Book of Church Order does in fact allow limited terms of active service for Ruling Elders and Deacons as it appears was intended by the First General Assembly, and

Whereas, many of our particular churches are using limited terms of active service for their Ruling Elders and/or Deacons, and strongly desire to continue to do so, and

Whereas, the particular churches using limited terms of active service also desire that there be no doubt or questioning as to whether the procedure they a reusing is allowed by the Book of Church Order, and

Whereas, the widespread use within our denomination of a procedure, the allowableness of which under the Book of Church Order is extensively doubted and questioned, erodes the authority of and respect for the Book of Church Order an dis inimical to effective church government. Therefore, the Presbytery of Southern Florida hereby overtures the Eighth (1980) General Assembly to initiate action under the provisions of paragraph 27-2 of the Book of Church
