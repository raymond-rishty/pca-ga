# GA20 O2 — Print RAO on Looseleaf Paper Compatible with BCO Binder

**Assembly:** 20th (1992)  ·  **Source:** OVERTURE 2 From Missouri Presbytery

*Source: [ga20_1992 p. 132](../markdown/ga20_1992.md#ga20-p132)*

---

"Print RAO to Fit in looseleaf BCO"

Whereas, the Rules of Assembly Operation (RAO) is about 25 pages long and is printed for every commissioner every year, resulting in large printing costs and a waste of paper, Therefore Be It Resolved that the General Assembly print the RAO on paper that will fit into the looseleaf BCO, so they can be saved and used every year, and updated as necessary in the same manner that the BCO is updated. Adopted by Missouri Presbytery, at its Spring Meeting, on the 17th day of April 1991. Attested by: /s/ Albert F. Moginot, Jr. Stated Clerk [Received too late for the 19th GA.]
