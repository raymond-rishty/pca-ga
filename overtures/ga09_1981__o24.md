# GA9 O24 — Amend BCO 11-3 to Allow Presbyteries to Elect a Moderator for One Year

**Assembly:** 9th (1981)  ·  **Source:** Pacific Presbytery  ·  **Disposition:** Referred  ·  **BCO:** 11-3

*Source: [ga09_1981 p. 49](../markdown/ga09_1981.md#ga09-p49)*

---

Whereas, it was moved, seconded, and carried to overture the 1981 General Assembly to amend the Book of Church Order, Chapter 11-3 so that the second sentence will read: "The Moderator of the Presbytery and of the General Assembly shall be chosen at each stated meeting of these courts, except that presbyteries may, if they desire, elect a Moderator to serve for a term of one year. The above overture was approved at the meeting of Pacific Presbytery on September 25, 1980. Attest: William F. Woodhall, Stated Clerk Pacific Presbytery Received too late for consideration by the Ninth General Assembly, carried over to the Tenth General Assembly. §9-65, III, C, 14, p. 122
