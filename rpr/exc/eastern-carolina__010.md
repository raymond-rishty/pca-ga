## Raised — 25th General Assembly (1997)
*[ga25_1997 p.204](../../markdown/ga25_1997.md#ga25-p204)*

Exception: April 20, 1996: Presbytery approved mission church elders’ relaxed view of the Sabbath as “within the bounds of current acceptable PCA interpretation and practice.” This statement appears to imply that this view is in conformity with WCF 21.8., rather than an allowable exception.
