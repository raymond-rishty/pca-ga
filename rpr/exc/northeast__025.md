## Response found satisfactory — 26th General Assembly (1998)
*[ga26_1998 p.247](../../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p247)*

Exception: June 15, 1996: Presbytery received into its teaching
membership a TE who was unwilling to use the term “evangelical church”
in fencing the table. (BCO 58-4).
Response: Presbytery agrees with the Exception and promises to be more
careful in the future. The following Corrective Action was taken by
Presbytery: Corrective Action: The Teaching Elder who took exception to
using the term “evangelical church” in fencing the table withdrew his
exception at the Feb. 11 & 12, 1998 Presbytery Meeting having come to a
better understanding of BCO 58-4. It was then moved, seconded and
passed by the presbytery of northern california that the entire paragraph
6A (2)(c) on page 3 of the presbytery minutes of june 15, 1996, which
gives the Teaching Elder’s exception, be rescinded.
