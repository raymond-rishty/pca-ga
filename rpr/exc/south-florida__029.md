## Raised — 44th General Assembly (2016)
*[ga44_2016 p.443](../../markdown/ga44_2016.md#ga44-p443)*

**Exception: August 11, 2015** ( _BCO_ 15-2) – Commission established
with only one RE. **Exception: August 11, 2015** ( _BCO_ 13-11) –
Complaint sent to Presbytery not recorded in minutes (“full and
accurate record”) see also _RAO_ 16-3.e.7.

## Response found satisfactory — 45th General Assembly (2017)
*[ga45_2017 p.415](../../markdown/ga45_2017.md#ga45-p415)*

**Exception: August 11, 2015** ( _BCO_ 15-2) – Commission established
with only one RE.
**Response:** We agree that the commission was formed improperly,
having only one RE but requiring two REs. The Commission
completed its task, and as the Chair of the MCRC stated, “Mea culpa.”
We regret this oversight and promise to be careful in the future by
forming all commissions properly.
