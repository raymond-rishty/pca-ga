## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.658](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p658)*

**2024-03: Nov 21, 2023** ( _BCO_ 21-4; RAO16-3.e.5) —
Incomplete record of ordination exam. No record of candidate
being examined thoroughly in Bible, Theology, and Church
Government. no record of examination in languages. No
record of exegetical and theology papers.

**Response:** We disagree with this exception in part.
The candidate was examined in Bible (designated in the
minutes as Bible Knowledge), Theology (designated in the
minutes as Bible Doctrine), and Church Government as part
of his licensure exam recorded in the September 19, 2023,
stated meeting minutes. CMP failed to examine the candidate
in his knowledge of Greek and Hebrew, or on his theological
and exegetical papers, as required by _BCO_ 21-4c. We
apologize for this oversight. As part of Ohio Valley

Presbytery since July 2024, a complete exam always will be
performed in the future.
