**Response:** this concerns avery sensitive issue regarding a teaching elder
who was being investigated by a committee of the presbytery. He met with
the investigative committee but refused to appear before the presbytery. The
committee with the agreement of the teaching elder involved recommended
his deposition from ministry. The presbytery approved the committee’s
recommendation to depose him. There was no formal indictment or trial
because the teaching elder did not want to appear before the presbytery.
