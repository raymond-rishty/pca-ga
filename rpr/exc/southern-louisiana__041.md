**Response:** The Southern Louisiana Presbytery
acknowledges the exception of substance cited by the
Committee on Review of Presbytery Records (RPR) for
General 2023, noting that the presbytery's minutes for 2023
do not include annual reports from candidates under care, as
required by _BCO_ 18-6.
To address this exception, the presbytery provides the
following:
1. Circumstances of the 2023 Omission: The

presbytery currently has two candidates under care: Candidate

[Name Omitted] and Candidate [Name Omitted]. Upon
review by consultation with the chair of the Candidates
Committee these candidates submitted annual reports to the
Candidates Committee throughout the year and the
Candidates committee reviews those reports and reports on
their review at the October presbytery meeting. These actions
were not recorded in our minutes due to a clerical oversight
by our Clerk.
2. Corrective Action: the commission of presbytery
assigned to deal with the exceptions of substance have

amended our minutes to reflect this report delivered at our
October 2023 stated Presbytery meeting.
The SOLA Presbytery affirms its commitment to
overseeing candidates under care per _BCO_ 18-6 and
respectfully requests that the General Assembly find this
response satisfactory. We are prepared to provide copies of
the 2023 candidate reports or additional documentation if
needed.
