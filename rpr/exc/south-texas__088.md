**Response:** The Ministerial Relations Committee
under the Presbytery Standing Rules adopted by the full
Presbytery "has the authority to act as a commission . . . in
dissolving pastoral relationships . . . where the minister and
congregation formally concurn in such dissolution
and...pastoral problems are not involved." The Presbytery has
received minutes of the electronic votes taken by the
committee, as is permitted under the Standing Rules adopted
by the full Presbytery, on May 10 and June 23, 2023 with
respect to Teaching Elders [Name Omitted] and [Name
Omitted]. The minutes of the Presbytery Stated Meeting on
August 12 have been corrected to note these minutes and the
Stated Clerk authorized to make a notation of this correction.

No separate minutes or the August 11 committee meeting and
the action with respect to Teaching Elder [Name Omitted]
were kept and the committee report of its action relled on the
provision of the Standing Rules. (There is no record in the
minutes of the absence of a quorum for this committee
meeting.)
