# Northeast Presbytery — BCO 20-1

*Presbytery approved a call that was not a proper call in that the call…*

**Presbytery:** Northeast  ·  **First raised:** 25th (1997)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 20-1

**Lifecycle:** raised (25th) → satisfactory (26th)

---

## Raised — 25th General Assembly (1997)
*[ga25_1997 p.212](../../markdown/ga25_1997.md#ga25-p212)*

Exception: January 12-13, 1996: Presbytery approved a call that was not a proper call in that the call promised to provide financial care of the teaching elder yet also required the teaching elder to raise his financial support (BCO 20-1 and 20-6.)

## Response found satisfactory — 26th General Assembly (1998)
*[ga26_1998 p.246](../../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p246)*

Exception: January 12-13, 1996: Presbytery approved a call that was not
a proper call in that the call promised to provide financial care of the
teaching elder yet also required the teaching elder to raise his financial
support (BCO 20-1 and 20-6).

Response: Northeast Presbytery agrees with General Assembly’s
exception and promises to be more circumspect in the future.
