# Korean Southern Presbytery — BCO 13-12

*Presbytery did not meet at least twice per year BCO…*

**Presbytery:** Korean Southern  ·  **First raised:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 13-12

**Lifecycle:** raised (28th) → raised (42nd) → satisfactory (43rd)

## Raised — 28th General Assembly (2000)
*[ga28_2000 p.327](../../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p327)*

Exception: General: Presbytery did not meet at least twice per year
BCO 13-12.

## Raised — 42nd General Assembly (2014)
*[ga42_2014 p.422](../../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p422)*

**Exception: General** ( _BCO_ 13-12) – No record of two presbytery
meetings.

## Response found satisfactory — 43rd General Assembly (2015)
*[ga43_2015 p.451](../../markdown/ga43_2015.md#ga43-p451)*

**Exception: General** ( _BCO_ 13-12) – No record of two presbytery
meetings.
**Response:** Korean Southern Presbytery usually has two meetings
annually in April and in October on the third Monday. So the
Presbytery submits its minutes of April and of October annually.
Therefore, the submission of two minutes naturally covers the
second half part of the two-year before the GA and the first part of
the year of the last GA.
