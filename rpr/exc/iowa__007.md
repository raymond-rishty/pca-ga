## Response found satisfactory — 43rd General Assembly (2015)
*[ga43_2015 p.444](../../markdown/ga43_2015.md#ga43-p444)*

**Exception:** **2012** **General** ( _BCO_ 8-7) – No record in the minutes of
any stated meetings of an annual report from TEs working out of
bounds

**Response:** Oral reports were given periodically for several of the
teaching elders who were laboring out of bounds in 2012.
Regrettably, however, Iowa did not always record these reports in
our minutes or maintain records to make sure a report was received
from each in a given year. Iowa Presbytery will be taking steps to
assign this responsibility to a committee to ensure better reporting in
the future.
