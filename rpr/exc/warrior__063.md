## Response found satisfactory — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.629](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p629)*

**Exception: Apr 16, 2019** ( _BCO_ 23-1; 22-4) – Presbytery approved
the dissolution of pastoral relations with no record of congregational
meeting or any record of the church commissioners appearing before
Presbytery.

**Response:** Warrior Presbytery acknowledges our failure to accurately
preserve the court record. The congregation held a meeting on March
3, 2019 and voted unanimously to dissolve the pastoral relationship.
This notice was sent to our membership committee who interviewed
the pastor and approved the dissolution. Representatives from the
church were present at Presbytery. We will endeavor to keep a more
accurate record of court proceedings.
