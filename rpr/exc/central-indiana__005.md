# Central Indiana Presbytery — BCO 19-2.d

*During licensure and ordination exams Presbytery accepted audio file for…*

**Presbytery:** Central Indiana  ·  **First raised:** 46th (2018)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 19-2.d

**Lifecycle:** raised (46th) → satisfactory (47th)

---

## Raised — 46th General Assembly (2018)
*[ga46_2018 p.379](../../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p379)*

**Exception: February 10, 2017; May 12, 2017; and May 26, 2017**
( _BCO_ 19-2.d; 21-4.c.4) – During licensure and ordination exams
Presbytery accepted audio file for sermon requirement instead of
preaching in front of committee or Presbytery.

## Response found satisfactory — 47th General Assembly (2019)
*[ga47_2019 p.458](../../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p458)*

**Exception: Feb 10, 2017; May 12, 2017; and May 26, 2017** ( _BCO_
19-2.d; 21-4.c.4) – During licensure and ordination exams Presbytery
accepted audio file for sermon requirement instead of preaching in
front of committee or Presbytery.

**Response:** We humbly apologize for our failure to comply with the
Book of Church order in this manner. For future licensure and

ordination exams, the sermons will be preached in front of either the
Presbytery or a committee of the Presbytery.
