## Response found satisfactory — 46th General Assembly (2018)

**Exception: August 2, 2016** ( _BCO_ 23-1) – no record o fa
congregational meeting in the dissolution of a pastoral relationship.
**Response:** Suncoast Florida Presbytery recognizes that the record of
a congregational meeting to dissolve the particular pastoral
relationship was not noted in the minutes. The congregational
minutes were received but the action was not noted. We apologize
for the oversight and will seek to be more diligent in the future
regarding such records. (Approved 02/16/18-SFP)
