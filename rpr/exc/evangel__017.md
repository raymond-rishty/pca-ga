# Evangel Presbytery — BCO 8-7

*Item 19.C &amp; 19.D: Teaching Elders were approved to labor out of bounds…*

**Presbytery:** Evangel  ·  **First raised:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 8-7

**Lifecycle:** raised (29th) → satisfactory (30th)

---

## Raised — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.327](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p327)*

Exception: January 25, 2000: Item 19.C &amp; 19.D: Teaching Elders were approved to labor out of bounds without following BCO 8-7 and 13-2.

## Response found satisfactory — 30th General Assembly (2002)
*[ga30_2002 p.309](../../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p309)*

Exception: January 25, 2000: Item 19.C & 19.D:
Teaching Elders were approved to labor out of bounds
without following BCO 8-7 and 13-2.

Response: We acknowledge that we failed to follow
the BCO 8-7 and 13-2 in approving Teaching Elders to
labor outside the bounds of Presbytery. Since being
made aware of our error we will be careful to follow the
proper procedures as outlined in BCO 8-7 and 13-2.
Adopted
