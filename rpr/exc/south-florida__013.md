## Response found satisfactory — 44th General Assembly (2016)
*[ga44_2016 p.444](../../markdown/ga44_2016.md#ga44-p444)*

**Exception: General** ( _BCO_ 15-1) – No commission reports/minutes.

**Response:** There were five ordination and installation commissions,
one judicial commission, one commission for the transfer of a TE
into the Presbytery, and one commission for a provisional session.
None of the commission reports were included in the Minutes. We
included an addendum to the Minutes of the proper months with
some of these reports of the commissions. Our Presbytery has not
been diligent in requiring the commissions to submit reports. We
regret this omission and this error and promise to be careful to
include all reports for all commissions in the future.
