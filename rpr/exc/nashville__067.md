## Raised — 52nd General Assembly (2025)

**2025-01: Feb 13, 2024** ( _BCO_ 38) — Presbytery judged the
Episcopal Church as another branch of the visible church –
which faithfully maintains the Word and Sacraments – by
utilizing _BCO_ 38-3.a instead of utilizing _BCO_ 38-3.b and
thereby judge this body as “failing to maintain the Word and
Sacraments in their fundamental integrity.”.
