## Raised — 43rd General Assembly (2015)
*[ga43_2015 p.466](../../markdown/ga43_2015.md#ga43-p466)*

**Exception: March 15, 2014 and November 15, 2014** ( _BCO_ 13-7) –
Ministerial obligation not shown to be signed by transferees.

## Response found satisfactory — 44th General Assembly (2016)
*[ga44_2016 p.438](../../markdown/ga44_2016.md#ga44-p438)*

**Exception: March 15, 2014 and November 15, 2014** ( _BCO_ 13-7) –
Ministerial obligation not shown to be signed by transferees.

**Response:** The Potomac Presbytery respectfully disagrees with the
stated exception. The Potomac Presbytery agrees that we did not
record the signing of the ministerial obligation for [name omitted] in
the March 2014 meeting. As has been our practice for over 20 years,
and never challenged in reviews, the receipt of [name omitted]'s
ministerial obligation form was recorded in the stated clerk's report
for the next stated meeting which was held in September 2014
(Please see Page 2 of Appendix 3 to the Minutes of the 109 [th] (March
15, 2014) stated meeting). While we usually have a transfer sign the
ministerial obligation at the meeting in which he was received, we
can find nothing in the _BCO_ (specifically _BCO_ 13-7) that says how
and when the signing of the ministerial obligation is recorded.
