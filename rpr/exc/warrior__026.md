## Response found satisfactory — 34th General Assembly (2006)
*[ga34_2006 p.302](../../markdown/ga34_2006.md#ga34-p302)*

**Exception: October 15, 2002:** No record of completion of academic
requirements. _BCO_ 21-4 and RAO 14-3.e.5

**Response:** Under Section 27878 “Membership Committee” the
record does refer to the accepting of the candidate’s seminary degree
in lieu of an exam in the original languages. Therefore, that academic

requirement was recognized in the exam. However, as the minutes do
not specifically state that the candidate presented presbytery with a
record of his college and seminary degrees, we stand corrected for not
specifying this requirement. The Membership Committee of Warrior
Presbytery shall be requested to clearly state before presbytery that a
candidate for ordination has provided such record of academic
accomplishment in all future examinations for ordination in this
presbytery. We shall be sure to record such in the minutes of our
proceedings.
