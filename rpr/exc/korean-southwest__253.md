## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)

**2023-15: Sep 13, 2022** ( _BCO_ 34-10) — No record of process
to remove TE’s from the roll.

**Response:** Detail procedure according to _BCO_ were
included in presbytery meeting dockets. But we have erred to
include records in meeting minutes.

**Rationale:** No record that Presbytery corrected its
record ( _RAO_ 16-10.b.1). Presbytery has not submitted the
record for review.

**Response:** In accordance with _BCO_ 38-2, each TE
voluntarily requested removal from roll and at the following
stated presbytery meeting, the request was concurred by the
presbytery. Moreover, with regard to _BCO_ 46-8, presbytery
was unable at the time to assign the TEs to a particular church
following their divestiture. The former TEs have since moved
to different states. We will in the future be more careful to

assign them to particular churches.
