# Northeast Presbytery — BCO 15-1

*Presbytery erred in allowing commissions to take up matters at their own…*

**Presbytery:** Northeast  ·  **First raised:** 28th (2000)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 15-1

**Lifecycle:** satisfactory (28th)

---

## Response found satisfactory — 28th General Assembly (2000)
*[ga28_2000 p.337](../../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p337)*

Exception: November 21, 1998: Presbytery erred in allowing commissions to take up matters at their own discretion. *BCO* 15-1.

Response: RECOMMEND that the Presbytery rescind the action of the November, 1998 Pro re nata meeting and that the regional committees be instructed that they no longer have the authority to act as regional commissions except as specifically authorized by Presbytery for a particular matter.
RECOMMEND that Presbytery direct the Stated Clerk to communicate this action to the General Assembly and to thank the Assembly for their oversight in this matter.

Adopted
