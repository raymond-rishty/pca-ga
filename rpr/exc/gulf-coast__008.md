## Raised — 30th General Assembly (2002)

Exception: May 8, 2001: Presbytery approved two
candidates with exceptions to WCF 24-3 in which they
stated, “the WCF has gone too far in the section on
marriage where in Chapter 24-3, it lumps Roman
Catholics, infidels, and other idolaters together.” BCO
26-1; 24-5
