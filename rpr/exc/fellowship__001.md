## Raised — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.327](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p327)*

Exception: July 20, 2000: Minister examined for transfer with no record of previous ecclesiastical connection. BCO 13-6.

## Response found satisfactory — 30th General Assembly (2002)
*[ga30_2002 p.310](../../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p310)*

Exception: July 20, 2000: Minister examined for
transfer with no record of previous ecclesiastical
connection. BCO 13-6.

Response: Presbytery failed to request transfer of
credentials in a timely manner. The Stated Clerk
spoke by phone to the Stated Clerk of the dismissing
Presbytery and sent a written request for the transfer.
Presbytery failed to enter these in the minutes.
Presbytery will seek to be more diligent in the future
to do these things in a timely manner and to enter them
in the minutes. Adopted
