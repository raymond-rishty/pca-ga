# Metropolitan New York Presbytery — BCO 21-4.c.4

*It appears that the candidate preached before a committee and not before…*

**Presbytery:** Metropolitan New York  ·  **First raised:** 40th (2012)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 21-4.c.4

**Lifecycle:** raised (40th) → satisfactory (41st)

## Raised — 40th General Assembly (2012)
*[ga40_2012 p.441](../../markdown/ga40_2012.md#ga40-p441)*

**Exception: May 14, 2011** ( _BCO_ 21-4.c.4) It appears that the
candidate preached before a committee and not before presbytery
without there being a require ¾ vote to have sermon heard by a
committee.

## Response found satisfactory — 41st General Assembly (2013)
*[ga41_2013 p.445](../../markdown/ga41_2013.md#ga41-p445)*

**Exception: May 14, 2011** ( _BCO_ 21-4.c.4) – It appears that the
candidate preached before a committee and not before Presbytery
without there being a required ¾ vote to have sermon heard by a
committee.

**Response:** Presbytery agrees and regrets that the motion to divide,
which was approved without objection, was not so noted in the
minutes.
