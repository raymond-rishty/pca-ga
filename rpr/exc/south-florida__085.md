## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)

**2024-10: Aug 8, 2023** ( _BCO_ 19-2; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) — All
specific requirements of licensure exam not recorded. No
record of written sermon.

**Response:** Presbytery agrees with the exception and
regrets the error. Each element of the licensure exam was
conducted by the Credentialing Committee, including the
submission of a written sermon, but each element was
unfortunately not recorded in the minutes. Presbytery has
corrected the record by a motion to Amend Something

Previously Adopted and has made a marginal notation in those
minutes pointing to this response. Presbytery will be more
diligent to record all examination requirements in the future.
