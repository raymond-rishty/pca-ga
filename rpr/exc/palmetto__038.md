# Palmetto Presbytery — BCO 23-1

*Pastoral relationship dissolved without citing local church to…*

**Lifecycle:** raised (48th) → satisfactory (49th)

## Raised — 48th General Assembly (2021)
*[ga48_2021 p.598](../../markdown/ga48_2021.md#ga48-p598)*

**Exception: Feb 14, 2019** ( _BCO_ 23-1) – Pastoral relationship
dissolved without citing local church to appear.

## Response found satisfactory — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.603](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p603)*

**Exception: Feb 14, 2019** ( _BCO_ 23-1) – Pastoral relationship
dissolved without citing local church to appear.
**Response:** In every case in which a pastoral relationship was
dissolved, there was both a request from the pastor and minutes of the
congregational meeting in which the congregation voted to approve
the pastor’s resignation. The Presbytery will require that a ruling
elder representative be present in future cases where a pastoral
relationship is dissolved, and both the presence of the elder and the
minutes of the congregation will be specified in the minutes of
Presbytery
