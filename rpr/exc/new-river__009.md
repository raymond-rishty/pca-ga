# New River Presbytery — WCF 21-7

*Man licensed who “holds exception in his view of the sabbath (he does not…*

**Presbytery:** New River  ·  **First raised:** 25th (1997)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** WCF 21-7

**Lifecycle:** raised (25th) → satisfactory (26th)

---

## Raised — 25th General Assembly (1997)
*[ga25_1997 p.211](../../markdown/ga25_1997.md#ga25-p211)*

Exception: July 12-13, 1996: Man licensed who “holds exception in his view of the Sabbath (he does not believe that the Sabbath Day and Lord’s Day are the same), the law (he is not absolutely sure that the Decalogue is the New Testament Ethic), and on who may administer the Sacraments (he cannot see that the administration of the sacraments is the sole prerogative of teaching elders; he believes that ruling elders and others who have demonstrated knowledge, understanding, and maturity concerning the sacraments should be allowed to administer them.)” WCF 21-7, 27-4, 19-2)

## Response found satisfactory — 26th General Assembly (1998)
*[ga26_1998 p.245](../../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p245)*

Exception: July 12-13, 1996: Man licensed who “holds exception in his
view of the sabbath (he does not believe that the Sabbath Day and Lord’s
Day are the same), the law (he is not absolutely sure that the Decalogue is
the New Testament Ethic), and who may administer the Sacraments (he
cannot see that the administration of the sacraments is the sole prerogative
of teaching elders, he believes that ruling elders and others who have
demonstrated knowledge, understanding, and maturity concerning the
sacraments should be allowed to administer them.)” (WCF 21-7, 27-4, 19-
2 ).

Response: Presbytery is currently in process of dealing with the licentiate
and will respond to the 27th General Assembly.
