## Response found satisfactory — 25th General Assembly (1997)
*[ga25_1997 p.215](../../markdown/ga25_1997.md#ga25-p215)*

Exception: April 29, 1995: Record is not clear if a candidate was examined.

Response: Pittsburgh Presbytery agrees that the presbytery itself must examine ordination candidates on the floor of presbytery, and in fact did so in this case in both theology and polity. However, the presbytery acknowledges that the wording of the minutes is imprecise, and in each case should have more correctly stated ‘that the theology/polity exam, led before presbytery by a member of the Credentials Committee, be approved.’ The minutes will be corrected, and presbytery will take greater care to be precise in recording future ordination exams.”

Adopted
