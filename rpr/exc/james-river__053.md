# James River Presbytery — BCO 5-9.d

*It is unclear whether officers were elected for a church petitioning for…*

**Presbytery:** James River  ·  **First raised:** 48th (2021)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 5-9.d

**Lifecycle:** raised (48th) → satisfactory (49th)

---

## Raised — 48th General Assembly (2021)
*[ga48_2021 p.565](../../markdown/ga48_2021.md#ga48-p565)*

**Exception: Oct 10, 2020** ( _BCO_ 5-9.d, 5-9.i.1) – It is unclear
whether officers were elected for a church petitioning for
particularization and whether the commission would install those
officers.

## Response found satisfactory — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.547](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p547)*

**Exception: Oct 10, 2020** ( _BCO_ 5-9.d, 5-9.i.1) – It is unclear whether
officers were elected for a church petitioning for particularization and
whether the commission would install those officers.

**Response:** The JRP acknowledges and agrees with the Assembly's
taken exception of substance, and expresses its commitment to
comply with all of the _BCO_ 's requirements for recording the election
and installation of officers for the particularizing church. We
acknowledge that we failed to record this matter in our minutes and
will strive to do better in the future.
