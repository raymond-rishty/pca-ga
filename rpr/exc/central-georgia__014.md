## Response found satisfactory — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.502](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p502)*

**Exception: General 2019** ( _BCO_ 13-9) – No record that session
minutes were examined.

**Response:** The minutes of the May and September Meetings stated
that an oral report was made by the Chairman of the subcommittee on
Sessional Records.
We agree the minutes did not specifically state that sessional records
were reviewed; however, in fact, the Chairman did report at the
September 2019 meeting that the Subcommittee had reviewed the
records and reported back to the church Sessions with exceptions and
notations.
In the future, the Presbytery will ensure a more detailed report will be
included in the Stated Meeting minutes.
Following is a portion of the Chairman’s notes at the September 2019
meeting:
_Records of all but 2 churches have been submitted to_
_Presbytery and reviewed. Minutes from six churches were_
_found to have had no exceptions or notations. For the_
_remaining churches, the Subcommittee cited their exceptions_
_or notations and provided a report to each of the church_
_Sessions._
