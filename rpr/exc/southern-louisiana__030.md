## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.763](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p763)*

**2024-06: General 2023** ( _BCO_ 18-6) — Annual reports of
candidates not included.

**Response:** The Southern Louisiana Presbytery
acknowledges the exception of substance cited by the
Committee on Review of Presbytery Records (RPR) for its
October 28, 2023, stated meeting (p. 42), noting that the
minutes lack a record of the ordination questions from _BCO_
21-5 being administered to Teaching Elder [Name Omitted]
during his ordination service.
To address this exception, the presbytery provides the
following:
1. Circumstances of the November 12, 2023,
Ordination: On November 12, 2023, TE [Name Omitted] was
ordained as a teaching elder during a church service held at

[Church Name Omitted], in accordance with _BCO_ 21-5. The
presbytery's commission, appointed at the October 28, 2023
meeting, conducted the service, and the ordination questions
from _BCO_ 21-5 were administered to TE [Name Omitted],
who answered them affirmatively, but the minutes of that
commission meeting incorrectly say that the questions from
_BCO_ 21-9 were administered.

2. Corrective Action: The commission of SOLA

Presbytery empowered to address this deficiency has
confirmed with the commission members that during the
November 12, 2023 service, TE [Name Omitted] was asked
the ordination questions from _BCO_ 21-5, including his
affirmation of PCA doctrine, discipline, and ministerial
duties, and he responded affirmatively. The commission has
amended the minutes of their commission to reflect this

correction by adding: "During the ordination service for TE

[Name Omitted] at [Church Name Omitted] on November 12,

2023 the commission administered the ordination questions
per _BCO_ 21-5, which he answered in the affirmative."
The SOLA Presbytery affirms its commitment to
conducting ordinations in accordance with _BCO_ 21-5, within
the context of a church service, and respectfully requests that
the General Assembly find this response satisfactory. We are
prepared to provide additional documentation, such as the
commission's report or service order, if needed.
