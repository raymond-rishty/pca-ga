## Raised — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.346](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p346)*

Exception: July 28-29, 2000: No record that man was a member of a church of NTP for 6 months or that he had the endorsement of his session. BCO 20-1, 13-10.

## Response found satisfactory — 30th General Assembly (2002)
*[ga30_2002 p.327](../../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p327)*

Exception: July 28-29, 2000: No record that man was
a member of a church of nt p for 6 months or that he
had the endorsement of his session. BCO 20-1; 13-10.
Response: The Stated Clerk inadvertently omitted
this information. Letters and records in the files
indicate them an was a member of ant p church for 6
months and the files do contain a letter of sessional
endorsement (dated 6/11/2000). The SC will take
greater pains not to leave this detail out of the Minutes
in the future. Adopted
