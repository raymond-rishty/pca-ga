## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.764](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p764)*

**2024-01: Apr 22, 2023** ( _BCO_ 18-2) — No record of sixmonths’ membership or endorsement by candidate’s Session.

**Response:** The Southern Louisiana Presbytery
acknowledges the exception of substance cited by the
Committee on Review of Presbytery Records (RPR) for its
October 28, 2023, stated meeting (p. 33), noting that the
minutes lack a record of Mr. [Name Omitted]'s six-months'
communicant membership in a PCA church or endorsement
by his Session, as required by _BCO_ 18-2, prior to his being
taken under care as a candidate for the gospel ministry.
1. Circumstances: At the October 28, 2023, stated
meeting, the presbytery voted to receive Mr. [Name Omitted]
as a candidate under care. As reported by our candidates
committee, it was determined that Mr. [Name Omitted] had
not been a communicant member of [Church Name Omitted]
at least six-months prior to October 28, 2023. The Presbytery
acted, upon the recommendation of the Candidates
Committee to approve Mr. [Name Omitted] as a candidate for
gospel ministry given his previous labors in the ministry and
his Session's endorsement prior to October 2023. These facts
were inadvertently omitted from the minutes due to clerical
oversight.
2. Corrective Action: The commission of SOLA

Presbytery assigned to respond to these exceptions has
confirmed with the Candidates Committee of Presbytery and

[Church Name Omitted] that Mr. [Name Omitted] was
endorsed by [Church Name Omitted]'s session and approved
by Presbytery under the exceptional provision of _BCO_ 18-2.
Further we have corrected our minutes of the October 28,
2023 stated meeting to reflect this action.

The SOLA Presbytery affirms its commitment to
adhering to _BCO_ 18-2 and respectfully requests that the
General Assembly find this response satisfactory. We are
prepared to provide additional documentation, including
Session records, if needed.
