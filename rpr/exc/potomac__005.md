## Response found unsatisfactory — 21st General Assembly (1993)
*[ga21_1993 p.247](../../markdown/ga21_1993.md#ga21-p247)*

Exception: The Presbytery was cited for the following: The minutes
for 2/9/91 and 5/14/91 showed no reports from interns (BCO 19-2) and,
in general, no reports given from candidates under care (BCO 18-6).
(Appendix 3-B).

Response: The following responses to these matters were:
i. It was M/A that the Presbytery agrees that we have failed
on occasion to provide time for reports of interns. We will attempt, in
the future, to comply with this requirement.
ii. It was moved that the Presbytery agrees that we have not
provided reports from candidates under care. Each candidate will be
reported on to Presbytery in the future.
Adopted
