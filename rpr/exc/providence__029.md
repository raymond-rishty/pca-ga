## Response found satisfactory — 47th General Assembly (2019)
*[ga47_2019 p.500](../../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p500)*

**Exception: Nov 7, 2017** ( _BCO_ 21-4c; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) – All specific
requirements of ordination exam not included.

**Response:** TE [name omitted]’s ordination exam was split between
the May, August and November Presbytery meetings. The August
meeting included Christian Experience and Calling (21-4.c.1.a),
English Bible (21-4.c.1.c), Theology (21-4.c.1.d), Church
Government (21-4.c.1.h) and Representative Sermon (21-4.c.4).
The November meeting included Church History (21-4.c.1.f),
Sacraments (21-4.c.1.e), Exegetical Paper (21-4.c.3) and
Theological Paper (21-4.c.2). We neglected to include an acceptance
of his Seminary Degree in lieu of an oral examination of the original
languages (21-4.c.1.b) in accordance with 21.c.1. We sincerely
apologize for the oversight and we will be sure to explicitly call out
this as part of a checklist in future meetings.
