# Southwest Florida Presbytery — BCO 8-6

*p 4, 3b & 2c: Some powers of evangelist are given in TE's Call, but he is not…*

**Presbytery:** Southwest Florida  ·  **First raised:** 23rd (1995)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 8-6

**Lifecycle:** satisfactory (23rd)

## Response found satisfactory — 23rd General Assembly (1995)
*[ga23_1995 p.206](../../markdown/ga23_1995.md#ga23-p206)*

Exception: p 4, 3b & 2c: Some powers of evangelist are given in TE's
Call, but he is not called an evangelist by separate act (BCO 8-6,21-11).

Response: The PSWFL Stated Clerk, in line with BOCO 8-6, will report
to the 23rd General Assembly that the October 1993 Minutes will be
corrected to show the action of Presbytery, that is, TE Villenueva was
called as an evangelist.
