## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.633](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p633)*

**2024-05: General 2023** ( _BCO_ 13-9.b; _BCO_ 40-1) —
Incomplete record of review of records of church sessions.
**Response:** Missouri Presbytery respectfully
disagrees with the exception and refers it back to the
Assembly. Although not all sessions submitted minutes for
review, Missouri Presbytery reported on all session minutes
that were reviewed during 2023. Therefore there was a
complete record of the session minutes that were reviewed.
The Presbytery continues to press for compliance with this
review obligation and, further, has reviwed the manner in
which it reports the review of session records for additional
clarity and to further encourage all session to timely submit
session minutes for review.

**Rationale:** _BCO_ 40-1 makes it clear that the
Presbytery has a duty to review the records of all sessions
within their bounds. A review is incomplete even if the
presbytery reviews all the records that are submitted, but fails
to receive records from all churches.
