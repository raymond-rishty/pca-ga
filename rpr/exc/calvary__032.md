## Response found satisfactory — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.324](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p324)*

Exception: April 23, 1998: There is no motion where a TE is approved for transfer from the ARP pending his release. There is no record in his exam that he was asked to ascribe to the questions of ordination. BCO 13-6.

Response: We had received his transfer and thus no motion was needed in this regard, otherwise we would have stated "pending his transfer." All questions were asked and answers given in the affirmative and he then signed the Book of Ministerial Obligations. We did everything in an orderly fashion but if anything was lacking, we apologize and will try to make it clearer in the future.
