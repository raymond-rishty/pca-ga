**Response:** The report of the Sessional Records Committee at the
January meeting was preliminary, in that churches have until the
September meeting to have their records reviewed and approved. As
such, we recorded only that the Committee Chairman gave a report,
without providing details of his report.
In the future, CGP agrees to record the narrative of the
Committee report at every meeting in which a report is given.
With respect to the January 21, 2017 Stated Presbytery meeting,
CGP minutes will be amended to include the following report which
was given orally by the Chairman of the Sessional Records
Committee.

1. Prayer
2. Overall submission of the 2016 minutes was better and

much more timely than last year. Eleven churches

emailed their minutes in and seven were brought in for
review today. All minutes indicate nothing was done
contrary to good order.
3. Clerks need to be more careful to document 1) Reports

from Presbytery meetings, 2) Deacons Minutes review.
3) Annual Statistical Reports and 4) A listing of
baptisms, marriages, deaths (recommend you put these
items in the minutes of your last meeting of the year).
4. Some facts. CGP churches held 103 Stated Meetings,

27 Called Meetings and 8 Congregational Meetings.
5. The best minutes were from [church names omitted].
6. Prayer.
At the CGP Stated Presbytery meeting on September 11, 2018, a
motion was moved, seconded and passed to approve the above
response to RPR. This response will be included in the minutes of
the September 11 [th] meeting.
