## Raised — 48th General Assembly (2021)
*[ga48_2021 p.555](../../markdown/ga48_2021.md#ga48-p555)*

**Exception: Jan 21, 2020** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) – All specific
requirements for ordination exam not recorded; seminary degree,

## Response found satisfactory — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.524](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p524)*

**Exception: Jan 21, 2020** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) – All specific
requirements for ordination exam not recorded; seminary degree,
thesis paper/ examination on knowledge of Greek and Hebrew; did
not preach a sermon.
**Response:** Gulfstream regrets the inadequacy of its minutes. In
preparing its minutes, Gulfstream has always relied upon _Form 039-_
_Checklist for Ordination,_ as found in the Clerks Handbook. The
specific omissions named in this exception have always been
examined as part of the preliminary checklist, and may not have been
included in the minutes. Nor have some other elements of the

preliminary checklist. Gulfstream will endeavor to include these
details in future minutes.
**Rationale:** Presbytery needs to correct the inadequacy in their
minutes and submit for review.

## Response found unsatisfactory — 50th General Assembly (2023)
*[ga50_2023 p.562](../../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p562)*

**Exception: Jan 21, 2020** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) – All specific
requirements for ordination exam not recorded; seminary degree, thesis
paper/ examination on knowledge of Greek and Hebrew; did not preach a

sermon.
**Response [2022]:** Gulfstream regrets the inadequacy of its minutes. In
preparing its minutes, Gulfstream has always relied upon _Form 039-_
_Checklist for Ordination,_ as found in the Clerks Handbook. The specific
omissions named in this exception have always been examined as part of the
preliminary checklist, and may not have been included in the minutes. Nor
have some other elements of the preliminary checklist. Gulfstream will
endeavor to include these details in future minutes.
**Rationale [2022]:** Presbytery needs to correct the inadequacy in their
minutes and submit for review.
**Response [2023]:** Gulfstream has amended its minutes accordingly.
**Rationale [2023]:** Minutes of presbytery relating to examinations must list
all specific requirements and trials for licensure and/or ordination which have
been accomplished, including that each candidate being examined for
ordination was required to “state the specific instances in which he may
differ with the Confession of Faith and Catechisms in any of their statements
and/or propositions” ( _BCO_ 21-4).
