## Response found unsatisfactory — 35th General Assembly (2007)
*[ga35_2007 p.220](../../markdown/ga35_2007.md#ga35-p220)*

**Exception: July 23, 2005:** The call for the meeting is not recorded
verbatim in the minutes. This is especially important because a
judicial matter is being considered. _RAO_ 14-03.c.1.

**Response:** The Sun Coast Florida Presbytery agrees with the
exception and corrects its records (when possible), its actions, and
promises to be more careful in the future.
