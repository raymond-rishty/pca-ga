## Response found satisfactory — 33rd General Assembly (2005)
*[ga33_2005 p.273](../../markdown/ga33_2005.md#ga33-p273)*

**Exception: September 9, 2003:** Commission to ordain and install
failed to provide complete record of proceedings. _BCO_ 15-1
**Response:** Presbytery agrees that there should have been a citation,
but disagrees as to the substance of it. The bound minutes of
Presbytery at this point reflect a failure in proofreading. What
appears in the bound minutes is actually the first part of one
commission report and the last part of a separate commission report.
It is so obvious that there are parts missing, that Presbytery finds it
difficult to believe that anyone reading these minutes would have
supposed that a report in such form would actually have been
approved by the Presbytery. In any case, the minutes should have
read as follows:
**Report of the Commission to Ordain and Install TE**
**Cangelosi.**
MSP to approve the report provided that some minor discrepancies
are corrected. _Adopted_
