## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.518](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p518)*

**2024-01: Mar 4, 2023** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) —
Incomplete record of ordination exam.

**Response:** The Minutes did not record the full extent
of the examination for the candidate, not withstanding their
previous sustaining of Licensure examination at a previous
Stated Meeting. The Candidate sustained examination in all
areas required for Ordination, including Christian experience
and sense of call to the ministry, scholastic credentialing,
Bible Content, Theology, Sacraments, Church History,
History of the PCA, the _BCO_, and principles of church
government and discipline. The minutes have been corrected
to address this oversight.

## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.522](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p522)*

**2024-01: May 20, 2023** ( _BCO_ 23-1) — No record that
congregation concurred with the dissolution of pastoral
relations.

**Response:** CVP acknowledges that the minutes of the
called meeting of [Church Name Omitted] that it had voted

unanimously in a congregational meeting on 3/19/23 to accept
the TE’s resignation was inadvertently omitted from the
Shepherding Committee report that it had acted as a
commission to dissolve the pastoral relationship. The minutes
of that congregational meeting as well as a letter from the
resigning TE have now been submitted to presbytery by the
Shepherding Committee. CVP voted on Sept 28, 2024 to
correct this action by adding the May 19, 2023 congregational
minutes and letter of resignation as an appendix to the May
20, 2023 CVP Minutes, and CVP promises to be more diligent
to include such documentation in our future minutes.
