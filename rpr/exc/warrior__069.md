# Warrior Presbytery — BCO 10-3

*The Moderator may be elected for at most 1 year at a time. The Standing Rules…*

**Presbytery:** Warrior  ·  **First raised:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 10-3

**Lifecycle:** raised (51st) → satisfactory (52nd)

---

## Raised — 51st General Assembly (2024)
*[ga51_2024 p.694](../../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p694)*

**2024-2: General 2023** ( _BCO_ 10-3) — The Moderator may be elected
for at most 1 year at a time. The Standing Rules state that he "shall
serve until his successor is elected", which has no limit.

## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.782](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p782)*

**2024-02: General 2023** ( _BCO_ 10-3) — The Moderator may
be elected for at most 1 year at a time. The Standing Rules
state that he "shall serve until his successor is elected", which
has no limit.

**Response:** Warrior Presbytery apologizes for this
oversight and has begun the process of revising our Standing
Rules to explicitly limit the moderator to, at most, one year of
service at a time.
