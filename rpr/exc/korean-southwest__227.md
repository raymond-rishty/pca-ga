**2023-18: Sep 13, 2022** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) —
Incomplete record of ordination exam. Men are approved for
ordination but no evidence of education requirements,
required papers, or original languages.
**Response:** We apologize for the oversight in not
properly documenting the candidate’s examination in all
required areas. The candidate was examined in committee on
all aspects, including the items mentioned above, and was
subject to open examination. At KSWOC’s March 11, 2025
meeting, the March 14, 2023 minutes were corrected by
appending the commission minutes through a motion to
“Amend Something Previously Adopted.”
**Rationale:** The response indicates a correction to the
record for a different meeting (March 2023) than the one that
was originally cited for the exception (September 2022).
