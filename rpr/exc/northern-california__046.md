**Response:** Presbytery agrees with this exception of substance and
regrets its oversight of not including the call to a definite work for
TE Ernest Kim. At the time of the meeting, the call to a definite
work was read aloud and approved by all parties, and placed in the
hands of TE Kim. However, presbytery failed to record the call to a
definite work. Presbytery promises to be more careful in the future.
