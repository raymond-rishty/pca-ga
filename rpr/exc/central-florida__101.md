**2024-1: Jan 17, 2023** ( _BCO_ 20-8) — No record commissioners

## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.525](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p525)*

**2024-01: Jan 17, 2023** ( _BCO_ 20-8) — No record
commissioners appointed by the church presented and
prosecuted the call of a TE before Presbytery.
**Response:** Agreed that there is no explicit record of
named commissioners actually presenting and prosecuting the
call. But there is record that the call was reviewed and the
terms were approved, which surely assumes it was presented
and prosecuted. Previous “Guidelines to Exceptions” do not
list the failure to explicitly state that named commissioners
presented and prosecuted the call as an “exception of
substance.” In the future, will this level of detail qualify under
_RAO_ 16-6.c.1) as an “Exception of Substance”? Will this be
considered a “serious irregularity” or “important
delinquency”?
**Rationale:** Presbytery must respond following the
pattern of _RAO_ 16-10.b.
