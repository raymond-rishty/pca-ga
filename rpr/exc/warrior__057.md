## Response found satisfactory — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.630](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p630)*

**Exception: Oct 16, 2018** ( _BCO_ 23-1) – No record of congregational
meeting to dissolve the call of a pastor.
**Response:** Warrior Presbytery appreciates RPR's diligence in
guarding the integrity of the pastor/church relationship and the
importance of care being given in the dissolution of a call. However,
in this instance, the Teaching Elder who resigned was not serving as
the church's pastor, but as Stated Supply. The court did not think that
the dissolution of that temporary relationship required a
congregational meeting or the approval of Presbytery
