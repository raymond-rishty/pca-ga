## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.707](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p707)*

**2024-01: General 2023** ( _RAO_ 16-10.a) — No response to the
Assembly concerning the disposition of the exception of
substance to which the response to the 50th GA was found to
be unsatisfactory.

**Response:** At our 129th stated meeting on 1/26/2024,
we approved responses to five exceptions of substance cited
by the 50th GA, to be submitted to the RPR committee, but
inadvertently omitted in our responses the other exception to
which our previous response had been found unsatisfactory.
At our next stated meeting (130th) on 4/25/2024, we
approved the new response to this missing exception of
substance, where our original response to the 50th GA had
been found to be unsatisfactory. We subsequently submitted
this new response separately to the RPR committee, which
was well after the committee deadline (3/15/2024), for which
we sincerely apologize.
This response was taken up on the floor of the RPR
committee meeting, along with responses to other exceptions
that had already been submitted, and all of them except for
one were recommended to be found satisfactory, which is the

action the 51st GA took (please see 51st GA minutes, pages
645-7).
We will be careful in the future to submit our responses in a
timely manner so as to avoid any confusion that our late
submission might have caused last year.
