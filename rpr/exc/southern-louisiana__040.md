shortcomings, the presbytery provides the following:
1. Circumstances of the October 22, 2022,

particular church. At that time, the congregation had not yet
elected ruling elders or called a pastor due and was going to
be doing so at a called meeting prior to the Commission's
meeting on December 19th.

19, 2022, a presbytery commission met fora particularization
service. The commission oversaw the election of 5 Ruling
Elders ([Names Omitted]) and facilitated the call of TE [Name
Omitted] as pastor, with the congregation voting to approve
both actions on that date. The commission's report, adopted at
the January 28, 2023 stated presbytery meeting has been
amended to reflect this action taken by the commission of
SOLA Presbytery.
3. Addressing Prior Unsatisfactory Responses: We
understand from the Clerk's Report that prior responses were
unsatisfactory due to a lack of detail on corrective actions. The
presbytery has addressed this by providing specific actions
taken on December 19, 2022, amending the 2022 minutes, and
establishing a preventive process.
The SOLA Presbytery affirms its commitment to
organizing churches in accordance with _BCO_ 5-9 and
respectfully requests that the General Assembly find this
response satisfactory and we are available to supply additional
documentation if needed.
