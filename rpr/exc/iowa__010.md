## Raised — 45th General Assembly (2017)

**Exception: July 9, 2016** ( _BCO_ 23-1) – No record of congregation

## Response found satisfactory — 46th General Assembly (2018)
*[ga46_2018 p.403](../../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p403)*

**Exception: July 9, 2016** ( _BCO_ 23-1) – No record of congregation
meeting for dissolution of pastoral relation.

**Response:** At the July 9 meeting, Iowa Presbytery was informed
verbally by the Session of that church that such a meeting had
happened and that the dissolution of pastoral relation had been
approved. We do regret that this was not recorded in our minutes
and will see to see to it that such information will be properly
recorded in future minutes of Iowa Presbytery.
