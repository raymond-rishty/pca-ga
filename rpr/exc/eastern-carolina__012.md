## Raised — 28th General Assembly (2000)
*[ga28_2000 p.323](../../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p323)*

Exception: October 6, 1999: Attachment G. No quorum for commission appointed to install TE. (RE failed to attend). BCO 15-2.

## Response found satisfactory — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.327](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p327)*

Exception: October 6, 1999: Attachment G. No quorum for commission appointed to install TE. (RE failed to attend). BCO 15-2.

Response: ECP apologizes for not taking the following action at the October 16 meeting: “ECP acknowledges that the commission to install TE Peterson did not have a quorum. By BCO 15-2 and Roberts Rules (RONR), the work they did would be invalid. However, the commission expected the fourth man to arrive by the announced time, 6 PM. Meanwhile, the invited guests had arrived and were anticipating the service. This particular task of a commission is to do work, rather than to deliberate, decide, and execute. The deliberation and decision portion of the installation task was already handled properly by the full ECP. ECP approves the decision, made at 6 PM by the short commission, that the ultimate purpose of the commission would be better served by (a) proceeding in violation of RONR and BCO 15-2 versus (b) announcing the lack of quorum and sending guests home. ECP will strive to appoint more than 4 men to ordain/install commissions in the future.”

Adopted
