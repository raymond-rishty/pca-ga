## Raised — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.531](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p531)*

**2025-07: Mar 12, 2024** ( _BCO_ 32; _BCO_ 31-2; _BCO_ 34; _BCO_
38-1; _RONR_ (12th ed) 48:14, 35:13) — Presbytery votes to
"strike from the minutes of its 86th Meeting of the Presbytery

[which would have been over 3 years prior] where it
erroneously records that TE [Name omitted] was suspended
from the sacraments." Presbytery then votes to instruct the
Stated Clerk to "communicate the judgment of CPJC" to the
TE, which judgment was the censure of deposition for the sin
of failing to manage his household well. There is no record
of the 2/3 vote needed to amend previous minutes. Further,
there is no record of any formal process, of a case without
process, or of any report as to how this matter came about. It
is not clear if this is a new censure or if it is imposing what
Presbytery intended to impose at the 86th meeting. Either is
constitutionally dubious at this point.
