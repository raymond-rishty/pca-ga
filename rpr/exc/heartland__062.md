## Raised — 51st General Assembly (2024)
*[ga51_2024 p.520](../../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p520)*

**2024-1: Feb 04, 2023** ( _BCO_ 6-2; _BCO_ 57) — Presbytery accepts a
response from a session that had “received members while imposing
conditions on their membership.” While session admitted their error
and promised not to repeat it in the future, there is no evidence that
they removed the “conditions” from the members in question.

## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)

**Response:** The Heartland Presbytery agrees with this
exception. The Session in question has removed the condition
for membership by passing the following motion to Amend
Something Previously Adopted (RONR [12th ed.] 48:15):
Motion to amend the action adopted at the July 22, 2021
Stated Meeting of the Session of [Church Name Omitted]
relating to the membership of the [names omitted] which
stated, “Motion to approve [names omitted] for membership
in [Church Name Omitted] with the condition that they would
not be allowed to teach. [Names omitted] each gave a credible
profession of faith in Christ during their interview. [Names
omitted] assented to the vows of church membership as
outlined in _BCO_ 57-5. [Names omitted] have been baptized.

[Names omitted] will be received by reaffirmation of faith.”
The amended motion will now read, "Motion to approve

[names omitted] for membership in [Church Name Omitted].

[Names omitted] each gave a credible profession of faith in

Christ during their interview. [Names omitted] assented to the
vows of church membership as outlined in _BCO_ 57-5. [Names
