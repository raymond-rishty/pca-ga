# Chicago Metro Presbytery — BCO 21-4.f

*Presbytery granted a doctrinal exception that may be hostile to our system of…*

**Presbytery:** Chicago Metro  ·  **First raised:** 49th (2022)  ·  **Final disposition:** unsatisfactory (outstanding)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 21-4.f

**Lifecycle:** raised (49th) → unsatisfactory (50th)

---

## Raised — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.506](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p506)*

**Exception: Aug 18, 2021** ( _BCO_ 21-4.f; 40-2) – Presbytery granted a
doctrinal exception that may be hostile to our system of doctrine.
More information or clarity is needed on the exception, wherein the
candidate expresses his belief that images of Christ may be suitable
“for purposes of instruction and (with great care) **public worship** ”
(emphasis added).
