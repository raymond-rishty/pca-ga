## Response found satisfactory — 46th General Assembly (2018)

**Response:** We agree with the RPR Committee that the minutes did
not record all specific requirements for the transfer exam for a TE
from another denomination. The TE was examined in Greek and

Hebrew and PCA History, but this was not recorded in the
examination by the presbytery clerk. The clerk apologizes for these
omissions and will record all examination requirements in the future.
