## Response found unsatisfactory — 35th General Assembly (2007)
*[ga35_2007 p.219](../../markdown/ga35_2007.md#ga35-p219)*

**Exception: January 15, 2005:** Minutes record a man having 3
exceptions, but only 1 is recorded and Presbytery does not act on the
exceptions. _BCO_ 21-4, _RAO_ 14-3.e.5.

**Response:** The Sun Coast Florida Presbytery agrees with the
exception and corrects its records (when possible), its actions, and
promises to be more careful in the future.
