## Response found unsatisfactory — 23rd General Assembly (1995)
*[ga23_1995 p.194](../../markdown/ga23_1995.md#ga23-p194)*

Exception: 93.5.016 & Attachments E & F: Presbytery may not hold
members of dissolved church. M16GA,p 177,178.16-77,111,28.

Response: Mid-America Presbytery agrees with the exceptions, and
promises to be more careful in the future. As to the matter of Presbytery
holding members of a dissolved church, please note that the Commission
which gave oversight to these members was dissolved in May, 1994.
Adopted
