## Raised — 36th General Assembly (2008)

that minister seeking admission to the presbytery was examined in

## Response found unsatisfactory — 37th General Assembly (2009)
*[ga37_2009 p.106](../../markdown/ga37_2009.md#ga37-p106)*

**Exception: April 17, 2007; July 16, 2007:** _BCO_ 13-6. No record
that minister seeking admission to the presbytery was examined in
accordance to _BCO_ 13-6.

**Response:** Minister seeking admission to the presbytery was
examined in accordance with _BCO_ 13-6, as noted in minutes. We
failed to note the details of _BCO_ 13-6. The exams did include
sacraments, Christian experience, theology and church government.
