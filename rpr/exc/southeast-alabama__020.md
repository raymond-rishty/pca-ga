*January 29, 2002: Licensure exam incomplete; did not preach before Presbytery.…*

**Lifecycle:** raised (31st) → satisfactory (32nd)

## Raised — 31st General Assembly (2003)
*[ga31_2003 p.251](../../markdown/ga31_2003.md#ga31-p251)*

**Exception:** **January 29, 2002:** Licensure exam incomplete;
did not preach before Presbytery. _BCO_ 19:1, 21:4

## Response found satisfactory — 32nd General Assembly (2004)
*[ga32_2004 p.240](../../markdown/ga32_2004.md#ga32-p240)*

**Exception:** **January 29, 2002:** Licensure exam incomplete; did not
preach before Presbytery. _BCO_ 19:1, 21:4
**Response:** Concerning the failure of the Presbytery to require the
licentiate to preach before Presbytery, we failed to record specifically
what parts of the examination required for a minister coming from
another denomination were omitted. Though presbytery understood
(“Motion was made and passed that in view of his 23 years of pastoral
experience in the ARP, the licensure exam required in _BCO_ 19-1 be
replaced with an examination as to his views [19-2]”), this should
have been stated, along with reasons. _Adopted_
