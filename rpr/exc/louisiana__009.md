## Raised — 28th General Assembly (2000)
*[ga28_2000 p.328](../../markdown/ga28_2000.md#ga28-p328)*

Exception: General: no directory of presbytery churches, roll of
presbytery or roll of candidates and licentiates of the presbytery churches
is included,

## Response found satisfactory — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.340](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p340)*

Exception: General: no directory of presbytery churches, roll of
presbytery or roll of candidates and licentiates of the presbytery churches
is included;

Response: only one copy of these was submitted for 1999. The
Administrative Committee did not consider this to be complete since
three copies were not submitted, and thus the records were not passed
along to the review of presbytery records Committee. The Stated Clerk
takes responsibility for not having submitted the records in triplicate to
the Administrative Committee offices, and has submitted triplicate
copies of the present records for the year 2000.
A dopted
