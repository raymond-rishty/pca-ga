## Response found satisfactory — 34th General Assembly (2006)
*[ga34_2006 p.301](../../markdown/ga34_2006.md#ga34-p301)*

**Exception: General:** No annual reports from men serving out of
bounds. _BCO_ 8-7

**Response:** CRPR is correct in citing this exception. When the
current stated clerk of presbytery came into office there was no

evident standard practice on this requirement. The Administrative
Committee of Warrior Presbytery will devise a schedule as to when
such reports ought to be due.
