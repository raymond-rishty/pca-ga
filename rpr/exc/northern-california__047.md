# Northern California Presbytery — BCO 20-1

*Call not included in minutes thus causing confusion as to the calling…*

**Presbytery:** Northern California  ·  **First raised:** 42nd (2014)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 20-1

**Lifecycle:** raised (42nd) → satisfactory (43rd)

---

## Raised — 42nd General Assembly (2014)
*[ga42_2014 p.433](../../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p433)*

**Exception: May 3, 2013** ( _BCO_ 20-1) – Call not included in minutes
thus causing confusion as to the calling body/organization

## Response found satisfactory — 43rd General Assembly (2015)
*[ga43_2015 p.459](../../markdown/ga43_2015.md#ga43-p459)*

**Exception: May 3, 2013** ( _BCO_ 20-1) – Call not included in minutes
thus causing confusion as to the calling body/organization.

**Response:** Presbytery admits confusion on this exception of
substance. The minutes record that the call was read aloud, placed in
the hands of the ministers for whom there was a change in call, and
approved by all parties involved. Presbytery has no recollection of
including the terms of a call in its minutes for GA RPR for any

Teaching Elder in the past. If such terms are required for RPR
review, presbytery will gladly include them henceforth and will
agree with this exception of substance.
