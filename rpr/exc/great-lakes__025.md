# Great Lakes Presbytery — BCO 19-2

*No indication that candidate for li censure preached a sermon as part of his…*

**Presbytery:** Great Lakes  ·  **First raised:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 19-2

**Lifecycle:** raised (29th) → satisfactory (30th)

---

## Raised — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.328](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p328)*

Exception: October 13-14, 2000: No indication that candidate for
li censure preached a sermon as part of his exam. BCO 19-2.

## Response found satisfactory — 30th General Assembly (2002)
*[ga30_2002 p.311](../../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p311)*

Exception: October 13-14, 2000: No indication that
candidate for licensure preached a sermon as part of
his exam. BCO 19-2.
Response: Great Lakes Presbytery verifies that the
candidate did, indeed preach a sermon at that meeting
and that the sermon was approved by Presbytery. We
regret that the itemizing of the individual requirements
for licensure was not consistent with the action of
Presbytery recorded as ‘On separate motions all areas
of his exam were sustained’, and will be more careful
to be consistent in the future. Adopted
