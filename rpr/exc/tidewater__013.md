## Response found unsatisfactory — 50th General Assembly (2023)

**Rationale [2022]:** Presbytery needs to submit the commission minutes for

**Response [2023]:** While I failed to record the make up of the commissions

approved until a later Stated Meeting. At the Feb 1, 2020 meeting the

were not set at that meeting. One candidate left the PCA before his
ordination, and the other did have his ordination commission at a later date.
**Rationale [2023]:** No record that Presbytery corrected its record ( _RAO_ 1610.b.1). Presbytery has not submitted the commission minutes for review.
