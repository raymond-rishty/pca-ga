## Response found unsatisfactory — 50th General Assembly (2023)
*[ga50_2023 p.526](../../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p526)*

**Exception: Apr 9, 2021** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) – Stated differences not
judged with the prescribed categories.

**Response:** The Presbytery agrees with RPR Committee that, although the
minutes stated the exception was deemed acceptable, they did not cite the
prescribed category per _RAO_ 16-3.e5.
The Presbytery apologizes for the error and will correct the minutes to state
that _RAO_ 16-3.e5(c) applies: “The stated difference is more than semantic,
but not out of accord with any fundamental of our system of doctrine.” The
Presbytery promises to be more careful in the future.
