## Raised — 35th General Assembly (2007)
*[ga35_2007 p.217](../../markdown/ga35_2007.md#ga35-p217)*

**Exception: January 21, 2006:** Only Presbytery as a whole can conduct
examination of minister from another denomination. _BCO_ 15-2.

## Raised — 36th General Assembly (2008)

**Exception: January 21, 2006:** Only Presbytery as a whole can conduct
examination of minister from another denomination. _BCO_ 15-2.

**Response:** This was an error on our part. We apologize and we will
endeavor to be more diligent in the future.
