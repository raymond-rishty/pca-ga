# Evangel Presbytery — BCO 23-1

*No indication that a congregational meeting was held to approve dissolution of…*

**Presbytery:** Evangel  ·  **First raised:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 23-1

**Lifecycle:** raised (29th) → satisfactory (30th)

---

## Raised — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.327](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p327)*

Exception: May 9, 2000; September 26, 2000: No indication that a congregational meeting was held to approve dissolution of pastoral relationship. BCO 23-1.

## Response found satisfactory — 30th General Assembly (2002)
*[ga30_2002 p.309](../../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p309)*

Exception: May 9, 2000; September 26, 2000: No
indication that a congregational meeting was held to
approve dissolution of pastoral relationship. BCO 23-1.

Response: I have spoken to the Chairman of the
Ministers Committee who is responsible for the action
taken concerning the dissolution of pastoral
relationship. There was a congregational meeting
which was appropriately called and conducted. The
Secretary of the committee failed to include the
minutes of that meeting in the minutes of the
committee meeting. The Chairman of this committee
will ensure that from this point on all minutes of
Congregational Meetings which dissolve the pastoral
relationship will be recorded in their minutes and
reported to Presbytery.
