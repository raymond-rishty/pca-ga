*All specific requirements for ordination not listed as…*

**Lifecycle:** raised (42nd) → satisfactory (43rd)

## Raised — 42nd General Assembly (2014)
*[ga42_2014 p.407](../../markdown/ga42_2014.md#ga42-p407)*

**Exception: February 12, 2013** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) – All
specific requirements for ordination not listed as fulfilled

## Response found satisfactory — 43rd General Assembly (2015)
*[ga43_2015 p.437](../../markdown/ga43_2015.md#ga43-p437)*

**Exception: February 12, 2013** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) – All
specific requirements for ordination not listed as fulfilled.

**Response:** Evangel Presbytery agrees with this exception. The
person being examined for ordination had previously been examined
for licensure. When we recorded the requirements he had previously
met, we failed to note that he had met the requirements for Greek
and Hebrew. This oversight was unfortunate and with renewed
diligence, it should not happen again.
