# Southwest Presbytery — BCO 24-1

*January 20-21, 2011 ( BCO 24-1) Inadequate amount of time between examination…*

**Presbytery:** Southwest  ·  **First raised:** 40th (2012)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 24-1

**Lifecycle:** raised (40th) → satisfactory (41st)

---

## Raised — 40th General Assembly (2012)
*[ga40_2012 p.473](../../markdown/ga40_2012.md#ga40-p473)*

**Exception:** **January 20-21, 2011** ( _BCO_ 24-1) Inadequate amount of
time between examination of RE candidates and their election – only
21 days when 30 are required.

## Response found satisfactory — 41st General Assembly (2013)
*[ga41_2013 p.480](../../markdown/ga41_2013.md#ga41-p480)*

**Exception:** **January 20-21, 2011** ( _BCO_ 24-1) – Inadequate amount
of time between examination of RE candidates and their election –
only 21 days when 30 are required.
**Response:** The Presbytery of the Southwest humbly apologizes to
the General Assembly for this mistake. In our zeal to accomplish the
particularization of the Church of the Resurrection in a timely fashion
while accommodating commissioners' schedules and travel over long

distances, we simply missed this requirement. We will endeavor to
be more careful in the future.
