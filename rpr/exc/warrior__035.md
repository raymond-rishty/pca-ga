## Response found satisfactory — 35th General Assembly (2007)

**Exception: January 18, 2005** : No record of congregational meeting

**Response** : We do not concur with CRPR’s findings. Appendix B to
the minutes for this stated meeting includes a letter from the
interested church dated January 9, 2005 which reports the
congregational vote to accept the resignation of said pastor. The
document was referenced in the minutes under section 28091 on the

report of communications received by the stated clerk of the
presbytery. If referencing such information under the appropriate
committee report is more helpful we can do that.
