# Wisconsin Presbytery — BCO 38-3

*Teaching elder removed from the rolls without specifying nature of removal,…*

**Presbytery:** Wisconsin  ·  **First raised:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Final disposition:** raised (open)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 38-3

**Lifecycle:** raised (51st)

---

## Raised — 51st General Assembly (2024)
*[ga51_2024 p.703](../../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p703)*

**2023-4: Apr 23, 2022** ( _BCO_ 38-3) — Teaching elder removed from
the rolls without specifying nature of removal, and without including
either the “irregularity” ( _BCO_ 38-3.a) or the “full record of the matter”
( _BCO_ 38-3.b).

**Response:** The Presbytery agrees with the exception, has voted to
correct its minutes from April 23, 2022, noting the irregularity under
38-3.a. The Presbytery promises to be more careful in the future.
