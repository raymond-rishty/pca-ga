## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.684](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p684)*

**2024-02: May 13, 2023** ( _BCO_ 18-2) — No record of sixmonths membership for candidate.

**Response:** Presbytery respectfully disagrees with the
General Assembly. The six-month membership requirement
for this candidate is noted in Appendix M of the minutes,
where the Session endorsement reads: “[Candidate] has been
a member of [our church] since Sept 2022” (which was more
than six-months). There is no _BCO_ requirement that it be
recorded in the body of the meeting minutes, and RPR noted
that it did read the endorsement in the appendix. With that

said, presbytery will do its best to record the six-month
membership in the body of the meeting minutes from now on
for the sake of clarity.
