## Response found unsatisfactory — 35th General Assembly (2007)
*[ga35_2007 p.208](../../markdown/ga35_2007.md#ga35-p208)*

**Exception: January 27, 2005 and October 27, 2005** : No record of
signing ministerial obligation. _BCO_ 13-7.
**Response** : This is found under declaration of quorum in January.
We only do this one time a year and that is at our January meeting.
**Rationale** : _BCO_ 13-7 requires the signing of the ministerial
obligation which “shall be subscribed by all admitted to membership.”
The practice of signing once per year creates a circumstance where
some TEs may be admitted to membership without having subscribed
to the ministerial obligation.
