# Great Lakes Presbytery — BCO 13-12

*Call for m eeting is not in order. BCO 13-12 requires 3RE’s & 3TE’s from 3…*

**Presbytery:** Great Lakes  ·  **First raised:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 13-12

**Lifecycle:** raised (29th) → satisfactory (30th)

---

## Raised — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.328](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p328)*

Exception: August 23, 2000: Call for m eeting is not in order. BCO
13-12 requires 3RE’s & 3TE’s from 3 churches.

## Response found satisfactory — 30th General Assembly (2002)
*[ga30_2002 p.310](../../markdown/ga30_2002.md#ga30-p310)*

Exception: August 23, 2000: Call for meeting is not
in order. BCO 13-12 requires 3RE’s & 3TE’s from 3
churches.

Response: Great Lakes Presbytery, as well as the
former Stated Clerk of GLP, acknowledge their error
in listing only 2 Teaching Elders and 2 Ruling Elders
representing 3 churches in the call for the meeting, and
will be more careful in the future to document the

concurrence of at least 3 Teaching Elders and 3 Ruling
Elders from 3 churches as required in BCO 13-12.
