# Southwest Florida Presbytery — BCO 8-7

*No record that the presbytery is assured that an out of bounds TE will have…*

**Presbytery:** Southwest Florida  ·  **First raised:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 8-7

**Lifecycle:** raised (51st) → satisfactory (52nd)

---

## Raised — 51st General Assembly (2024)
*[ga51_2024 p.680](../../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p680)*

**2024-1: May 09, 2023** ( _BCO_ 8-7) — No record that the presbytery is
assured that an out of bounds TE will have full freedom to maintain

and teach the doctrine of our church.

## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.768](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p768)*

**2024-01: May 9, 2023** ( _BCO_ 8-7) — No record that the
presbytery is assured that an out of bounds TE will have full
freedom to maintain and teach the doctrine of our church.

**Response:** The Presbytery agrees with the Exception
of Substance. The Minutes should have noted that the TEs will

have full freedom to maintain and teach the doctrine of our

church. The Presbytery has noted in its record that the TEs
have full freedom to maintain and teach the doctrine of our

church. The Presbytery promises to be more careful in the
future to state in the minutes that out of bounds TEs will have

full freedom to maintain and teach the doctrine of our church.
