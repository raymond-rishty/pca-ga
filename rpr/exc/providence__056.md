## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.692](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p692)*

**2024-02: Aug 1, 2023** ( _BCO_ 15-1; _RAO_ 16-3.e.4) — Minutes
of commission not entered in Presbytery minutes, and these
must be presented for review next year.
**Response:** The Presbytery agrees with this exception
and promises to be more careful in the future. It has not been
our practice to include these Minutes when a Standing
Committee has been empowered to act as a Commission and
the Presbytery is informed of the action, thus creating a
second set of Minutes that address the action taken.

Furthermore, the Presbytery receives the Pastoral Care
Committee’s Minutes within Docketed materials.
