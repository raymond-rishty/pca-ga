## Raised — 26th General Assembly (1998)
*[ga26_1998 p.235](../../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p235)*

General: Omissions to parts of ordination exams; no mention of church, session, or endorsement for any candidate; more than one candidate’s ordination exam omits languages and PCA history (BCO 21-4 and RAO 14-3.e.5, BCO 18-2; RAO 14-6.c.2). These exceptions are repeated from last year.
