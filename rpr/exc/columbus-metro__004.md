## Raised — 47th General Assembly (2019)
*[ga47_2019 p.459](../../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p459)*

**Exception: Jan 23, 2018; May 15, 2018; and Nov 13, 2018** ( _BCO_
13-4, 11; _SR_ 12.1, 4; _RONR_ 347-8) It does not appear from the
record or roll of Presbytery that a quorum of at least 3 TEs and 3
REs was present. By failing to immediately adjourn, and instead to
continue to conduct business without a quorum, the Presbytery put
itself at risk that a future meeting would fail to ratify their actions.

actions taken a ta meeting without a quorum are by themselves null
and void. (Note: An ordination exam occurred at one of these
meetings.
