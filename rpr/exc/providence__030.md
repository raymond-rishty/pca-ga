## Response found satisfactory — 47th General Assembly (2019)
*[ga47_2019 p.499](../../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p499)*

**Exception: Feb 14, 2017 (pg. 6, lines 36-37** ) – Minutes from
executive session not included.

**Response:** We sincerely apologize, the lack of executive session
minutes as part of the Feb 14, 2017 Presbytery minutes are without
excuse. The contents of the executive session consisted of a TE

taking a leave of absence as an announcement. This announcement
had been released to the congregation two days earlier and thus we
made the announcement in Executive Session in error. Although this
had been corrected in the minutes we neglected to reflect the change.
A criteria for entering Executive Session has been implemented to
ensure that this oversight does not occur in the future.
