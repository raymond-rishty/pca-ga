## Response found satisfactory — 35th General Assembly (2007)
*[ga35_2007 p.224](../../markdown/ga35_2007.md#ga35-p224)*

**Exception: October 18, 2005** : No record of educational requirements
being met for candidates. _BCO_ 21-4, _RAO_ 14-3.e.5.
**Response** : In regard to the first case, we do not believe that the
exception applies in that the candidate was approved for ordination
under the extraordinary clause ( _BCO_ 21-4). The required ¾ vote for
such a case is recorded (section 28171, page 3). Also in Appendix D,
“Membership Committee Report” reasons are given as to the pursuit
of ordination of this candidate via the extraordinary clause. Therefore,
it did not seem necessary to report educational accomplishments in
that candidate’s case.
