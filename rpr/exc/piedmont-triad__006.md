# Piedmont Triad Presbytery — BCO 18-2

*No record of candidate for coming under care met requirements of BCO 18-2 & 3…*

**Presbytery:** Piedmont Triad  ·  **First raised:** 33rd (2005)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 18-2

**Lifecycle:** raised (33rd) → satisfactory (34th)

---

## Raised — 33rd General Assembly (2005)
*[ga33_2005 p.304](../../markdown/ga33_2005.md#ga33-p304)*

**Exception: January 17, 2004:** No record of candidate for coming
under care met requirements of _BCO_ 18-2 & 3 including: PCA
membership, examination by presbytery, and affirmation of
constitutional questions.

## Response found satisfactory — 34th General Assembly (2006)
*[ga34_2006 p.291](../../markdown/ga34_2006.md#ga34-p291)*

**Exception: January 17, 2004:** No record of candidate for coming
under care met requirements of _BCO_ 18-2 & 3 including: PCA
membership, examination by presbytery, and affirmation of
constitutional questions.

**Response:** Presbytery concurs with General Assembly that it erred in
failing to note that the specifics of _BCO 12-2, 3_ were met. Presbytery

assures General Assembly that the requirements of _BCO 13-2, 3_ were
met in bringing the candidate under care of Presbytery and has
corrected its minutes to reflect such.
