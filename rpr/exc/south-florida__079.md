regrets this mistake in recordkeeping. Each of these
candidates have been members of a PCA church for six

to Amend Something Previously Adopted and has made a
marginal notation in those minutes pointing to this response
(attached). We will be more diligent to record the six-month
memberships of candidates in future minutes.
