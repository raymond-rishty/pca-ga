**5. Exception: Nov 18, 2021** ( _BCO_ 40-4) – Presbytery failed to

“deliberate and judge in the whole matter” regarding a report
received according to _BCO_ 40-4. (“Whether [church name

because the person in question was no longer employed, and
presbytery approved this recommendation).
