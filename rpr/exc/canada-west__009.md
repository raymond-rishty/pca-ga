## Response found satisfactory — 50th General Assembly (2023)
*[ga50_2023 p.520](../../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p520)*

**Exception: Mar 5-6, 2021** ( _BCO_ 21-4; _RAO_ 16-3.e.5) – All specific
requirements of ordination exam not recorded (Christian experience).

**Response:** The Candidate was examined during the previous Presbytery in
October of 2020 when he was approved for Licensure which did include his
reporting on, and being examined in, his Christian Experience. In the March
2021 Presbytery meeting the Presbytery was reminded of this and the details
of his Christian Experience and he did give a summary of the same once
more, however the Clerk did not record that this issue was revisited at the
March 2021 meeting. The Clerk regrets the oversight and apologizes for not
being thorough in recording all the necessary information and will endeavor
to ensure it does not happen again.
