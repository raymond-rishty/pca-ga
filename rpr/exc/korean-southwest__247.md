## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.607](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p607)*

**2023-10: Mar 15, 2022; Sep 13, 2022** ( _BCO_ 21-4) — No
record of call to a definite work.

**Response:** All the documents were submitted to
presbytery, included in dockets. But we have erred to include
records in meeting minutes.
**Rationale:** No record that Presbytery corrected its
record ( _RAO_ 16-10.b.1). Presbytery has not submitted the
record for review.

**Response:** Until now, KSWP approved the call of all
candidates without asking for the terms of the call or for the
assurance that he will have freedom to preach the Gospel. Our
committees viewed the details. However, the details were not
presented before the floor of the Presbytery for explicit
approval. We understand now that approval of call includes
terms of call plus assurance of freedom to preach the Gospel.
We began explicitly asking for and approving such things
beginning this presbytery. We will do this in the future.
**Rationale:** The response does not address the matter
o fa call to a definite work. [HOWEVER, the presbytery was
likely confused by the incorrect _BCO_ reference citation
provided. Instead of _BCO_ 21-4, it should have been _BCO_ 201.

## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.616](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p616)*

**2023-10: Mar 15, 2022** ( _BCO_ 21-4) — No evidence of
educational requirements, required papers, or sermon for one
being ordained; and no evidence the man was asked to state
differences with the Standards.

**Response:** We apologize for our oversight in not
recording his own words in the minutes. We will be more
diligent and attentive in the future to ensure such details are
properly documented.
**Rationale:** Presbytery must also correct its record
and note whether the candidate preached a sermon. Presbytery
must also document the candidate’s exception(s) in his own
words, along with the Presbytery’s judgment of the stated
difference(s).
