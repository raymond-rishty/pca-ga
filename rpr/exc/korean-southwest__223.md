## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.603](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p603)*

**2023-09: Mar 15, 2022; Sep 13, 2022** ( _BCO_ 21-4) — No
record of requiring statement of differences with our
Standards.

**Response:** All the documents were submitted to
presbytery, included in dockets. But we have erred to include
records in meeting minutes
**Rationale:** No record that Presbytery corrected its
record ( _RAO_ 16-10.b.1). Presbytery has not submitted the
candidate’s stated differences in his own words for review.

**Response:** KSWP made an error not to record
correctly. Presbytery did ask candidates to state their
differences. All candidates stated that they had no differences.
Presbytery judged all responses to be acceptable. However,
we simply failed to record it properly. So, we made the
corrections to the 77th and 79th minutes. We will reflect this
in our commission report, which will reported to the 84th
presbytery in Fall 2025.

## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.615](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p615)*

**2023-09: Mar 15, 2022** ( _BCO_ 19-2.d, e, f) — No sermon and
no statement of differences with the Standards for one being
licensed.

**Response:** We apologize for our negligence in
failing to record his own words in the minutes. We will be
more attentive and diligent in ensuring that such details are
accurately documented in the future.
**Rationale:** Presbytery must also correct its record
and document whether the candidate preached a sermon as
required and also note the candidate’s exception(s) in his own
words, along with the Presbytery’s judgment of the stated
difference(s).
