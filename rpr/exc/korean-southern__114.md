# Korean Southern Presbytery — BCO 13-9

*No record of review of records of church…*

**Presbytery:** Korean Southern  ·  **First raised:** 43rd (2015)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 13-9

**Lifecycle:** raised (43rd) → satisfactory (44th) → raised (46th) → satisfactory (47th)

---

## Raised — 43rd General Assembly (2015)
*[ga43_2015 p.450](../../markdown/ga43_2015.md#ga43-p450)*

**Exception: General** ( _BCO_ 13-9; 40-1) – Review of sessional
records not recorded.

## Response found satisfactory — 44th General Assembly (2016)
*[ga44_2016 p.420](../../markdown/ga44_2016.md#ga44-p420)*

**Exception: General** ( _BCO_ 13-9; 40-1) – Review of sessional
records not recorded.

**Response:** Although Presbytery had requested that sessional records
should be checked (18-3 on page 12 of the minute), member

churches failed to do that. After this mistake, Presbytery records the
review of sessional records and will continue to do that.

## Raised — 46th General Assembly (2018)
*[ga46_2018 p.421](../../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p421)*

**Exception: General** ( _BCO_ 13-9) – No record of review of records
of church sessions.

## Response found satisfactory — 47th General Assembly (2019)
*[ga47_2019 p.481](../../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p481)*

**Exception: General** ( _BCO_ 13-9) – No record of review of records
of church sessions.
**Response [2019]:** We are aware of this and continue to encourage
member churches to submit their session records. We think that
there are some improvements in this area. But it may take a while to
have all the member churches to submit their minutes.
