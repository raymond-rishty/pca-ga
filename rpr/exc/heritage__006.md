## Raised — 21st General Assembly (1993)
*[ga21_1993 p.235](../../markdown/ga21_1993.md#ga21-p235)*

September 19,1992:
6-A: There is no record that, when the pastor was allowed to labor in a
church outside the bounds of presbytery, he was guaranteed full freedom
to teach and practice the Reformed faith. (BCO § 8-7)
6-A: The minutes are not clear as to the nature of the questions
concerning the Sabbath, nor how his questions related to his subscription
vows. (BCO § 21-4, 5)
6E-4: A ruling elder was being allowed to continue in office who does
not hold to infant baptism. (WCF § 28-4)
