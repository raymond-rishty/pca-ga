# Central Carolina Presbytery — BCO 20-1

*TE was installed at the organizational service, but the call was not approved…*

**Presbytery:** Central Carolina  ·  **First raised:** 29th (2001)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 20-1

**Lifecycle:** satisfactory (29th)

---

## Response found satisfactory — 29th General Assembly (2001)
*[ga29_2001 p.325](../../markdown/ga29_2001.md#ga29-p325)*

Exception: January 22-23, 1999: TE was installed at the organizational service, but the call was not approved by the congregation until November 8, 1998 and organizational service was held on October 25, 1998. BCO 20-1 &amp; 5-11.

Response: The congregation voted to call TE Jim Powell at a congregational meeting on October 18, 1998 prior to the October 25 organizational meeting, but the moderator did not include the terms of call and did not intend to change them, so the moderator simply neglected to present them. So, while Mr. Powell was installed at the organizational meeting on October 25, there had to be an additional meeting the following Sunday, November 8 to present the congregation with the terms of call, which were then unanimously approved. We respectfully submit this response.
Adopted
