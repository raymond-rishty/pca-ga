## Response found satisfactory — 47th General Assembly (2019)
*[ga47_2019 p.509](../../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p509)*

**Exception: Jan 23, 2016** ( _BCO_ 21-4) – All specific ordination
requirements not recorded (theology exam missing).
**Response:** Wisconsin Presbytery acknowledges that the minutes of
January 23, 2016 do not show a theology examination for Mr. [name
omitted], who presbytery examined for ordination at that meeting **.**
Wisconsin Presbytery further acknowledges it was negligent in not
including a note explaining that presbytery gave Mr. [name omitted],
a very thorough and rigorous examination in theology at the time of
his Licensure examination on September 26, 2015 and did not think
another theology exam was necessary so soon after the September 26,

2015 exam. Presbytery also recognizes it should have included
another theology exam at the time of Mr. [name omitted]’s
ordination examination so the minutes would reflect that presbytery
met the requirements of _B CO_ 21-4.

Editorial Note: As of the date of this report, there are 88 presbyteries.
