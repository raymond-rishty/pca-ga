## Raised — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.530](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p530)*

**2025-06: Mar 12, 2024** ( _BCO_ 46-6; _BCO_ 46-7) — A TE is
received from another PCA Presbytery with the understanding

that the other Presbytery will issue a certificate of transfer.
There is no record in subsequent minutes that the transfer
certificate was ever received, and the TE was installed without
reference to the certificate having been received.
