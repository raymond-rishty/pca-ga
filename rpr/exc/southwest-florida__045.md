# Southwest Florida Presbytery — BCO 8-7

*No record that out of bounds TE is engaged in preaching and teaching the…*

**Presbytery:** Southwest Florida  ·  **First raised:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 8-7

**Lifecycle:** raised (51st) → satisfactory (52nd)

---

## Raised — 51st General Assembly (2024)
*[ga51_2024 p.680](../../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p680)*

**2024-2: May 09, 2023** ( _BCO_ 8-7) — No record that out of bounds TE
is engaged in preaching and teaching the Word.

## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.769](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p769)*

**2024-02: May 9, 2023** ( _BCO_ 8-7) — No record that out of
bounds TE is engaged in preaching and teaching the Word.

**Response:** The Presbytery agrees with the Exception
of Substance. The Minutes should have noted that the TEs are

engaged in preaching and teaching the Word. The Presbytery
has noted in its record that the TEs are engaged in preaching
and teaching the Word. The Presbytery promises to be more
careful in the future to state in the minutes that out of bounds

TEs will be engaged in preaching and teaching the Word.
