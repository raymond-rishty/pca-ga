## Response found satisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.685](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p685)*

**2024-04: Feb 11, 2023** ( _RAO_ 15-2) — Presbytery allowed an
the same RE to serve on Overtures Committee for more than

two consecutive assemblies without two-thirds vote of

Presbytery.

**Response:** Presbytery agrees with the General
Assembly. We did not record that the vote to empower the
Moderator to fill any openings for General Assembly was
unanimous, which meets the 2/3 threshold required by the
_BCO_ when sending a commissioner to the Overtures
Committee after more than two consecutive years. Given that
the _BCO_ has this special vote threshold in order to protect the
minority in cases where more than one candidate is nominated
for Overtures, we do not believe the action of our Moderator
to appoint a commissioner fora fourth consecutive year was
unjustified given that there was an opening and no minority
was prevented from sending another delegate. We regret the
oversight and will be more careful to record our specific
actions in the future.
