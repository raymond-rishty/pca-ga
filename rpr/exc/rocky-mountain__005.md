# Rocky Mountain Presbytery — BCO 21-

*p. 23 19a: A "devotional message" does not appear to be a "sermon" as required…*

**Presbytery:** Rocky Mountain  ·  **First raised:** 23rd (1995)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 21-

**Lifecycle:** satisfactory (23rd)

## Response found satisfactory — 23rd General Assembly (1995)
*[ga23_1995 p.203](../../markdown/ga23_1995.md#ga23-p203)*

Exception: September 24-25, 1993: p. 23 19a: A "devotional
message" does not appear to be a "sermon" as required by (BCO 21-
4(d).
Respoase: The Rocky Mountain Presbytery respectfully disagrees with
the exception. The terminology used by the clerk to describe a sermon
or message, was not intended, nor should it be interpreted to introduce
suspicion that the motion passed by the Presbytery (with 17 Teaching
Elders and 11 ruling Elders present) i.e., "....to accept the devotional
message brought before the Presbytery by ....[the Candidate]... as
fulfilling the requirement of BOCO .... 21 4 d " (which requirement is
"to preach a sermon before the Presbytery") is somehow disingenuous in
calling something that is less than a sermon, a sermon. The motion is
self-substantiating in declaring the requirement fulfilled.
Adopted
