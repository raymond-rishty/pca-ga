## Response found unsatisfactory — 51st General Assembly (2024)
*[ga51_2024 p.645](../../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p645)*

**2024-13: General 2023** ( _BCO_ 8-7; _BCO_ 13-2) — No record of annual
reports from men without call or from some men serving out of
bounds. Further, the reports that are received come only to a
committee with no record that they are shared with Presbytery, even
in summary form.

## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.708](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p708)*

**2024-13: General 2023** ( _BCO_ 8-7; _BCO_ 13-2) — No record
of annual reports from men without call or from some men
serving out of bounds. Further, the reports that are received
come only to a committee with no record that they are shared
with Presbytery, even in summary form.

**Response:** The Presbytery has customarily received
oral reports from such TEs during the time set 8apart at the
beginning of each Stated Meeting for Church Reports and
Prayers of Intercessions. We will review and shore up our
recording practices to receive and clearly document written
annual reports in future minutes.
