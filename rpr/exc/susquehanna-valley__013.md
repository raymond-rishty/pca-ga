## Response found satisfactory — 36th General Assembly (2008)
*[ga36_2008 p.270](../../markdown/ga36_2008.md#ga36-p270)*

**Exception: February 18, 2006:** No record of ordinand being asked
to state exceptions to the Westminster Standards or his response.
_BCO_ 21-4.

**Response:** See September 15, 2007 minutes, page 890 (copy
attached) section 14.c. Although TE (name omitted) may have stated
his exceptions to the WCF during his exam that day, stated clerk
failed to record them. TE (name omitted) disagreed with WLC 109
that representation of the incarnate Christ or of false gods is
prohibited by the second commandment. He also disagreed with the
prohibition of the WCF against all recreation on the Lord’s Day. The
presbytery was satisfied with those responses and minutes were
amended as such.
