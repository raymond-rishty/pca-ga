## Raised — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.651](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p651)*

**2025-01: Jan 20, 2024** ( _BCO_ 38-3) — Presbytery dismissed
a minister per _BCO_ 46-6 as if he was being received by a
NPARC church rather than according to _BCO_ 38-3, which
would require a judgment about whether the receiving
ecclesiastical body maintains the Word and sacraments in
their fundamental integrity.
