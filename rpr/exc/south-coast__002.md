# South Coast Presbytery — BCO § 18-

*No minutes record the annual reports from candidates or ministers laboring out…*

**Presbytery:** South Coast  ·  **First raised:** 21st (1993)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO § 18-

**Lifecycle:** satisfactory (21st)

---

## Response found satisfactory — 21st General Assembly (1993)
*[ga21_1993 p.249](../../markdown/ga21_1993.md#ga21-p249)*

Exception: General: No minutes record the annual reports from
candidates or ministers laboring out of bounds as required by BCO § 18-
6; § 8-7.
Response: It was moved, seconded and passed that the Presbytery
concur with the judgment of the 20th General Assembly that the
Presbytery failed to record the reception of the annual reports of
candidates and ministers laboring outside the bounds of Presbytery; and
that the Stated Clerk inform the General Assembly that it is our intention
not only to receive each of these reports this year, but also to record their
reception and disposition.
Adopted
