## Raised — 25th General Assembly (1997)
*[ga25_1997 p.212](../../markdown/ga25_1997.md#ga25-p212)*

Exception: March 30, 1996: Presbytery approved a man for membership who took exception to the standards on double predestination without recording the specifics of his differences.

## Response found satisfactory — 26th General Assembly (1998)
*[ga26_1998 p.246](../../markdown/ga26_1998.md#ga26-p246)*

Exception: March 30, 1996: Presbytery approved a man for membership
who took exception to the standards on double predestination without
recording the specifics of his differences.
Response: Northeast Presbytery acknowledges that we erred in describing
TE’s views as exceptions TE submitted a letter stating his views which
Presbytery judged not to be an exception to the confession. Presbytery
therefore agrees with General Assembly’s exception and promises to be
more circumspect in the future.
Adopted
