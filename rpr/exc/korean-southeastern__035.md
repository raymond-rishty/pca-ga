## Raised — 33rd General Assembly (2005)
*[ga33_2005 p.282](../../markdown/ga33_2005.md#ga33-p282)*

**Exception** : **April 12, 2004** : Presbytery may not remove men from
their roll simply by virtue of their have failed to respond to a contact
from Presbytery. Either formal process must be followed, or the
provisions of _BCO_ 34-10 must be used (if applicable), or Presbytery
must demonstrate that _BCO_ 38 applies.
