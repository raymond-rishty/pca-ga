## Response found satisfactory — 50th General Assembly (2023)
*[ga50_2023 p.588](../../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p588)*

**Exception: Jan 17, 2020; Aug 21, 2020** ( _BCO_ 18-3) – Candidates not
examined on their “motives for seeking the ministry.”

**Response:** The Candidates were examined on their “motives for seeking the
ministry.” That was subsumed under our notion of “the examination in
Christian Experience.” We will amend that in the future to note: “Approve
the examination in Christian Experience, including motives for seeking the
ministry.”
