# Pee Dee Presbytery — BCO 23-1

*Resignation request received and pastoral relationship dissolved without…*

**Presbytery:** Pee Dee  ·  **First raised:** 48th (2021)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 23-1

**Lifecycle:** raised (48th) → satisfactory (49th)

---

## Raised — 48th General Assembly (2021)
*[ga48_2021 p.604](../../markdown/ga48_2021.md#ga48-p604)*

**Exception: Oct 24, 2020** ( _BCO_ 23-1) – Resignation request
received and pastoral relationship dissolved without citing the local
church to appear.

## Response found satisfactory — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.605](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p605)*

**Exception: Oct 24, 2020** ( _BCO_ 23-1) – Resignation request received
and pastoral relationship dissolved without citing the local church to

appear.
**Response:** Although we received approval of release from both the
Pastor and the congregation and a RE from the church was present at
the Presbytery Meeting to present personal thanks for the service of
the pastoral relationship, we are aware that we failed to “cite the
church to appear by its commissioners, to show cause why the
Presbytery should or should not accept the resignation”. We apologize
for the oversight and will do so in the future.
