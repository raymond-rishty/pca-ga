**Response:** Presbytery has not reviewed Session records for 4 years.

2018 General Assembly, the Philadelphia Presbytery has begun to
work to read and approve Session Minutes, beginning with 2018 and

then reviewing previous years. The collection of minutes began in
2018 and the review of minutes has continued through 2019.
Churches will be cited if they fail to submit their minutes to
Presbytery as directed by the provisions of the _BCO_ .”
