## Raised — 35th General Assembly (2007)
*[ga35_2007 p.224](../../markdown/ga35_2007.md#ga35-p224)*

**Exception: March 3-4, 2006** : Commission appointed by moderator
without Presbytery authorization. _BCO_ 15-2.

## Response found satisfactory — 36th General Assembly (2008)
*[ga36_2008 p.272](../../markdown/ga36_2008.md#ga36-p272)*

**Exception: March 3-4, 2006** : Commission appointed by moderator
without Presbytery authorization. _BCO_ 15-2.
**Response** : Presbytery respectfully disagrees with Exception of
Substance No. 1 in that WCP’s Standing Rule II-A states “Unless
WCP shall appoint Presbyters to commissions by its own action, the
Moderator shall appoint commissions to execute the decisions of
WCP except in judicial cases.” The Moderator was authorized by
Presbytery, through its standing rules, to appoint the installation
commission since this did not involve a judicial case.
