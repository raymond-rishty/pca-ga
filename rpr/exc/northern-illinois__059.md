# Northern Illinois Presbytery — BCO 23-1

*No record of four-fifths (4/5) majority vote of congregation on petition to…*

**Presbytery:** Northern Illinois  ·  **First raised:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Final disposition:** unsatisfactory (outstanding)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 23-1

**Lifecycle:** raised (51st) → unsatisfactory (52nd)

---

## Raised — 51st General Assembly (2024)
*[ga51_2024 p.605](../../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p605)*

**2024-13: Sep 09, 2023** ( _BCO_ 23-1) — No record of four-fifths (4/5)
majority vote of congregation on petition to request that an associate
pastor succeed the senior pastor.

## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.650](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p650)*

**2024-13: Sep 9, 2023** ( _BCO_ 23-1) — No record of four fifths
(4/5) majority vote of congregation on petition to request that
an associate pastor succeed the senior pastor.
**Response:** Presbytery agrees with the exception and
will correct the record. Presbytery’s intention in recording that
“The church brings the petition in accord with _BCO_ 23-1,”
was to speak to the 4/5 requirement. This should have been
explicitly stated. The Presbytery will be more careful in the
future.
**Rationale:** No record that Presbytery corrected its
action or record ( _RAO_ 16-10.b.1). Presbytery should correct
the record to reflect the church's satisfaction of the

supermajority requirement.
