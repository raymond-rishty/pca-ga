## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.537](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p537)*

**2024-11: Aug 16, 2023** ( _BCO_ 15-1; _RAO_ 16-3.e.4) —
Minutes of commission not entered in Presbytery minutes,
and these must be presented for review next year.
**Response:** Presbytery acknowledges the error and
has included the minutes with this response. Upon review of
the minutes, we noted the commission to ordain and install
Michael Fenimore did have two teaching elders, but one was
from another presbytery. It was not clear to the commission
from The Book of Church Order that a teaching elder from
another Presbytery could not property be counted in the
quorum of a commission, and Presbytery did not catch the
error until after the installation service. Presbytery will be sure
to include minutes in our record in the future and to make sure

that all commission members counted in the quorum are in
this Presbytery.
**Rationale:** Presbytery needs to ratify the actions of
the incomplete commission.
