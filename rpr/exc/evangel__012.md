Exception: September 24, 1996: Session recommendation of candidate is
waived (BCO 18-2).

Response: Mr. Andrew Southwell had been a member of the Kirk of the
Hills Presbyterian Church from 1990 to 1994. He then served at the
chesterfield presbyterian church of St. Louis from 1993-1994 He then
went to Oxford, England for two years for advanced studies. It was from
there that he came to the Southwood Presbyterian Church in Huntsville,
Alabama.
because of the above events, the Presbytery unanimously adopted a
motion to wave the six month membership requirement and the Sessional
recommendation.
