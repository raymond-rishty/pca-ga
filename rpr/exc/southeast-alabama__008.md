## Response found satisfactory — 25th General Assembly (1997)
*[ga25_1997 p.218](../../markdown/ga25_1997.md#ga25-p218)*

Exception: October 24, 1995. Appendix C. Commission to ordain did not have proper quorum (BCO 15-1, 2).

Response: Concerning the lack of a proper quorum in the October 24, 1995 minutes, we humbly submit that the reviewer of this Commission’s minutes understandably overlooked that there appear the names of four teaching elders present (including three from this Presbytery) and five ruling elders. (Please see the attached copy of the Commission minutes). We would ask that this be acknowledged.

Adopted
