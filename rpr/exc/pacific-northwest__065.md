**Response:** We acknowledge the citation regarding
the October 12, 2023, minutes, which noted the absence of
specific requirements of the licensure exam for Mr. [ ]. Upon
review, we confirm that all specific requirements of _BCO_ 192 were administered during Mr. [ ]’s licensure exam,
including the requirement to preach a sermon. However, these
elements were not recorded in the minutes.

To address this oversight, the minutes have been
amended to include the specific areas of examination, as
outlined in _BCO_ 19-2: Christian experience, theology, English
Bible, church government, and the preaching of a sermon. The
relevant minutes of the Feb. 7, 2025 meeting at which we
approved this amendment are attached.
We regret the omission and appreciate the
Committee’s diligence in ensuring compliance with the _BCO_ .
Moving forward, we will ensure that all examinations are
thoroughly documented in the minutes.
