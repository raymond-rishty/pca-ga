# Central Florida Presbytery — BCO 21-4)

*by appointing a committee to further instruct a man whom it had just approved…*

**Presbytery:** Central Florida  ·  **First raised:** 23rd (1995)  ·  **Final disposition:** satisfactory (closed)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 21-4)

**Lifecycle:** raised (23rd) → satisfactory (24th)

---

## Raised — 23rd General Assembly (1995)
*[ga23_1995 p.184](../../markdown/ga23_1995.md#ga23-p184)*

October 15, 1994: by appointing a committee to further instruct a man
whom it had just approved for ordination, the presbytery acknowledged
that it was not fully satisfied with the individual's qualifications for
ordination (BCO 21-4).

## Response found satisfactory — 24th General Assembly (1996)
*[ga24_1996 p.245](../../markdown/ga24_1996.md#ga24-p245)*

Exception: October 15, 1994: By appointing a committee to further
instruct a man whom it had just approved for ordination, the presbytery
acknowledged that it was not fully satisfied with the individual’s
qualifications for ordination (BCO 21-4).
Response: there is nothing in the minutes of october 15, 1994,
regarding a committee appointed to “further instruct” anyone.
Adopted
