## Raised — 49th General Assembly (2022)
*[ga49_2022 p.510](../../markdown/ga49_2022.md#ga49-p510)*

**Exception: Apr 20, 2021** ; **May 18, 2021** ( _RONR_ 25:10; 40:6-10) –
Quorum was not present – by declaration of the moderator. By failing
to immediately adjourn, and instead to continue to conduct business
without a quorum, the Presbytery put itself at risk that a future meeting
would fail to ratify their actions. actions taken a ta meeting without a
quorum are by themselves null and void.

## Response found satisfactory — 50th General Assembly (2023)
*[ga50_2023 p.534](../../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p534)*

do note that the actions of these meetings were reviewed and ratified at the
next stated meeting at which a quorum was present.
