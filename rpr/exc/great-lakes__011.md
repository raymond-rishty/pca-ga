# Great Lakes Presbytery — BCO 18-7

*p. 9, 13.b: No evidence that a certificate of dismission was received for a…*

## Response found satisfactory — 23rd General Assembly (1995)
*[ga23_1995 p.187](../../markdown/ga23_1995.md#ga23-p187)*

Exception: p. 9, 13.b: No evidence that a certificate of dismission was
received for a candidate coming from another presbytery to be ordained
(or that he became a member and candidate of Great Lakes Presbytery).
(BCO 18-7,18-2).
Respoase: GLP acknowledges the oversight in not recording the
reception of the certificate of dismission and will be more careful in the
future.
