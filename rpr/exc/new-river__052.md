## Response found satisfactory — 50th General Assembly (2023)
*[ga50_2023 p.658](../../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p658)*

**Exception: Nov 6, 2021** ( _BCO_ 13-2) – No record of TE laboring out of
bounds with concurrence of Presbytery within whose bounds he labors.
**Response:** We respectfully disagree with this exception. _BCO_ 13-2 does
specify that a minister laboring out of bounds (“or in a work not under the
jurisdiction of” the presbytery) requires “the full concurrence of and under
circumstances agreeable to his Presbytery.” We do not believe that this rule
has been broken, however, since NRP’s approval of the ministries of TE

[name omitted] and TE [name omitted] were both given at previous
presbytery meetings, and relevant excerpts of those meetings are recorded in
**APPENDIX 1.**

**APPENDIX 1**

was approved for ministry out of bounds.
