## Response found unsatisfactory — 52nd General Assembly (2025)
*[ga52_2025 p.670](../../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p670)*

**2024-13: Oct 12, 2023** ( _BCO_ 13-2) — no record of TE
laboring out of bounds with concurrence of Presbytery within
whose bounds he labors.

**Response:** We acknowledge the citation regarding
the October 12, 2023, minutes, which noted the absence of a
record that the minister was approved to labor out of bounds

with the concurrence of the Presbytery within whose bounds
he labors, as required by _BCO_ 13-2.
Upon review, we confirm that the Ministers &
Churches Committee reported the following to the Presbytery:
- The minister was approved to labor out of bounds in the
North Texas Presbytery in a part-time position as Campus
Associate with RUF at the [University Name Omitted].
- The North Texas Presbytery had approved this arrangement
pending the concurrence of the Pacific Northwest Presbytery,
which was subsequently granted.
This information was inadvertently omitted from the
minutes. To address this oversight, the Presbytery has
amended the minutes of Presbytery to include the committee’s
report and the Presbytery’s concurrence, ensuring compliance
with _BCO_ 13-2. Moving forward, we will ensure all such
approvals and concurrences are explicitly recorded in the
minutes.

Those minutes are attached.
