## Opening of the report

> issue of temperance:
> In answering Overture No. 11 in the negative the General Assembly
> recognizes the problem of the abuse of alcoholic beverages in our society.
> For this reason the General Assembly encourages local churches to:
> (1) Teach and counsel as to the sin involved in the intemperate or escapist
> use of any part of creation:
> (2) Teach and counsel the kind of love and wisdom which may lead to
> individual decisions to curb or to refrain from the use of a particular
> aspect of the creation; and
> (3) Proclaim and embody the reality of the work of the Holy Spirit in the
> bond of vital fellowship within the body of Christ which is the biblical
> antidote to the intemperance and escapism of our day. (cf. Ephesians
> 5:18, in context.) Adopted.
