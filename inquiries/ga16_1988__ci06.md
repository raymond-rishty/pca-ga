# 16-77 III, 26 — Session limiting eligible nominees on officer election ballot

*A session may not withhold qualified nominees from the congregation's ballot to limit board size; BCO 24-1 requires all eligible men to be reported to the congregation.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Constitutional inquiry  ·  **Assembly:** 16th (1988)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 24-1  ·  **Disposition:** advice given

Constitutional Inquiry #2: From Covenant Presbyterian Church, Palm Bay, Florida. QUESTION Re: Election of Officers under BCO 24-1. 'If a Session finds that four nominees for office adequately meet the Biblical requirements, may they, for the sake of limiting board size, only put two of those men on the ballot?' ANSWER: No. 1. BCO 24-1 requires that the session shall report all eligible men to the congregation, after examination. 2. BCO 24-1 says that the congregation determines the number of elders to be elected. 3. BCO 24-1 allows the Session to recommend the number to be elected.
