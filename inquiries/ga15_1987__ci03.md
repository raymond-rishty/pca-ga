# 15-83 III, 9 — Deacons delegating the treasurer role to a non-deacon congregation member

*The CCB advised that delegating the treasurer's responsibilities to a non-deacon member of the congregation does not conflict with BCO 9-4.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Assembly:** 15th (1987)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 9-4  ·  **Disposition:** advice given

*Source: [ga15_1987 lines 10342–10349](../markdown/ga15_1987.md#ga15-p170)*

## Digest headnote
*Editorial summary from the PCA Digest, Part II (Interpretations of the Constitution) — this is the Digest's wording, not the verbatim minutes. The authoritative text is the verbatim record below / linked above.*

That the advice of the Permanent Committee regarding Constitutional Inquiry (hereafter designated 'CI') #3 be ratified. Constitutional Inquiry #3. From All Saints Reformed Presbyterian Church, Richmond, VA, regarding a church treasurer. 'Is it in conflict with BCO 9-4 for the deacons to delegate the responsibilities of treasurer to a capable member of the congregation who is not a deacon?' ANSWER: 'No.'

**Key words:** BCO 9-4

**Inquiry from:** All Saints Reformed Presbyterian Church, Richmond, VA
