# Supplemental II.2 — Proposed amendment to RAO 16-6.c.1 on presbytery records review

*The CCB found the CRPR's proposed amendment to RAO 16-6.c.1 not in conflict with the Constitution, adopting the finding 8-0-0.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Overture/amendment advice  ·  **Assembly:** 51st (2024)  ·  **Provisions:** RAO 16-6.c.1, RAO 16-3.e  ·  **Disposition:** not in conflict; Adopted 8-0-0

*Source: [ga51_2024 p. 231](../markdown/ga51_2024.md#ga51-p231)*

---

## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

7. Amend _RAO_ 16-6.c.1 _57-0-0_

1. Exceptions of substance: Apparent violations of the Scripture or
serious irregularities from the Constitution of the Presbyterian Church
in America, actions out of accord with the deliverances of the General
Assembly, and matters of impropriety and important delinquencies,
and any noncompliance with _RAO_ 16-3.e.5-6 should be reported under
this category.

### CCB advice

In the opinion of the CCB, the proposed change to _RAO_ 16-6.c.1) is **not in**
**conflict** with other portions of the constitution or _RAO_ .

CCB notes that this amendment would not be necessary if the first amendment
is not adopted by the General Assembly as this amendment is correcting a
cross-reference. _Adopted 8-0-0_
