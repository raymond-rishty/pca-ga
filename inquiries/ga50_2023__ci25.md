# Supplemental II.4 — Presbyteries' required responses to CRPR exceptions under RAO 16-10.a

*The CCB found a proposed CRPR amendment governing presbyteries' responses to Committee on Review of Presbytery Records exceptions to be not in constitutional conflict.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Assembly:** 50th (2023)  ·  **Provisions:** RAO 16-10.a, RAO 16-4.d  ·  **Disposition:** not in conflict; Adopted 7-0-0

*Source: [ga50_2023 lines 21665–21666](../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p461)*

---

## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

9. Amend _RAO_ 16-10.a:
Presbyteries shall be advised of exceptions of form; however, they shall take
note in their minutes of exceptions of substance taken by the Assembly,
together with their responses adopted by the presbytery to these exceptions.
These responses should normally be adopted by Presbytery in the same
calendar year as the exceptions were taken by the Assembly. Regardless,
responses must be filed ~~no less than one month prior to General Assembly~~ in
accordance with _RAO_ 16-4.d.

_45-0-0_

### CCB advice

In the opinion of the CCB, the proposed changes to _RAO_ 16-10.a. are **not**
**in conflict** with other portions of the constitution. _Adopted 7-0-0_
