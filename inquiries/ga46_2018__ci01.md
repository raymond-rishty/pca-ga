### CCB advice

**B** . Overture 2 from Grace Presbytery: “Amend _BCO_ Chapter 59 and

Grant Full Constitutional Authority”
In the opinion of the CCB, Overture 2 **is in conflict** with the
Constitution. It is the opinion of the CCB that if Overture 2 was to be
adopted, it would be the same as changing the _BCO_ since giving this
chapter full constitutional status is essentially adding to the
Constitution of the PCA _,_ and thus requires the same process of
approvals as required for any change to the _BCO_ (cf. _BCO_ 26-2) _._ It is
the opinion of the CCB that a single GA does not have authority to
“grant” constitutional status per the provisions of _BCO_ 26-2.
_Adopted by the CCB_
