# Supplemental II.1 — AC Committee of Commissioners authority to nominate a Stated Clerk

*The CCB ruled that the Administrative Committee's Committee of Commissioners may not make a substitute nomination for Stated Clerk when the matter does not arise from AC recommendations.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Constitutional inquiry  ·  **Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Provisions:** RAO 14-6.d, RAO 14-6.h, RAO 14-6.k  ·  **Disposition:** No — AC CoC may not make a substitute nomination for Stated Clerk if the matter does not arise in AC recommendations; Adopted 8-0-0

*Source: [ga52_2025 lines 15122–15157](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p262)*

## Verbatim record

**II. Advice to the Stated Clerk**

the CCB received a request for advice from the stated clerk ina
communication dated June 2, 2025, on behalf of the Administrative
Committee:

1. If the AC didn’t make a nomination for Stated Clerk, could the AC
CoC make a substitute motion along the lines of, “That Mr. X be
nominated for Stated Clerk.” I.e., can they make a substitute fora
motion that (in our mind) never existed?

2. Further if the AC didn’t make a nomination for Stated Clerk, would
“additional nominations” from the floor ( _RAO_ 3-4) be allowed?

The CCB adopted the following responses to the requests from the Stated
Clerk:

1. In the opinion of the CCB, no. _RAO_ 14-6.d., h. give a Committee of
Commissioners power only to Amend a recommendation presented to

<a id="ga52-p263"></a>
<!-- PAGE ga=52 pdf_page=265 printed_page=263 -->

it, with a substitute recommendation being a form of motion to amend
( _see_ RONR [12th ed.] 12:8). A Committee of Commissioners may not
take up new business itself, except a recommendation to commend the
committee or staff ( _RAO_ 14-6.k.). This precludes the Committee of
Commissioners for the Administrative Committee from itself

recommending the nomination of a stated clerk if the matter of a
Stated Clerk does not arise in recommendations of the Administrative

Committee.

_Adopted 8-0-0_
