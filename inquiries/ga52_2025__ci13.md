# II.M — Revision of membership vows and their conformity to Westminster Standards

*Proposed revision of BCO 57-5 membership vows was found not in conflict with the BCO, though the CCB urged careful attention to Westminster Standards.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Overture/amendment advice  ·  **Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 57-5, Westminster Standards  ·  **Disposition:** not in conflict with BCO; CCB advises careful attention to theological questions raised in light of the Westminster Standards; Adopted 8-0-0

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 254](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p254)*

## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

##### OVERTURE 19 from the Eastern Carolina Presbytery (to CCB, OC)

“Amend _BCO_ 57-5 to Revise the Membership Vows”

**Whereas** our current five membership vows have been unchanged for many

years without review;

**Whereas** our current five membership vows have some antiquated language

that must be explained;

**Whereas** our current five membership vows are inherited from other

Reformed denominations, and we are an “ _ecclesia reformata, semper_
_reformanda_ ” denomination;

**Whereas** our current five membership vows mention nothing of the love of

God for sinners;

**Whereas** our current five membership vows give no indication of what human

beings are saved unto, just what we are saved from;

**Therefore, be it resolved** that the General Assembly amend _BCO_ 57-5 to

revise our membership vows to include 2 addenda and one language
update (underlining for additions; strikethrough for deletions):

Add an entire new first vow for membership and renumber the existing

vows.

**1.** **Do you agree that you are made in the image of God,**

**designed by God to glorify God and enjoy Him forever?**

Insert the words “both beloved and” into the former vow #1.

**12. Do you acknowledge yourselves to be both beloved and**

**sinners in the sight of God, justly deserving His**
**displeasure, and without hope save in His sovereign**
**mercy?**

Change the word “study” to “promote” in the final vow.

**56. Do you submit yourselves to the government and**

**discipline of the Church, and promise to study promote**
**its purity and peace?**

So that the amended vows would read as follows:

**1.** **Do you agree that you are made in the image of God,**

**designed by God to glorify God and enjoy Him forever?**
**2.** **Do you acknowledge yourselves to be both beloved and**
**sinners in the sight of God, justly deserving His**
**displeasure, and without hope save in His sovereign**
**mercy?**
**3.** **Do you believe in the Lord Jesus Christ as the Son of**
**God, and Savior of sinners, and do you receive and rest**
**upon Him alone for salvation as He is offered in the**
**Gospel?**
**4.** **Do you now resolve and promise, in humble reliance**
**upon the grace of the Holy Spirit, that you will endeavor**
**to live as becomes the followers of Christ?**
**5.** **Do you promise to support the Church in its worship**
**and work to the best of your ability?**
**6.** **Do you submit yourselves to the government and**
**discipline of the Church, and promise to promote its**
**purity and peace?**

_Adopted by the Eastern Carolina Presbytery at its stated meeting, January_
_25, 2025_
_Attested by TE Wes Martin, stated clerk_

### CCB advice

**M.** Overture 19 from the Eastern Carolina Presbytery: “Amend _BCO_ 57-5 to
Revise the Membership Vows”

In the opinion of the CCB, Overture 19 is **not in conflict** with the _BCO_ ;
however, CCB advises careful attention to the theological questions raised in
light of the _Westminster Standards_ .
_Adopted 8-0-0_
