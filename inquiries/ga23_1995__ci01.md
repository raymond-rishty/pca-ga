# 23-15 II, Reference No. 1 — Congregational vote required on pastor's financial severance

*BCO 23-1 requires a congregational vote on a financial severance package when a pastor resigns his call without another call.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Assembly:** 23rd (1995)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 23-1  ·  **Disposition:** advice given — yes, congregation must vote on financial severance

*Source: [ga23_1995 lines 2892–2897](../markdown/ga23_1995.md#ga23-p56)*

## Digest headnote

Does BCO 23-1 mean that if a pastor resigns his call, without another call, then is the congregation required to vote on his financial severance package? Response: It is the advice of the CCB that the answer to Reference No. 1 from Central Georgia Presbytery is: yes.
