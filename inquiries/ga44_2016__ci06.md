# App. O IV.C — Validity of a transferred minister's ordination credentials

*The CCB advised that the minister in question was properly ordained and was a member in good standing of the presbytery at the time of his transfer.*

## Digest headnote
*Editorial summary from the PCA Digest, Part II (Interpretations of the Constitution) — this is the Digest's wording, not the verbatim minutes. The authoritative text is the verbatim record below / linked above.*

The CCB answered a non-judicial reference from Korean Southwest Presbytery regarding the ordination credentials of a former Presbytery minister by stating that the TE in question was properly ordained and at the time of his transfer was a member in good standing of KSWP.

**Inquiry from:** Korean Southwest Presbytery

**In the minutes:** [ga44_2016 p.350](../markdown/ga44_2016.md#ga44-p350)

---

## Verbatim record

**C.** The Korean Southwest Presbytery submitted a second non-judicial
reference for the CCB’s consideration regarding regarding ordination
credentials of a former presbytery member.

The CCB responded as follows:

It is the opinion of the CCB that records indicate that Reverend
Jung Hyun Oh was properly ordained and at the time of his
transfer was a member in good standing of KSWP.
_Adopted. 8-0-0._
