# III.B — Whether overtures received on or by April 25 should be officially handled for this year's GA

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Provisions:** RAO 11-6  ·  **Disposition:** Overtures received on or by April 25 are timely per RAO 11-6 (60-day deadline); Adopted 8-0-0

*Source: [ga52_2025 lines 14993–15005](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p259)*

---

## Verbatim record

**B.** the c cb received a request for advice from the stated clerk ina
communication dated April 25, 2025, and in person at the meeting:

Should overtures received on or by April 25 be officially handled for
this year’s General Assembly by the Stated Clerk’s office?

It was moved and carried to adopt the following advice to the Stated Clerk:

Overtures received on or by April 25 should be deemed timely for this
year’s General Assembly, having arrived within the 60-day deadline
according to _RAO_ 11-6.
_Adopted 8-0-0_
