# II.L — Conforming BCO 36-5 suspension language to BCO 37-3

*Proposed amendment aligning BCO 36-5's suspension-from-office language with BCO 37-3 was found not inc onflict with the BCO.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Overture/amendment advice  ·  **Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 36-5, BCO 37-3  ·  **Disposition:** not inc onflict; Adopted 8-0-0

## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

##### OVERTURE 16 from Mississippi Valley Presbytery (to CCB, OC)

“Amend _BCO_ 36-5 to Conform the Language to _BCO_ 37-3 re
Suspension from Office”

**Whereas** the censure of indefinite suspension is “administered to an accused

who, upon conviction, remains impenitent” ( _BCO_ 30-1), and is
administered “until he exhibits signs of repentance, or until by his
conduct, the necessity of the greatest censure be made manifest” ( _BCO_
30-3), and

**Whereas** with respect to church officers, “suspension from Sacraments shall

always be accompanied by suspension from office. But suspension
from office is not always necessarily accompanied with suspension
from Sacraments” ( _BCO_ 30-3), and

**Whereas** the language stipulated in _BCO_ 36-5 to be used for the administration

of the censure of indefinite suspension does not address the case when
an officer is indefinitely suspended from his office but not from the
sacraments as clearly a sit could, and

**Whereas** the language stipulated in _BCO_ 37-3 to be used for the restoration of

one who has been indefinitely suspended more clearly addresses the
case when an officer is indefinitely suspended from his office but not
from the Sacraments, and

<a id="ga52-p1200"></a>
<!-- PAGE ga=52 pdf_page=1202 printed_page=1200 -->

**Whereas** there is wisdom in bringing the language of _BCO_ 36-5 into

conformity with the language of _BCO_ 37-3,

**Therefore, be it resolved** that _BCO_ 36-5 be amended by adding “/or” to the

word “and” in “(and from the exercise of your office)” (underlining
for additions):

**Whereas, you, ___________________ (here describe the**
**person as a teaching elder, ruling elder, deacon, or private**
**member of the church) are convicted by sufficient proof (or are**
**guilty** **by** **your** **own** **confession)** **of** **the** **sin** **of**
**___________________ (here insert the offense), we the**
**___________________ Presbytery (or Church Session) in the**
**name and by the authority of the Lord Jesus Christ, do now**
**declare you suspended from the Sacraments of the Church**
**(and/or from the exercise of your office), until you give**
**satisfactory evidence of repentance.**

So that the amended section will read as follows:

**Whereas, you, ___________________ (here describe the**
**person as a teaching elder, ruling elder, deacon, or private**
**member of the church) are convicted by sufficient proof (or are**
**guilty** **by** **your** **own** **confession)** **of** **the** **sin** **of**
**___________________ (here insert the offense), we the**
**___________________ Presbytery (or Church Session) in the**
**name and by the authority of the Lord Jesus Christ, do now**
**declare you suspended from the Sacraments of the Church**
**(and/or from the exercise of your office), until you give**
**satisfactory evidence of repentance.**

_Adopted by the Mississippi Valley Presbytery at its stated meeting, February_
_4, 2025._
_Attested by TE Chris Wright, stated clerk_
