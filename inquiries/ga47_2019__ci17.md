# II.Q — Local session discretion to permit women serving as deacons

*This overture proposed amending multiple BCO provisions to allow individual sessions to decide locally whether women may serve as deacons; the CCB found it not in conflict.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Overture/amendment advice  ·  **Assembly:** 47th (2019)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 5-9.c, BCO 5-9.i(1), BCO 7-2, BCO 9-3, BCO 17-3, BCO 24, BCO 25  ·  **Disposition:** not in conflict; Adopted by the CCB

*Source: [ga47_2019 lines 21063–21073](../markdown/ga47_2019.md#ga47-p401)*
