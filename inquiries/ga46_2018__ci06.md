## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

17. That **Overture 17** from Western Canada Presbytery (“Amend _BCO_

25-11 to Require a Thirty-days’ Notice to Leave PCA,” p. 708) be
answered by **reference** to the action taken in Overture 10. _Adopted_

### CCB advice

**O** . Overture 17 from Western Canada Presbytery: “Amend _BCO_ 25-11

to Require a Thirty-days’ Notice to Leave PCA”
In the opinion of the CCB, Overture 17 is **not in conflict** with
other parts of the Constitution. _Adopted by the CCB_

Dissenting Opinion of the Minority: the creation of a more stringent
requirement that applies only to churches wishing to withdraw **is in**
**conflict** with the congregational competency and civil sufficiency
clauses of _BCO_ 25-11.
