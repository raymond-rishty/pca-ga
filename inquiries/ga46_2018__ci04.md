# App. O II.H — Overture defining roles of unordained deaconesses and deacon assistants

*CCB found Grace Presbytery's overture on deaconesses and women officers in conflict with the Constitution, noting the session, not the congregation, is the appointing body requiring vows.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Overture/amendment advice  ·  **Assembly:** 46th (2018)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 9-7, BCO 11-2, BCO 24-11, BCO Preliminary Principle 7, BCO 26-2  ·  **Disposition:** in conflict

*Source: [ga46_2018 lines 15670–15679](../markdown/ga46_2018.md#ga46-p318)*

---

## Digest headnote
*Editorial summary from the PCA Digest, Part II (Interpretations of the Constitution) — this is the Digest's wording, not the verbatim minutes. The authoritative text is the verbatim record below / linked above.*

In the opinion of the CCB, Overture 9 from Grace Presbytery ("Amend BCO 9-7 Regarding Assistants to Deacons or Deaconesses and Amend BCO 24-11 by Adding New 24-11 regarding Women Officers") was in conflict with other parts of the Constitution and conflicted with BCO Preliminary Principle 7, BCO 11-2 and BCO 26-2. The CCB noted that it was not the congregation that requires vows, but the Session as the appointing body.

## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

9. That **Overture 9** from Grace Presbytery (“Amend _BCO_ 9-7 Regarding

Assistants to Deacons or Deaconesses and Amend _BCO_ 24-11 by
Adding New 24-11 regarding Women Officers,” p. 684) be answered
in the **negative.** _Adopted_

### CCB advice

**H** . Overture 9 from Grace Presbytery: “Amend _BCO_ 9-7 Regarding

Assistants to Deacons or Deaconesses and Amend _BCO_ 24-11 by
Adding New 24-11 regarding Women Officers”
In the opinion of the CCB, Overture 9 **is in conflict** with other
parts of the Constitution. The proposed addition of _BCO_ 24-11
conflicts with _BCO_ Preliminary Principle 7, _BCO_ 11-2, and _BCO_ 26-2.
CCB notes that it is not the congregation that requires vows, but
the Session as the appointing body. _Adopted by the CCB_
