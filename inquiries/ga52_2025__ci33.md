# VII — SJC proposed amendment to RAO 17-3

*The CCB found the SJC's proposed amendment to RAO 17-3 to be not in conflict with the BCO, and the Assembly adopted it unanimously.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Overture/amendment advice  ·  **Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Provisions:** RAO 17-3  ·  **Disposition:** not in conflict; Adopted 8-0-0

*Source: [ga52_2025 p. 261](../markdown/ga52_2025.md#ga52-p261)*

## Verbatim record

**VII. Proposed Amendment to the** _**Rules of Assembly Operations**_

The CCB received a proposed amendment to the _RAO_ being recommended
to the 52 [nd] General Assembly from the Standing Judicial Commission.

In the opinion of the CCB, the SJC’s proposed amendment to _RAO_ 17-3 is **not**
**in conflict** with the Constitution.

_Adopted 8-0-0_
