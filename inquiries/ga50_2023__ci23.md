# Supplemental II.2 — Recording ministerial call arrangements in presbytery records under RAO 16-3.e.6

*The CCB found a proposed CRPR amendment on recording ministerial call arrangements in presbytery records to be not in constitutional conflict.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Overture/amendment advice  ·  **Assembly:** 50th (2023)  ·  **Provisions:** RAO 16-3.e.6  ·  **Disposition:** not in conflict; Adopted 7-0-0

*Source: [ga50_2023 p. 461](../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p461)*

---

## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

6. Amend _RAO_ 16-3.e.6):
Minutes of presbytery relating to ministerial calls shall record that the
specific arrangements ( _BCO_ 20-1, and _BCO_ 8-7 as applicable) ~~and~~ of the call
were found to be in order. The call document need not be attached to the

minutes. _45-0-0_

### CCB advice

In the opinion of the CCB, the proposed changes to 16-3.e.6) are **not in**
**conflict** with other portions of the constitution. _Adopted 7-0-0_
