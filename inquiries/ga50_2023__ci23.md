## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

6. Amend _RAO_ 16-3.e.6):
Minutes of presbytery relating to ministerial calls shall record that the
specific arrangements ( _BCO_ 20-1, and _BCO_ 8-7 as applicable) ~~and~~ of the call
were found to be in order. The call document need not be attached to the

minutes. _45-0-0_

### CCB advice

In the opinion of the CCB, the proposed changes to 16-3.e.6) are **not in**
**conflict** with other portions of the constitution. _Adopted 7-0-0_
