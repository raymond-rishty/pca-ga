# Supplemental II.3 — CRPR review scope for presbyteries with pending judicial cases

*The CCB found a proposed CRPR amendment addressing judicial cases and CRPR review under RAO 16-3.e.8 to be not in constitutional conflict.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Overture/amendment advice  ·  **Assembly:** 50th (2023)  ·  **Provisions:** RAO 16-3.e.8, BCO 40-3  ·  **Disposition:** not in conflict; Adopted 7-0-0

*Source: [ga50_2023 lines 21662–21663](../markdown/ga50_2023.md#ga50-p461)*

---

## Verbatim record

### As referred / posed

8. Amend _RAO_ 16-3.e.8):
Minutes of presbytery dealing with judicial cases shall not be dealt with by
Committee on Review of Presbytery Records ~~(~~ ~~_BCO_~~ ~~40-3)~~ when notice of
appeal or complaint has been given the lower court ( _BCO_ 40-3). ~~, but still~~
Nevertheless, minutes of judicial cases must always be submitted ~~for review~~
as part of the record of presbytery.
_45-0-0_

### CCB advice

In the opinion of the CCB, the proposed changes to 16-3.e.8) are **not in**
**conflict** with other portions of the constitution. _Adopted 7-0-0_
