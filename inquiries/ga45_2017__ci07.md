# App. O IV.B.2 — Session authority over church budget versus congregational call terms

*The CCB answered yes: under BCO 12-5.b, the session's duty to approve the budget supersedes the congregation's authority to set pastoral call terms in any fiscal year.*

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Type:** Constitutional inquiry  ·  **Assembly:** 45th (2017)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 12-5.b, BCO 20-6  ·  **Disposition:** advice given — yes

The CCB answered a non-judicial reference from New Jersey Presbytery, which consisted of four parts. To Part Two, inquiring whether the Session's duty to approve the budget supersedes the authority of the congregation to set the terms of a pastoral call in any fiscal year, the CCB responded "yes," referencing BCO 12-5.b.
