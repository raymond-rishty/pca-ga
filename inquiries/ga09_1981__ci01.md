# 9-65 II, I, 2 — BCO 19 (Internship/Licensure)

**Body:** Committee on Constitutional Business (CCB)  ·  **Assembly:** 9th (1981)  ·  **Provisions:** BCO 19  ·  **Disposition:** adopted — internship requirement applies only to new candidates not yet in process

*Source: [ga09_1981 lines 6466–6467](../markdown/ga09_1981.md#ga09-p142)*

## Digest headnote
*Editorial summary from the PCA Digest, Part II (Interpretations of the Constitution) — this is the Digest's wording, not the verbatim minutes. The authoritative text is the verbatim record below / linked above.*

Implementation of Internship requirement: 'That the General Assembly set as a procedure of implementation of the required year of licensure for ministerial candidates that it be understood that this would apply only to candidates or seminarians not yet in the process. All new candidates would therefore come under this ruling.' Adopted.

**Key words:** BCO 19

**In the minutes:** [ga09_1981 p.142](../markdown/ga09_1981.md#ga09-p142)

## Verbatim record

2. That the General Assembly set as a procedure of implementation of the required year of licensure for ministerial candidates that it be understood that this would apply only to candidates or seminarian not yet in the process. All new candidates would therefore come under this ruling.
Adopted
