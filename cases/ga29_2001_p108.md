#### I. SUMMARY OF THE FACTS

At its summer stated meeting in July 1999, ECP ruled that a transferring licentiate’s view regarding the length of the creation days was not an exception to the Westminster Standards. TE Black’s complaint against this ruling was considered at ECP’s fall stated meeting in October 1999. A motion to deny the complaint failed. A motion to sustain the complaint also failed. In November 1999, TE Black complained to the General Assembly. An SJC Panel heard his complaint on March 2, 2000. The Panel affirmed the ruling of the Presbytery. Below is a brief chronology of the facts.
