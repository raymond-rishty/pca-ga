## Case #5: Appeal of RE D. Keith Abee et al. vs. James River Presbytery

This communication is a formal appeal of the five undersigned defendants, in accordance with the Book of Church Order, Chapter 42, of the findings of a Judicial hearing of a Commission of James River Presbytery appointed to consider a Reference from Evangelical Presbyterian Church, Chesapeake, Va., concerning disturbances within said church. See enclosure 1.

This hearing extended over a period of three nights beginning November 21, 1985 at 7:00 P. M. at Eastminster Presbyterian Church, Virginia Beach, Virginia and the two remaining nights at Evangelical Presbyterian Church, Chesapeake, Virginia, ending at approximately 1:00 A. M., November 27, 1985.
