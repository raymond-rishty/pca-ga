21-57 Personal Privilege on Christian Reformed Church
TE Eric Perrin, chairman of the permanent Committee on Interchurch Relations,
informed the Assembly of a possible misrepresentation of actions taken by the
Assembly concerning the Christian Reformed Church to the Christian Reformed Synod
which was presently meeting. He asked the Moderator to instruct the Stated Clerk to
transmit to the CRC Synod a copy of the actions taken by this Assembly concerning the
CRC, without editorial comment, as soon as possible. The Moderator agreed.
