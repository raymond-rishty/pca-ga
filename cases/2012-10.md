**PCA**

**VS.**

**KOREAN CAPITAL PRESBYTERY**

The 40 [th] General Assembly cited Korean Capital Presbytery ( _RAO_ 16-4 e.,
_BCO_ 40-1, 4, 5) for failure to respond to exceptions of substance in minutes
of meetings of October 6, 2008 (M39GA, pp. 434, 489-490). The SJC notes
for the record that Korean Capital Presbytery has submitted responses that
were approved by the Presbytery ( _BCO_ 10-4). The SJC finds that the
responses are acceptable.
