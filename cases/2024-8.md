**CASE 2024-08**

**MS. JENNIFER BIGELOW**

**v.**

**NORTH FLORIDA PRESBYTERY**

#### DECISION ON COMPLAINT

**October 17, 2024**

The Complaint is Out of Order and cannot be put in order _(OMSJC_ 9.2)
because the Complainant is not a member of the Presbytery against whose
action she has complained. Therefore, the SJC rules the Complainant did not
have standing to file a _BCO_ 43-1 complaint with Presbytery. Presbytery
correctly found the Complaint out of order.

See similar SJC rulings on standing in:

Case 2023-01: _Hann v. Pee Dee Presbytery_ ( _M51GA_, 2024, p. 2024),
Case 2020-13: _Benyola v. Central Florida_ ( _M48GA_, 2021, p. 817),
