Case 2001-11
COMPLAINT OF RE BYUNG HAN YOO
VS.
KOREAN NORTHWEST PRESBYTERY
The case was found out of order on the ground that it was not timely filed
under BCO 43-2 (namely the action complained of was taken on October 10,2000
and the complaint was filed on March 12,2001, well outside the 30 day period.)
