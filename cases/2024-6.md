# Case 2024-6 — Presbyterian Church in America South Coast Presbytery

**Court:** SJC  ·  **Assembly:** 52nd (2025)  ·  **Disposition:** administrative  ·  **Vote:** 19-0-1

*Source: [ga52_2025 pp. 1009–1010](../markdown/ga52_2025.md)*

**CASE 2024-06**

**PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH IN AMERICA**

**v.**

**SOUTH COAST PRESBYTERY**

**RULING ON CASE**

**February 19, 2025**

This case came before the SJC at the direction of the 51 [st] General Assembly
based upon recommendations of the Committee on Review of Presbytery
Records (CRPR). By Citation of August 12, 2024, South Coast Presbytery
(SCP) was directed to respond to multiple exceptions. The Citation stemmed
from a pattern of SCP noncompliance, a continuing failure to respond to noted
exceptions to its minutes.

A Panel was appointed, and organized with RE Dan Carrell serving as
Chairman, RE Jim Eggert as Secretary, and TE Paul Kooistra as the other
principal member. TE Paul Bankson and RE Caleb Stegall served as
Alternates. The Panel was informed that the noncompliance was due to the
failure of the then previous stated clerk of SCP to carry out his duties. He was
replaced by one who diligently prepared and filed the requested responses with
the CRPR representative handling this matter, which had been approved by
Presbytery in two parts, the latter in January 2025.

From the outset the parties (through the then SCP stated clerk and the CRPR
representative) communicated with each other and agreed to submit the matter
to the SJC in writing in lieu of a hearing. Their having since agreed that the
requested responses had been received, the SJC has unanimously concluded
that SCP has fully complied with the Citation of August 12, 2024. The SJC,
however, takes no position on the contents of those responses, for those
contents are beyond the scope of the Citation and have yet to be approved by
CRPR.

SJC reviewed the proposed ruling and approved the final version of the Ruling
by vote of **19-0-1, with 3 absent and 1 recused** .
