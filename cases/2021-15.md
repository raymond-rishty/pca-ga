*Source: [ga50_2023 p. 818](../markdown/ga50_2023.md)*

**CASE No. 2021-14**

_**TE HERRON et al.**_

_**v.**_

_**CENTRAL INDIANA PRESBYTERY**_

#### DECISION ON COMPLAINT

April 5, 2023

In the SJC's June 2, 2022 Decision in Case 2021-06 _Herron et al. v. Central_
_Indiana,_ the SJC "postponed consideration of all pending (Cases 2021-14,
2021-15 & 2022-02) and future Complaints on any matter related to TE Daniel
Herron or related judicial matters." Therefore, consideration of this Complaint
was postponed until after the final decision was rendered in Case 2022-10 _PCA_
_v. Herron_ (trial). Without objection, the SJC ruled that this Complaint be
answered and decided by reference to the April 5, 2023 Final Decision in Case
2022-10.
