**Case 2018-04**
_**RE GLEN MAPES, et al.**_

_**v.**_

_**METROPOLITAN NEW YORK PRESBYTERY**_

#### DECISION ON COMPLAINT

**February 7, 2019**

This Complaint is not judicially in order, having been rendered moot. The
remedy sought by the complaint was a congregational meeting to allow a
vote on leaving the PCA. While the requested meeting was denied by the
temporary session of the church in November 2017, both parties agreed at
the Panel Hearing that Metropolitan New York Presbytery voted in October
2018 to dismiss the Congregation pending their consent ( _BCO_ 13-9(f)), which
consent was given at a called congregational meeting in December 2018.
