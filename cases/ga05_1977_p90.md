MINUTES - FRIDAY MORNING
September 16, 1977
Ninth Session
5-82 Assembly Reconvened
The Assembly was reconvened at 8:00 A.M . Friday morning with prayer by
the Rev. George Mitchell.
5-83 Report of the Judicial Commission to Adjudicate the Complaint of
the Rev. Thomas Barnes, et al, against the Presbytery of the Evangel
Ruling Elder Leon Hendrick presented the Report of the Judicial Commission appointed to adjudicate the Complaint of the Rev. Thomas Barnes et al
against the Presbytery of Evangel.
The Report was approved and admitted to record as amended :
Commission Meeting
The Commission met in the Sanctuary of the First Baptist Church after the Evening Session of
the Assembly on September 13 , 1977. Members present were: Ruling Elders: John C. Snyder. Earl
Bolton, Chip Howell , Leon Hendrick. Teaching Elders: Thomas G. Kay , Joseph A. Pipa , John P.
Oliver, Stuart Perrin, Jack Ross.
Ruling Elder Leon Hendrick , Convener , opened the meeting with prayer. Mr. Hendrick was
elected Chairman of the Commission by common consent.
The place of meeting and the time of meeting for the purpose of dealing with the matters
referred to the Commission were set as the First Baptist Church Chapel at I :00 PM Wednesday.
September 14th.
The meeting was adjourned with prayer.
