**PACIFIC PRESBYTERY**

#### DECISION ON COMPLAINT

**OCTOBER 16, 2014**

The Complaint is Judicially Out of Order, because it has to do with matters in
a judicial case that an accused should reserve for proper disposal in an
appeal, not through a complaint ( _BCO_ 32-14; 42-3), and because it arose in a
judicial case in which an appeal is now pending ( _BCO_ 43-1) .
