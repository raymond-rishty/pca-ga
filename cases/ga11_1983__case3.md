ADJUDICATION OF CASE 7

STATEMENT OF THE FACTS

Complaint was brought against North Georgia Presbytery for its action in

sustaining the examination for transfer of license of John Parrish from another

presbytery. In the complaint, it was stated that ‘Mr. Parrish clearly stated that he did

not believe that the first day of the week is, from the resurrection of Christ to the end

of the world. the Christian Sabbath’.

This was seen by the complainants as a denial of the Westminster Confession of Faith (XX1.7), the Larger Catechism (Question 116), and the Shorter Catechism

(Question 9).

Il

STATEMENT OF THE ISSUES

The Commission felt that the Issue in this case was whether or not a strict view of

the Christian Sabbath as defined by the Westminster Confession of Faith (XX1.7) is

necessary for a man to be licensed.

Ill.

To direct North Georgia Presbytery to instruct John Parrish in this fundamental

element of doctrine: and if and when his view is brought into conformity with

Scripture, the doctrinal standards and the BCO to re-examine him in this area.
