**CASE No. 2022-02**

_**TE HERRON & RE BAYSINGER**_

_**v.**_

_**CENTRAL INDIANA PRESBYTERY**_

#### DECISION ON COMPLAINT

April 5, 2023

In the SJC's June 2, 2022 Decision in Case 2021-06 _Herron et al. v. Central_
_Indiana,_ the SJC "postponed consideration of all pending (i.e., Cases. 202114, 2021-15 & 2022-02) and future Complaints on any matter related to TE
Daniel Herron or related judicial matters." Therefore, consideration of this
Complaint was postponed until after the final decision was rendered in Case
2022-10 _PCA v. Herron_ (trial). Without objection, the SJC ruled that this
Complaint be answered and decided by reference to the April 5, 2023 Final
Decision in Case 2022-10.
