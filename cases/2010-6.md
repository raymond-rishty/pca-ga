**38-31 Report of the Standing Judicial Commission**
RE John White, Chairman, led the Assembly in prayer and presented
the report.
The Chairman reminded the Assembly that the proposed amendments
to the Manual of the SJC – striking current sections 6, “Eligibility for
Voting,” and 7, “Conduct of Members,” adding a new section 2, “Conduct of
Commission Members,” and amending 3.1, 11.8, 17.1, 2, 8, and 19.5 – were
previously adopted (see 38-10, p. 64). The proposed amendment to SJCM
11.7 was not adopted. For the administering of the Oath of Office, see 38-33,
p. 266.
