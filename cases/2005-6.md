The committee received two additional non-judicial references at the April 24,
2006 meeting.

D. Non-judicial Reference 1 from Westminster Presbytery: “…whether
severance packages for ministers must be approved by the
congregation or may they be approved by the session alone?”

It is the opinion of the CCB that sessions can approve severance
packages because these are budgetary matters. Approval of
budgetary matters are the responsibility of the Session ( _BCO_ 12-5b).
There is no constitutional mandate that severance packages be
approved by the congregation. _Adopted by the CCB_

E. Non-judicial Reference 2 from Westminster Presbytery: “Is it in
accord with the Westminster Standards to hold and teach the view
that the reprobate, who is a member of the visible church, is in
covenant (of grace) with God?”

It is the opinion of the CCB that hypothetical issues of doctrine are
beyond our authority to answer and must be settled either by
adjudication or determination by the appropriate court.
_Adopted by the CCB_
