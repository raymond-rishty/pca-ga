# Case p113 — Mays v. New River Presbytery; Snapp and Hope v. Calvary Presbytery; Campbell v. Mid-Atlantic Presbytery

*Source: [ga08_1980 p. 113](../markdown/ga08_1980.md)*

MINUTES — THURSDAY EVENING
June 19, 1980
Eleventh Session
8-83 Assembly Reconvened
The Assembly reconvened at 7:30 p.m. with prayer by RE William DuBose.
8-84 Committee of Commissioners on Judicial Business
TE Rodney T. King, chairman, presented a partial report of the Committee of
Commissioners on Judicial Business, amending a previously adopted action of a partial
report § 8-13, p. 26 by:
1. Editorially amending Recommendation I by changing RE Bill Busbee to
RE Wilson Barbee; Adopted.
2. Amending Recommendation I by deleting, at their request, TE Paul Settle
and RE Jack Williamson, and replacing them with TE Rodney King and
RE William Joseph; Adopted.
3. Amending Recommendation II by replacing “ at the” with “ to report to the
Ninth” . Adopted.
8-85 Committee of Commissioners on Stewardship Ministries (continued)
The Assembly returned to the report of the Committee of Commissioners on
Stewardship Ministries. On separate motions the budgets for Administration, Christian
Education and Publications, Mission to the United States, Mission to the World, PCA
Foundation, Ridge Haven, Stewardship, and Five in Five were adopted as amended.
RE W. Jack Williamson assumed the chair at 9:15 p.m.
On motion the report as a whole was approved. Adopted.
8-86 Time for Recess Extended
On motion the time of recess was set as immediately following the report of the
Committee of Commissioners on Judicial Business.
8-87 Docket Amended
On motion the docket was amended to hear the report of the Committee of
Commissioners on Judicial Business immediately following the report of the Committee
of Commissioners on Stewardship Ministries.
8-88 Committee of Commissioners on Judicial Business Report
The chairman, TE Rodney King, led the Assembly in prayer, and then presented the
report of the Committee.
