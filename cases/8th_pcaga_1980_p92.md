THE PRESBYTERY OF MID-ATLANTIC
The Commission convened pursuant to the call of the Convener, W. J. Stanway,
June 16, 1980 at the Savannah Civic Center, Savannah, Georgia at 8:45 p.m.
A Quorum was present with the following in attendance:
Ruling Elders Teaching Elders
EarlJaggers Red Whited R. C. Cannada Andrew Silman Allan Story

Present too were the respondents of the Presbytery of Mid-Atlantic, Ruling Elder

The meeting was opened with prayer by the Convener. On motion duly made and
seconded, Teaching Elder R. C. Cannada was elected Chairman. On motion duly made
and seconded, Teaching Elder Andrew Silman was elected Secretary.
A brief overview of the case was presented by Respondent Harry Miller. Copies of a
written record of the substance of the complaint were presented to each Commissioner.
The Chairman reminded the Commission of Book of Church Order, Chapter 33-12,
and portions of Chapters 43-45.
Upon motion duly made and seconded, the Commission recessed until 12:45 p .m .,
June 17, 1980 to allow the complaintant time to appear.
The Commission reconvened at 12:45 p.m ., June 17, 1980 in the Savannah Civic
Center, Savannah, Georgia.
A quorum was declared present with the following in attendance:

Present too were Ruling Elder Daniel B. Ice and Teaching Elder Harry Miller,

The complainant, Ruling Elder James H. Campbell, was not present.
The meeting was opened with prayer by the Chairman. W. J. Stanway was elected
temporary Clerk.
The Chairman called the attention of the Commission to BCO 44-4 and 43-10.
The following motion was duly made and seconded: “ In the light of Paragraph 44-4
and Paragraph 43-10, and in the light of the absence of the complaintant or his counsel to
pursue his complaint, the Commission recommends that the Assembly take cognizance
of these facts and allow the judgment of the Presbytery of Mid-Atlantic to stand, this
action to be effective as of the end of the second day of General Assembly. ’ ’
A motion was duly made, seconded and approved to table the previous motion.
A motion to recess and reconvene immediately after the program presentation on
Wednesday morning, June 18, 1980 was seconded and approved.
The meeting was recessed with prayer by Ruling Elder Earl Jaggers.
The Commission reconvened on Wednesday, June 18 at 9:25 a.m. in the Savannah
Civic Center, Savannah, Georgia with prayer by Andrew Silman.

previous meeting.
The motion was duly made and seconded, and approved to amend the main motion
by deleting the last phrase beginning with the words “ this action.”

Commission went into executive session.

or counsel to pursue his complaint, the Commission recommends that the Assembly take
cognizance of these facts and allow the judgment of the Presbytery of Mid-Atlantic to
stand.”
