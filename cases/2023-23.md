**CASE 2023-23**

**COMPLAINT OF MUELLER V. SOUTH FLORIDA PRESBYTERY**

On December 21, 2023 the SJC received a letter signed by the Complainant,
the Presbytery Clerk, and the Chairman of Presbytery's Judicial Commission
indicating the Complaint is withdrawn by agreement of the parties and will be
heard by Presbytery. Without objection, the SJC approved the request, and the
matter has been remanded without instructions and without prejudice.

**CASE 2024-01**

**TE ANDY MOEHN**

**v.**

**WESTMINSTER PRESBYTERY**

#### DECISION ON COMPLAINT

**March 6, 2025**

**I.** **SUMMARY OF THE FACTS**

Fall 2022 The Session of Walnut Hill Presbyterian Church (WHPC) had
discussions with TE Andy Moehn (pastor of WHPC)
regarding his concerns about the state of his marriage. Equine
couples therapy was begun.

02/16/23 The Session of WHPC met with TE and Mrs. Moehn to

discuss the state of their marriage and correspondence each
had sent to Session. The ruling elders of WHPC had several
other meetings in February to consider the state of the
marriage.

03/06/23 TE Moehn wrote to the Session of WHPC to follow up on a
discussion at a Session meeting, apparently held on March 2.
In that letter he shared thoughts on the state of his marriage
and his reflections on the question of whether his wife was
guilty of willfully deserting him.
